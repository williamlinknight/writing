写议论文最怕什么？不是没有观点，而是**观点的表达方式太单薄**。很多同学写"这不是真的"就是 *It's not true*，写"不仅如此"就是 *Moreover* —— 这些表达没错，但永远停留在6分水平。

今天我们读一段FT英文原文，看历史学家如何用精妙的论证语言讨论一个深刻话题——**道歉是否等于真正的历史清算**。

## FT原文

> One might at first glance think of these statements as a step in the right direction as western nations finally appear to be confronting their violent past. ... The fact that the German statement was the end result of five years of negotiation shows that it was **anything but spontaneous**. Instead, it was closely linked to various financial and geopolitical considerations. Historic apologies are intended to settle political deals, not historical facts.

> Former colonial powers, it seems, will say **anything short of admitting** that the imperial project may not have been so progressive after all...

> Yet **it is far from evident** that slavery and colonial atrocities can be so easily consigned to the past.

> The French and German statements are **less a break with the past than a preservation of the status quo**.

> We are still a long way from facing up to the brutal reality of western imperialism, **let alone** addressing its continued legacies in the modern world.

## 中文对照

> 乍看之下，人们可能会认为这些声明是朝着正确方向迈出的一步，因为西方国家似乎终于开始正视自己的暴行历史。...德国发表的致歉声明是五年谈判的最终结果，这一事实表明，此举**绝不是**自发的行为。相反，它与各种金钱和地缘政治考量密切相关。

> 曾经的殖民主义国家似乎什么都愿意承认，**但绝不肯承认**帝国主义事业归根到底可能并没有那么进步。

> 然而，**远不能证明**奴隶制和殖民者的暴行可以如此轻易地归为陈年旧事。

> 法国和德国的道歉声明**与其说是**与过去一刀两断，**不如说**是在维持现状。

> 我们距离正视西方帝国主义的残酷现实还有很长的路要走，**更不用说**解决它在现代世界中的持续影响了。

## 5个高分论证表达

### 1. anything but — "绝不是"

**出处：** *it was anything but spontaneous*

**为什么好：** 学生说"这不真实"只会用 *It is not true*。*anything but* 的意思是"绝不是、一点也不"，语气比单纯否定强烈得多，而且非常地道。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The result was **not** satisfactory. | The result was **anything but** satisfactory. |
| This solution is **not** perfect. | This solution is **anything but** perfect. |

**例句：** The government's response to the crisis was **anything but** timely, leaving thousands of people without help for weeks.

---

### 2. far from it / far from + adj — "远非"

**出处：** *it is far from evident that slavery and colonial atrocities can be so easily consigned to the past*

**为什么好：** *far from* 比 *not* 语气强得多，而且后面可以直接跟形容词或动名词，表达"远不是这样"。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| It is **not** clear whether... | It is **far from** clear whether... |
| The problem is **not** solved. | The problem is **far from** solved. |

**例句：** The debate on climate change is **far from** over; in fact, it has only just begun to gain real attention.

---

### 3. less A than B — "与其说A，不如说B"

**出处：** *less a break with the past than a preservation of the status quo*

**为什么好：** 这个结构是议论文的"神器"。当你需要对比两个事物的本质时，*less A than B* 比 *rather than* 更加精准、优雅。学生几乎不会用，用了就是亮点。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| It is **more** about money **than** about principle. | It is **less** about principle **than** about money. |
| The book is **more** entertaining **than** educational. | The book is **less** educational **than** entertaining. |

**例句：** The new policy is **less** a solution to the problem **than** a temporary measure to calm public concern.

---

### 4. short of + doing — "只差做某事（但没做）"

**出处：** *will say anything short of admitting that the imperial project may not have been so progressive*

**为什么好：** *short of doing* 的意思是"除了做某事之外的一切都做了"，用来描述某人几乎做到极限但就差最后一步。在议论文中可以用来批评"不彻底"的行为。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The company did **everything except** apologize. | The company did everything **short of** apologizing. |
| They **almost** admitted their mistake. | They did everything **short of** admitting their mistake. |

**例句：** The government took every measure **short of** declaring a state of emergency, but the situation continued to deteriorate.

---

### 5. let alone — "更不用说"

**出处：** *We are still a long way from facing up to the brutal reality, let alone addressing its continued legacies*

**为什么好：** *let alone* 是议论文中最常用的递进否定结构之一。当你表达"连A都做不到，更别说B"时，这个表达比 *not to mention* 更加地道，也更受考官欢迎。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| Many students can't afford textbooks, **not to mention** tuition. | Many students can't afford textbooks, **let alone** tuition. |
| He hasn't finished his homework, **not to mention** studying for the exam. | He hasn't finished his homework, **let alone** studying for the exam. |

**例句：** Many people in developing countries still struggle to access clean drinking water, **let alone** reliable internet connections.

## 写作建议

这5个表达有一个共同特点：它们都是**论证性短语** —— 不是教你怎么描写风景，而是教你怎么**推进一个观点**、**评价一个事物**、**对比两个概念**。这正是雅思Task 2和高考议论文最需要的语言能力。

建议你把它们抄在笔记本上，下次写议论文时，刻意用1-2个。不需要全用上，用对1个就比全文用 *very* 和 *important* 强得多。
