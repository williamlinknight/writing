许多学生在写英语作文时，最容易被扣分的地方不是单词拼写，而是**句与句之间的逻辑关系不清晰**。最常见的问题就是：全文靠"and"和"because"从头连到尾，读起来像流水账。

今天我们从《金融时报》一篇关于**波音第一季度营收**的报道中，学习4个让作文逻辑瞬间进阶的逻辑衔接技巧。

## 原文精选

先看原文段落：

> Boeing reported lower first-quarter revenue **than a year ago, when it was in the midst of the 737 Max crisis, hurt by delays in 787 deliveries and a global pandemic that continues to depress airlines' demand for planes.**
>
> Revenue fell 10 per cent to $15.2bn in the first quarter, and the company reported a net loss of $561m. Its loss shrank slightly from the $641m net loss it posted for the same period **a year ago.**
>
> **"While** the global pandemic continues to challenge the overall market environment, we view 2021 as a key inflection point for our industry **as** vaccine distribution accelerates, and we work together across government and industry to help enable a robust recovery," chief executive David Calhoun said.
>
> The flow of cash out of the door has slowed. The company reported free cash outflow — operating cash minus capital expenditures — of $3.7bn for the quarter, **compared with** an outflow of $4.7bn a year ago.
>
> Boeing resumed deliveries of its wide-body 787 planes last month **after** quality problems caused months of delays, which hurt first-quarter results. Boeing is dropping the production rate to five per month from six **as** demand for wide-bodies remains low.

> **While** it was "marginally encouraging" that Boeing reported a net rise in orders in March for the second month in a row, the 29 aircraft deliveries last month were "lacklustre", Cowen analyst Cai von Rumohr said in a note.

> Many, though not all, airline executives have predicted a recovery in aviation fuelled by pent-up demand for air travel.

—— Boeing quarterly earnings report, FT

## 技巧一：while — "虽然……但是"的反击式论证

### 为什么好

中文学生在写让步（concession）时，绝大多数只会用 **"but"**。但"but"的问题是：它只是简单地否定前一句话，没有给读者"你已经被我反驳了"的感觉。

**while** 比"but"更高级的地方在于：它在同一句话里完成了"承认对方合理+坚持自己观点"两个动作。读者读到"While A, B"时，会立刻意识到A是背景，B才是核心观点。

### 学生写法 vs 升级写法对照

| 学生写法 | 升级写法（while） |
|---------|----------------|
| The pandemic is still a problem. But the company is optimistic. | **While** the pandemic continues to challenge the market, the company sees 2021 as a key inflection point. |
| Some people say studying abroad costs too much. But it's worth it. | **While** studying abroad is indeed expensive, the long-term career benefits far outweigh the cost. |

### 模板

```
While [承认反方事实], [坚持正方观点].
```

### 延伸练习

**题目：** 写关于"人工智能是否会导致失业"

> 学生版：Many people think AI will take away jobs. But AI also creates new jobs.

> 升级版：**While** AI may replace certain routine jobs, it also creates new roles in data analysis and machine learning that didn't exist before.

## 技巧二：compared with — "事实对比"比"However, ..."更客观

### 为什么好

学生写对比时，最常见的是 **"However, ..."** 或 **"On the other hand, ..."**。这两个词没错，但它们的问题是：只表达了"我要说另一件事了"，但没有**直接呈现数据的对比关系**。

**compared with** 直接把两个数据放在一起让读者自己感受差距——不需要形容词，数字本身就说明了问题。

### 学生写法 vs 升级写法对照

| 学生写法 | 升级写法（compared with） |
|---------|------------------------|
| The number of students who chose science increased. However, the number of art students dropped. | The number of science students rose to 500, **compared with** only 320 in the art department. |
| This year's profit is $3.7bn. But last year it was $4.7bn. | The company reported a free cash outflow of $3.7bn for the quarter, **compared with** $4.7bn a year ago. |

### 模板

```
[结果/数据], compared with [参照数据].
```

### 延伸练习

**题目：** 比较两所学校的高考成绩

> 学生版：School A had many students go to good universities. But School B had fewer.

> 升级版：School A sent 85% of its graduates to Tier-1 universities, **compared with** only 62% from School B.

## 技巧三：as — 一个词解决"因为所以"的啰嗦

### 为什么好

中文学生写因果关系时，习惯用 **"Because...so..."**（甚至两个词一起用，这是大错）。而英文里，**as** 可以干净利落地表达"原因"，而且它比"because"更轻、更自然，特别适合用在原因不是主信息、只是补充交代的场景。

原文出现了两次 as：
1. "as vaccine distribution accelerates" — 作为公司乐观的原因
2. "as demand for wide-bodies remains low" — 作为减产的原因

### 学生写法 vs 升级写法对照

| 学生写法 | 升级写法（as） |
|---------|--------------|
| The company is optimistic. Because vaccines are making progress. | The company sees 2021 as a key inflection point **as** vaccine distribution accelerates. |
| The school reduced its fees. Because many families couldn't afford it. | The school reduced its fees **as** many local families struggled with rising living costs. |

### 模板

```
[主句], as [原因从句].
```

（注意：as引导的原因从句通常放在主句之后，这和because一致。as放在句首通常表示"当……时"的时间含义，需注意区分。）

### 延伸练习

**题目：** 讨论政府为什么要推广电动车

> 学生版：The government wants more people to buy electric cars because pollution is bad.

> 升级版：The government is accelerating electric vehicle adoption **as** air pollution levels in major cities exceed safe limits.

## 技巧四：after — 用时间关系替代"wasthen"的流水账

### 为什么好

学生写时间顺序时，喜欢**用and连接两个句子**："There was a problem. And then the company did something." 这种写法让文章像小学生日记。

**after** 可以把两个事件压缩到一句话里，让读者立刻明白先后关系和因果关系。

原文：*Boeing resumed deliveries of its wide-body 787 planes last month **after** quality problems caused months of delays.*

这句话用"after"把"质量问题→延迟→恢复交付"三个事件压缩成一个紧凑的句子。

### 学生写法 vs 升级写法对照

| 学生写法 | 升级写法（after） |
|---------|----------------|
| There were quality problems and delays happened. Then the company started delivering again. | Boeing resumed deliveries **after** quality problems caused months of delays. |
| Many students worried about the exam. Then the school changed the rules. | The school adjusted the grading system **after** many students expressed concerns about exam fairness. |

### 模板

```
[后续事件/结果] after [先前事件/原因].
```

### 延伸练习

**题目：** 描述一所学校改变政策的原因

> 学生版：The school found that students were under too much pressure. Then they decided to reduce homework.

> 升级版：The school decided to reduce homework **after** a survey revealed that 65% of students reported high stress levels.

## 写作建议

逻辑衔接的核心不是**背更多连接词**，而是**精确配对逻辑关系和连接词**：

1. **让步反驳用 while**（不是 but）
2. **数据对比用 compared with**（不是 however）
3. **轻量因果用 as**（不是 because…so…）
4. **时间因果用 after**（不是 and then）

每次写完作文，花1分钟检查：每个段落之间的连接词是否精准？有没有一个"and"或"but"从开头用到结尾？

先掌握这4个，写到形成肌肉记忆，再扩充你的衔接词库。
