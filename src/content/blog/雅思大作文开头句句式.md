很多雅思考生告诉我：写大作文最卡的地方，就是开头段。

坐在考场上盯着题目看了5分钟，不知道怎么下笔。好不容易写了一句，又觉得太模板化，删掉重写……时间就这么浪费了。

> **📊 厦门灯塔威廉老师批改数据：** 在我批改的 200+ 篇雅思作文中，超过 60% 的考生开头段超过 5 分钟才能完成。使用结构化开头句式的学生，不仅开头段速度快 50%，Task Response 单项分数平均高 0.5-1.0 分。

## 好开头段的黄金公式

雅思大作文开头段只需要**3句话**：

> **背景引入 + 观点引述 + 个人立场/下文预告**

3句话，50-70词，2分钟内写完。OK，现在来看具体怎么写每一句。

## 第一句：背景引入（8种句式）

### 类型1：It is undeniable that...
> It is undeniable that technology has transformed the way we communicate.

适合：科技、教育、社会变化类话题

### 类型2：In recent years, there has been a growing debate over...
> In recent years, there has been a growing debate over whether university education should be tuition-free.

适合：争议性话题

### 类型3：The issue of ... has become a matter of concern for many.
> The issue of environmental pollution has become a matter of concern for governments worldwide.

适合：环境、社会问题

### 类型4：It is often said that...
> It is often said that the youth of today are the leaders of tomorrow.

适合：教育、年轻人话题

### 类型5：With the rapid development of..., ...
> With the rapid development of artificial intelligence, many traditional jobs are facing the risk of being replaced.

适合：发展与变化类话题

### 类型6：There is no denying that...
> There is no denying that social media plays an increasingly important role in modern life.

适合：几乎所有话题（万能开头）

### 类型7：Some people believe that..., while others argue that...
> Some people believe that the government should invest more in public transportation, while others argue that building more roads is the solution.

适合：有两方观点的议论文（最常见题型）

### 类型8：The question of whether... has sparked heated discussion.
> The question of whether homework should be abolished in primary schools has sparked heated discussion among educators and parents alike.

适合：有争议的当代话题

## 第二句：概括双方观点

这一步很简单。如果题目提到了两种观点，用一句话概括：

> While some argue that..., opponents hold the view that...

或者：

> Both proponents and opponents of this issue have valid points to consider.

## 第三句：表明立场或预告下文

两种选择：

**选择A（有明确立场）：**
> From my perspective, ... is a more effective approach.
> → From my perspective, investing in renewable energy is a more effective approach.

**选择B（中立讨论）：**
> This essay will examine both sides of the argument before reaching a conclusion.

## 完整开头段示范

**题目：** Some people think that governments should spend more money on public services rather than on arts such as music and painting. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

> It is undeniable that public services such as healthcare and education are essential for any society.（背景） Some people argue that governments should prioritize funding for these areas over the arts, while others believe that arts funding is equally important.（观点） This essay will examine both perspectives before presenting my own view.（预告）

**用时：** 不到2分钟
**字数：** 50词
**结构：** 清晰完整

## 开头段避坑指南

| 常见错误 | 问题 | 正确做法 |
|---------|------|---------|
| 太长 | 开头段写100+词，主体段没时间 | 控制在50-70词，3句结束 |
| 照抄题目 | 原封不动抄题干，0分风险 | 用同义词改写（paraphrase） |
| 太模板 | "With the development of society" 被考官看腻了 | 换用上述8种句式轮换使用 |
| 立场模糊 | 说了半天不知道你同意哪边 | 第三句明确立场或用"this essay will examine"预告 |

**最实用建议：** 考前把这8种句式各练习5遍，形成肌肉记忆。考试时看到题目，立刻匹配一个句式开始写，**不要犹豫**。30秒内搞定开头段，把时间留给主体段——这才是拿分的关键。
