| 中文 | English |

| 保持健康 | keep fit / stay healthy |

## 范文一：How to Keep Healthy

**题目：** 假如你是李华，请以「How to Keep Healthy」为题，写一篇80词左右的短文，给校报投稿。

**范文：**

> Everyone wants to be healthy. Here are some suggestions.
>
> First, we should eat a balanced diet. Eat more fruit and vegetables, and eat less junk food. Second, doing exercise is very important. We can run, swim or play ball games for at least 30 minutes every day. Third, we need enough sleep. Don't stay up late. Go to bed early and get up early.
>
> In a word, if we follow these tips, we can live a healthy and happy life.

**亮点句型分析：**
- 「Here are some suggestions.」 — 引出建议的万能开头
- 「First... Second... Third...」 — 层次清晰的列举
- 「Eat more..., and eat less...」 — 对比结构
- 「if we follow these tips」 — 条件句做结尾

## 范文二：My Healthy Lifestyle

**题目：** 请描述你的一天，说明你是如何保持健康的。词数80左右。

**范文：**

> I have a healthy lifestyle. Let me tell you about my daily habits.
>
> In the morning, I get up at 6:30 and drink a glass of water. Then I go out for a 20-minute run. After breakfast, I walk to school. It takes me about 15 minutes.
>
> At school, I drink enough water and eat fruit at break. I never buy junk food from the shop. After school, I play basketball with my classmates for half an hour.
>
> In the evening, I finish my homework before 9:30 and go to bed at 10:00. I never stay up late.
>
> I think having a healthy lifestyle is not difficult. Just start with small things.

**亮点句型分析：**
- 「Let me tell you about...」 — 引出话题的自然表达
- 「It takes me about...」 — 花费时间的表达
- 「I never...」 — 否定句表习惯
- 「Just start with small things.」 — 有深度的结尾句

## 写作小贴士

1. **分段结构**：开头(总起)→中间(2-3个要点)→结尾(总结)，三段式最稳
2. **连接词**：First of all / Besides / What's more / Finally — 让文章更连贯
3. **具体数字**：写「30 minutes」「15 minutes」比写「some time」更真实
4. **对比手法**：eat more... eat less... / do... don't... — 提升语言层次
