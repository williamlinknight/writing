初中英语作文最常见的扣分点之一：整篇文章像在念流水账——"First... Then... Next... Finally..."，读起来就是一个清单，不是一篇文章。

> **📊 厦门灯塔威廉老师批改数据：** 在我批改的150+篇初中英语作文中，超过80%的作文只使用3种以下过渡词。而使用5种以上过渡词的作文，平均分高出1.5-2分（满分20分制）。

## 问题在哪里

看这段学生原文：

> I woke up at 7 o'clock. Then I had breakfast. Then I went to school. Then I had classes. Then I went home. Then I did homework. Then I went to bed.

这不能叫文章，这是日程表。

## 解决方案：按功能分类学过渡词

### 1. 时间顺序（代替 Then）

| 场景 | 用这个 | 不要再用 |
|------|-------|---------|
| 做完A接着做B | After that, / Following that, | And then |
| A的同时B发生 | Meanwhile, / At the same time, | At this time |
| A之后很久才做B | Eventually, / In the end, | Finally |
| 表示突然变化 | Suddenly, / All of a sudden, | Then |

### 2. 追加信息（代替 And）

- Besides, / What's more, / Moreover, / In addition,
- Not only... but also...

**示例：**
> I helped my mom wash the dishes. Besides, I also cleaned my room.

### 3. 转折对比（代替 But）

- However, / On the other hand, / Instead,
- Although + 句子

**示例：**
> I wanted to go out and play. However, I still had homework to finish.

### 4. 因果关系（代替 So）

- Therefore, / As a result, / Because of this,
- This is why...

**示例：**
> I practiced English every day. As a result, my grades improved significantly.

### 5. 举例说明

- For example, / For instance,
- Such as / Like

**示例：**
> I enjoy outdoor activities, such as hiking and cycling.

## 一篇示范

**题目：An Unforgettable Day**

> Last Saturday was an unforgettable day for me. **In the morning**, our family went hiking in the nearby mountain. **While** we were climbing, I suddenly noticed a beautiful view of the whole city from the top.
>
> **After that**, we had a picnic by the lake. **What's more**, my dad taught me how to fish. **Although** I didn't catch any fish, I enjoyed the experience very much.
>
> **In the afternoon**, it started raining unexpectedly. **However**, instead of going home, we found a small pavilion and played chess together.
>
> **This experience taught me that** sometimes the best moments come from unexpected situations.

数一下：这段短文用了7种不同的过渡词，没有重复用"then"或"but"。

## 初中生最容易记住的8个高级过渡词

为了记起来方便，就记这8个：

1. **However** — 然而（代替but）
2. **Therefore** — 因此（代替so）
3. **Moreover** / **Furthermore** — 而且（代替and）
4. **Meanwhile** — 与此同时
5. **Eventually** — 最终（代替finally）
6. **In addition** — 除此之外
7. **As a result** — 结果是
8. **For instance** — 例如

每次写作文，要求自己至少从这8个里用4个。用熟了以后，这些词就变成你自然表达的一部分了。
