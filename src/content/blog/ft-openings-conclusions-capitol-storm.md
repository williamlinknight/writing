关于开头，大部分同学只学过一种写法——**概括背景**。但FT的新闻写作提供了更好的选择：**用场景开头，让读者「看到」事件的发生。**

今天我们用一篇FT突发新闻报道，学习两种实用技巧：场景式开头 + 多声部收尾。

## FT原文

> **The peaceful transfer of power in America ground to a halt on Wednesday after angry mobs of pro-Trump supporters stormed the Capitol building and forced lawmakers to abandon the certification of Joe Biden's presidential victory.**
>
> **The violent clashes capped two months of political turmoil during which President Donald Trump repeatedly refused to accept his defeat to Mr Biden and stoked anger among his supporters by peddling baseless allegations of mass voter fraud.**
>
> 周三，支持唐纳德•特朗普(Donald Trump)的愤怒暴徒袭击了美国国会大厦，迫使议员放弃认证乔•拜登(Joe Biden)胜选，这使得美国权力的和平移交陷入停滞。
>
> 这场暴力冲突让两个月以来政治动荡走到顶峰。在此期间，美国总统特朗普一再拒绝接受自己输给拜登的事实，并通过兜售存在大规模选民欺诈的毫无根据的指控在他的支持者中激起愤怒情绪。

这是2021年1月7日FT关于国会山事件的报道开头，记者在同一件事中写出了情绪、画面、因果。

---

## 技巧一：事件反转式开头（Contrast + Drama）

**结构公式：** [预期/常态] + [破坏性动词] + after + [具体事件]

**原文示范：**
> The peaceful transfer of power in America **ground to a halt** after angry mobs of pro-Trump supporters **stormed** the Capitol building and **forced** lawmakers to abandon the certification...

FT在开头第一句话就制造了强烈的反差："权力的和平移交"（本该发生的事）——"陷入停滞"（被破坏了）。三个动作动词（ground to a halt, stormed, forced）直接交代了冲突的核心。

| | 写法 |
|---|------|
| **❌ 学生初版** | On Wednesday, Trump supporters entered the Capitol building. This stopped the transfer of power. Many people were angry. |
| **✅ 升级版** | The peaceful transfer of power in America ground to a halt after angry mobs of pro-Trump supporters stormed the Capitol building and forced lawmakers to abandon the certification. |
| **💡 效果** | 一句话融合了"预期-破坏-结果"三个层次，没有一句废话。stormed比entered激烈一个等级，ground to a halt比stopped更形象 |

**考场用法：**
- *The carefully planned celebration ground to a halt after heavy rain flooded the venue.*
- *Years of diplomatic efforts came to an abrupt end when tensions between the two nations escalated overnight.*

这个结构特别适合写**对比鲜明的议论文**——先说"理论上应该是这样"，再说"但现实是那样"。

## 技巧二：场景开头的动词选择

一个好的场景开头，关键在于**动词的选择**。比较下面两组：

| | 写法 |
|---|------|
| **❌ 学生版** | Some Trump supporters went into the Capitol building. The police told them to leave. |
| **✅ FT版** | Thousands of Trump supporters had **amassed** in Washington to protest... protesters... **swept through** the hallways of Congress. |
| **💡 效果** | `amass`（聚集）比`gathered`更正式精准；`swept through`（席卷）比`ran into`更有画面感 |

FT在整篇报道中的动词选择非常讲究：

| 学生常用词 | FT升级词 | 使用场景 |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| went in / entered | stormed | 强力闯入 |
| gathered | amassed | 大规模聚集 |
| left | evacuated | 撤离（正式） |
| stopped | ground to a halt | 完全中止（画面感） |

**考场用法：**
- *Protesters amassed in the city square, demanding immediate action on climate change.*
- *News of the accident swept through the campus within minutes.*

## 技巧三：多声部收尾（Multi-Voice Conclusion）

很多同学的结尾只有一个人发声——通常是作者自己的观点。FT的写法更高级：**用不同人物的声音收尾，让结论更有层次感。**

原文末尾用了两个"声音"：

**第一个声音：彭斯的谴责（行动层面）**
> "The violence and destruction taking place at the US Capitol Must Stop and it Must Stop Now. Anyone involved must respect Law Enforcement officers and immediately leave the building."

**第二个声音：彭斯的法律立场（原则层面）**
> "Peaceful protest is the right of every American but this attack on our Capitol will not be tolerated and those involved will be prosecuted to the fullest extent of the law."

同一人物、两个层次的发言——先呼吁立刻停止，再上升到法律原则。收尾不拖泥带水，但也不草草了事。

**结构公式：** [人物A的行动呼吁] + [人物B的原则陈述] + （可选作者评论）

| | 写法 |
|---|------|
| **❌ 学生初版** | In conclusion, we should all be peaceful and follow the law. It is very important. |
| **✅ 升级版** | "The violence must stop and it must stop now," the vice-president said, adding: "This attack on our Capitol will not be tolerated and those involved will be prosecuted to the fullest extent of the law." |
| **💡 效果** | 用真实人物的原话替代空洞的"In my opinion"，既有力又有信息来源支撑 |

**考场用法：**
- *"We cannot afford to ignore this problem any longer," the principal stated. "Every student deserves a safe learning environment — and that starts with action, not words."*
- *The local business owner summed it up: "This isn't just about profit. It's about whether our community survives."*

## 实战建议

| 场景 | 推荐开头公式 | 推荐结尾公式 |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| 议论文（正反对比） | 事件反转式（预期→破坏→结果） | 多声部收尾（A→B→总结） |
| 记叙文（个人经历） | 场景式（用动词制造画面） | 一人多层次（行动→感悟） |
| 图表描述 | 背景→转折（用动态动词） | 趋势句+影响句 |

下次写作文，试试**不用"But I think..."的开头**，用事件反转式开头，用多声部收尾——效果立竿见影。
