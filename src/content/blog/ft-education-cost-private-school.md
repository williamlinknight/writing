## 为什么要读这篇？

你的学生写"教育成本高"时，是否永远只有 `expensive` 和 `cost a lot of money`？

今天这篇FT文章讨论的是英国私立学校费用不断上涨的现象——但其语言远不止说"贵"。作者用了一组**精准的财务类动词和因果结构**来描述"成本上涨如何挤压家庭选择空间"。这种"先说现象 → 再分析原因 → 然后给出数据支撑 → 最后谈影响"的写作结构，正是中高考和雅思议论文最需要的逻辑骨架。

我们从这篇FT报道中提取5个**教育/经济/社会类议论文通用**的高级表达，让你的作文在"成本与选择"类话题中不再贫瘠。

## FT原文精选

> Privately-funded schools have many advantages, mostly **born of** the extra money they can spend per child.
>
> However…funding that choice **is becoming harder**. Rising house prices mean mortgage payments are taking a bigger proportion of household income, **leaving less to cover** school fees.
>
> Eton, for example, will **set you back** £14,698 a term (plus a £400 registration fee and £3,200 "acceptance fee").
>
> Schools with good reputations and facilities are in high demand among rich overseas families, so they can **ratchet up** fees, knowing it will not deter many "customers".

> **中文对照：** 私立学校有很多优势，主要源于它们在每个孩子身上能花更多钱。但为这种选择筹措资金正变得更加困难。不断上涨的房价意味着房贷占了家庭收入的更大比例，留给学费的钱就少了。伊顿公学每学期将花掉你1.47万英镑。声誉好的学校可以提高学费，因为他们知道这不会吓跑客户。

## 5个考场高分表达

### 1. born of 源于 / 产生于

**原文出处：** "Privately-funded schools have many advantages, mostly **born of** the extra money they can spend per child."

**为什么好：** 写"原因"时，大部分学生只会用 `because`。`born of` 是一个优雅的书面语表达，意思是"由……产生的/源于……"，它自带一种因果关系，同时比 `result from` 更具画面感——仿佛某种结果是"出生"于某个源头。用在议论文中解释**某种现象的根本原因**，非常出彩。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The problem is because of the lack of funding. | The crisis is **born of** years of underinvestment in public education. |
| Many students feel stressed because of exam pressure. | The rising anxiety among teenagers is **born of** an exam-driven education system. |

> **例句：** The growing resistance to smartphones in schools is **born of** concerns over declining attention spans and social isolation among students.

---

### 2. set someone back 花掉某人（多少钱）

**原文出处：** "Eton, for example, will **set you back** £14,698 a term…"

**为什么好：** 99%的学生写"某物花了多少钱"时只会写 `cost`。`set someone back` 是一个地道、生动且略带夸张感的表达，非常适合在**议论文中举例说明费用之高**。它比 `cost` 更有语言趣味，比 `charge` 更口语化但又不失正式感。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| A year of private school costs £30,000. | A year of private school will **set you back** £30,000. |
| The new laptop cost me ¥8,000. | The latest model **set me back** nearly ¥8,000. |

> **例句：** A month of summer camp can **set parents back** as much as ¥20,000, not including airfare and pocket money.

---

### 3. ratchet up 逐步提高 / 步步攀升

**原文出处：** "…so they can **ratchet up** fees, knowing it will not deter many 'customers'."

**为什么好：** 写"上涨"时，大多数学生只会用 `increase` 和 `go up`。`ratchet up` 是一个**有画面感的动词短语**——就像棘轮（ratchet）只能单向转动不能后退一样，这个词暗示"只会越来越贵，不会降下来"。用在描述费用、压力、紧张局势的持续上升时非常有力量。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The cost of education keeps increasing. | Schools continue to **ratchet up** tuition fees year after year. |
| Stress among students is going up. | Exam pressure **ratchets up** as the gaokao approaches. |

> **例句:** Competition among top universities continues to **ratchet up**, with acceptance rates dropping to record lows.

---

### 4. leaving less to cover 剩下的钱不足以覆盖…… / 留给……的钱更少了

**原文出处：** "Rising house prices mean mortgage payments are taking a bigger proportion of household income, **leaving less to cover** school fees."

**为什么好：** 这个结构由三部分组成：原因 → 中间变量 → 结果。`leaving less to cover X` 是**因果链条的收尾表达**。它比简单的 `so people have less money for X` 更正式、更书面，适合在议论文中做"连锁反应"的描述。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| People spend more on housing, so they have less for education. | Rising rents **leave less to cover** children's extracurricular activities. |
| Because of tuition fees, families cannot save money. | High tuition fees **leave less to cover** daily living expenses. |

> **例句：** The rising cost of textbooks and accommodation **leaves less to cover** students' social and mental health needs, which schools often overlook.

---

### 5. funding that choice is becoming harder 为这个选择筹措资金越来越难

**原文出处：** "…funding that choice is becoming harder."

**为什么好：** 这句话看似简单，但其中的**抽象名词 + choice + 动词结构**非常值得模仿。大多数学生会写 `people have less money for this`，而FT的写法把"选择"（choice）作为主语中心词——`funding that choice`。这体现了英语写作中**用抽象概念做主语**的高级思维。

这个句型可以灵活替换不同的名词：`funding that project / sustaining that lifestyle / continuing that tradition / justifying that decision`。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| It's hard for families to pay for private education. | For many families, **funding that choice is becoming harder** each year. |
| People cannot afford this lifestyle anymore. | **Sustaining that lifestyle is becoming harder** as inflation rises. |

> **例句：** With university fees approaching ¥30,000 a year in some cities, **justifying that expense is becoming harder** for middle-class families.

## 写作示范：教育成本类议论文的"四步论证法"

这篇FT文章最值得学习的不仅是单个表达，更是其**论证结构**。当你写"某件事（教育/医疗/住房）成本越来越高"时，可以套用这个结构：

| 步骤 | 功能 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| ① 优点 | 先承认合理性 | Private education has clear advantages, **born of** better resources. |
| ② 转折 | 但问题出现了 | However, **funding that choice is becoming harder**. |
| ③ 数据 | 用具体数字说明 | A top-tier school can **set a family back** £15,000 per term. |
| ④ 推论 | 连锁后果 | As fees **ratchet up**, rising costs **leave less to cover** other essentials. |

> **考场应用：** 如果作文题是"Should parents spend heavily on children's education?"，你可以在主体段这样展开：
>
> *"After-school tutoring has clear advantages, mostly **born of** the individualised attention it offers. However, **funding that choice is becoming harder** for many families. A year of intensive English classes can **set a family back** ¥30,000. As competition for university places continues to **ratchet up**, the financial burden **leaves less to cover** children's hobbies and rest time."*
