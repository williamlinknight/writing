讨论"改革"和"政策冲突"是雅思大作文和高考议论文的高频场景——教育体制改革、城市规划、环保法规……每个话题都离不开对"变化力度"和"博弈过程"的描述。

但学生最常见的写法是：**made a big change** / **many people disagreed**。

今天的素材来自FT关于阿根廷新任总统哈维尔·米莱（Javier Milei）向国会提交改革法案的报道。这位自由主义经济学家试图在短时间内重塑阿根廷经济——无论你对他的政策是否认同，这篇报道中的动词和搭配，是学习"如何描述全面改革"的绝佳范本。

---

## 📖 FT原文选段

> Argentine President Javier Milei **has sent an extensive state reform bill to Congress**, with measures such as tax amnesties and restrictions on protests, **opening up a new front** in the libertarian economist's **battle to rapidly reshape** the South American country.
>
> **Coming a week after** Milei **issued a wide-ranging emergency order to deregulate** Argentina's economy, the new bill **tackles** the four areas of policy — tax, penal, electoral and the party system — that presidents cannot effect by decree. It **dramatically expands his to-do list** in Congress, where he **holds only a small minority** of seats, and where opposition lawmakers **have pledged to strike down** the decree and **challenge** Milei's **ambitious agenda**.
>
> The 664-article bill **proposes allowing** Argentines to register undeclared assets at home and abroad without paying **hefty taxes**, **scrapping** the country's proportional representation voting system for Congress, **giving** stronger legal penalties to those who organise road-blocking protests and **granting** new powers for the security ministry to limit demonstrations.

> **中文对照**
>
> 阿根廷总统哈维尔·米莱向国会提交了一份内容广泛的国家改革法案，其中包括税收赦免和限制抗议活动等措施，为这位自由主义经济学家迅速重塑这个南美国家的斗争开辟了一条新战线。
>
> 一周前，米莱发布了一项范围广泛的紧急命令，解除对阿根廷经济的管制。新法案涉及四个政策领域：税收、刑罚、选举和政党制度。这大大增加了他在国会的待办事项清单，他在国会只占少数席位，而反对派议员已经承诺要推翻这项政令，挑战米莱雄心勃勃的议程。
>
> 该法案共664条，建议允许阿根廷人在国内外登记未申报的资产而无需缴纳高额税款，废除国会的比例代表投票制，对组织拦路抗议活动的人给予更严厉的法律惩罚，并赋予安全部门限制示威活动的新权力。

## 🎯 四大类词汇解析

### 第一类：「改革力度」——如何表达"变化的规模"

#### 1️⃣ extensive state reform bill / wide-ranging emergency order

**原文出处：** "…has sent an **extensive state reform bill** to Congress" / "…**issued a wide-ranging emergency order** to deregulate Argentina's economy"

**为什么好：** 学生写"改革"只会 **reform** 或 **change**。这里 FT 用了两个不同的短语描述不同程度的改革动作——**extensive**（内容广泛的）+ **state reform bill**（国家改革法案）用于立法层面；**wide-ranging**（范围广泛的）+ **emergency order**（紧急命令）用于行政层面。用定语（extensive / wide-ranging）来量化"力度"，比单纯说 big 或 important 精确得多。

**❌ 学生初版**
The government **made a big change** to the education system.

**✅ 升级版**
The education ministry **has sent an extensive reform bill to parliament**, overhauling everything from the examination system to teacher training.

**💡 效果：** 从模糊的 "made a big change" 升级到 "sent an extensive reform bill"——具体动作（sent to parliament）+ 力度尺度（extensive）+ 内容域（reform bill）。

#### 2️⃣ battle to rapidly reshape

**原文出处：** "…opening up a new front in the libertarian economist's **battle to rapidly reshape** the South American country."

**为什么好：** **reshape**（重塑）比 **change** 更有画面感——就像把一团黏土重新捏成新形状。加上 **rapidly**（迅速地）强调速度，再用 **battle**（战斗）暗示过程的艰难。这三个词的组合，一句话交代了"目标"（reshape）、"速度"（rapidly）和"困难"（battle）。

**❌ 学生初版**
The leader **wants to change** the country **quickly**.

**✅ 升级版**
The new mayor has embarked on a **battle to rapidly reshape** the city's public transport network, from bus routes to subway lines.

**💡 效果：** "embarked on a battle to rapidly reshape" 比 "wants to change quickly" 强太多了——battle 暗示阻力，reshape 暗示重塑，rapidly 暗示紧迫性。

### 第二类：「政治博弈」——如何表达"对抗与平衡"

#### 3️⃣ holds only a small minority / strike down / challenge... agenda

**原文出处：** "…where he **holds only a small minority of seats**, and where opposition lawmakers **have pledged to strike down** the decree and **challenge** Milei's **ambitious agenda**."

**为什么好：** 这句话是"政治博弈"写作的教科书级范本。**holds only a small minority** 简洁有力地交代了弱势地位（比 **doesn't have enough seats** 正式一百倍）。**strike down**（推翻/废除）是法律领域的高频短语，比 **reject** 或 **oppose** 更精准——特指"通过正式程序废除一项法律或命令"。**challenge... ambitious agenda** 中的 **challenge** 语气比 **oppose** 更积极（不仅是反对，而是"发起挑战"），**ambitious agenda** 既可以是褒义（雄心勃勃）也可以是中性（野心勃勃），这种模糊性让议论文表达更灵活。

**❌ 学生初版**
The opposition **doesn't agree** with the plan and **wants to stop** it.

**✅ 升级版**
The opposition **holds only a small minority** of committee seats, but it **has pledged to strike down** the proposed legislation and **challenge** the government's **ambitious agenda**.

**💡 效果：** "holds only a small minority" 揭示力量对比，"strike down" 精确描述法律行动，"challenge... ambitious agenda" 表达对抗姿态。三组词汇写尽一个政治博弈画面。

#### 4️⃣ tackle

**原文出处：** "…the new bill **tackles** the four areas of policy — tax, penal, electoral and the party system"

**为什么好：** 学生写"处理（问题/议题）"永远只有 **deal with** 或 **solve**。**tackle** 字面是体育中的"擒抱/拦截"，用在政策语境中比喻"迎难而上地处理棘手问题"——比 **deal with** 更有主动迎战的意味。这里还展示了一个好用法：用破折号列举 **tackles** 的具体对象。

**❌ 学生初版**
The new policy **deals with** several important issues.

**✅ 升级版**
The new environmental plan **tackles** three key areas — industrial emissions, vehicle pollution and waste management.

**💡 效果：** tackle 让政策动作从"处理"升级为"迎击"；破折号列举让信息结构一目了然。

### 第三类：「政策清单」——如何用平行结构写"多项措施"

#### 5️⃣ proposes allowing... scrapping... giving... granting...

**原文出处：** "The 664-article bill **proposes allowing** Argentines to register undeclared assets… **scrapping** the country's proportional representation voting system… **giving** stronger legal penalties… and **granting** new powers for the security ministry to limit demonstrations."

**为什么好：** 这是英文写作中经典的 **平行结构（parallel structure）**——用四个分词（allowing / scrapping / giving / granting）罗列改革措施。平行结构的核心原则是：同一级别的信息用同一语法形式呈现。这里的四个现在分词都在修饰 **proposes**，读起来一气呵成，信息量大但不凌乱。

**❌ 学生初版**
The new law has many measures. It allows people to do A. It also stops B. It also makes the rules for C stricter.

**✅ 升级版**
The new legislation **proposes allowing** young graduates to defer student loan payments, **reducing** the interest rate on existing debts, **extending** repayment periods, and **granting** additional financial support to low-income families.

**💡 效果：** 一个 propose + 四个平行分词 = 一种措施→四种具体行动，清晰、高效、有层次。

#### 6️⃣ hefty taxes

**原文出处：** "…without paying **hefty taxes**"

**为什么好：** 学生写"很高的税"只会 **high taxes** 或 **a lot of tax**。**hefty** 字面是"又大又重的"，用于形容金额时特指"高得令人感到负担"（a hefty fine / hefty price tag）。比 high 更有质感，比 heavy 更适合正式写作。

**❌ 学生初版**
People have to pay **a lot of tax** on imported goods.

**✅ 升级版**
Consumers face **hefty taxes** on imported electronics, making them far more expensive than locally produced alternatives.

**💡 效果：** hefty 不仅表示"多"，还暗示"重到让人肉疼"。在议论文中用于描述经济负担时，比 high 更生动。

### 第四类：「时间与递进」——如何表达"接续关系"

#### 7️⃣ Coming a week after...

**原文出处：** "**Coming a week after** Milei issued a wide-ranging emergency order..."

**为什么好：** 学生写"在……之后"只会 **after**。**Coming a week after** 是一个加分的时间状语结构——**Coming + 时间段 + after + 背景事件**。它比单纯的 **After** 多了"紧跟着"的意味，暗示两件事之间有因果关系或策略衔接。

**❌ 学生初版**
**After** the government made the first plan, it made another plan.

**✅ 升级版**
**Coming just months after** the central bank raised interest rates, the finance ministry's new stimulus package has surprised many economists.

**💡 效果：** Coming just months after 比简单的 After 多了一层"事情接连发生"的紧迫感和因果暗示。

## 📝 考场实战模板

**描述"全面改革"的高分句型：**

> **[主体] has sent an extensive [领域] reform [文件] to [机构], tackling [N个方面], dramatically expanding [主体的角色] in [场景].**

**范文示例（教育与科技）：**
> The Ministry of Education **has sent an extensive digital education reform bill to parliament, tackling** three key areas — rural internet access, teacher training and online curriculum development, **dramatically expanding** the government's role in the digital learning landscape.

> **[主体] proposes allowing [对象A], scrapping [对象B], giving [惩罚/奖励C], and granting [权力D], in a battle to [最终目标].**

**范文示例（环境与城市）：**
> The city government **proposes allowing** electric scooter rentals in residential areas, **scrapping** the old licensing system for small businesses, **giving** additional tax breaks to green enterprises, and **granting** new inspection powers to the environmental protection agency, **in a battle to reshape** the city into a model of sustainable urban living.
