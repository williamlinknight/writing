"She likes reading, swimming, and to ride a bike."

这句话读起来哪里不舒服？——reading和swimming是ing形式，to ride a bike是不定式，三种不同的结构并列在一起。

这就是平行结构（parallelism）的问题：**几个并列的成分应当使用相同的形式。**

> **📊 厦门灯塔威廉老师批改数据：** 平行结构错误在我批改的作文中发生率约30%，集中在三个场景：并列动词形式不一致（占40%）、not only...but also前后不对齐（占35%）、以及并列名词与动名词混搭（占25%）。奇怪的是，这类错误更容易出现在"想写好"的学生中间——因为他们在尝试复杂结构时，忘了保持一致性。

## 一、什么是平行结构？

平行结构是指在一句话中，两个或两个以上并列的成分（词、短语、从句）使用相同的语法形式。

**对比：**
- ❌ 不平行：I enjoy **to travel, reading,** and **to cook.**
- ✅ 平行：I enjoy **travelling, reading,** and **cooking.**

- ❌ 不平行：She is smart, beautiful, and **has kindness.**
- ✅ 平行：She is **smart, beautiful,** and **kind.**

说白了就是"同样的事，用相同的写法"。

## 二、并列连词是最常见的触发点

以下连接词会触发平行结构：

| 连接词 | 不平行 | 平行 |
|--------|--------|------|
| and | He is tall **and** **a kind person.** | He is tall **and** **kind.** |
| or | You can **study hard** **or** **to give up.** | You can **study hard** **or** **give up.** |
| but | She is young **but** **has experience.** | She is young **but** **experienced.** |

## 三、三个"套餐结构"最容易出错

### 1. not only...but also...

**规则：** not only后面的结构和but also后面的结构必须完全一致。

- ❌ He **not only plays** basketball **but also** he is good at swimming.
- ✅ He **not only plays** basketball **but also swims** well.

- ❌ The trip **not only** was interesting **but also** **an educational experience.**
- ✅ The trip was **not only interesting** **but also educational.**

### 2. either...or... / neither...nor...

- ✅ You can **either** pay now **or** pay later.（动词短语平行）
- ✅ He is **neither** a teacher **nor** a student.（名词平行）
- ✅ She speaks **neither** loudly **nor** quickly.（副词平行）

### 3. prefer...rather than... / would rather...than...

- ✅ I **prefer** staying at home **rather than** going out.（ing平行）
- ✅ I **prefer to** stay at home **rather than** go out.（不定式平行）
- ✅ I **would rather** stay at home **than** go out.（动词原形平行）

## 四、写作中常见的三种平行结构对照

### 对照1：动词时态不一致

| ❌ 不平行 | ✅ 平行 |
|-----------|---------|
| He came in, **sat down,** and **was opening** his book. | He came in, **sat down,** and **opened** his book. |
| She likes **cooking** and **to bake.** | She likes **cooking** and **baking.** |
| The job requires **organizing** events and **to write** reports. | The job requires **organizing** events and **writing** reports. |

### 对照2：名词与动名词混搭

| ❌ 不平行 | ✅ 平行 |
|-----------|---------|
| His hobbies are **reading** and **golf.** | His hobbies are **reading** and **playing golf.** |
| She is interested in **art, music,** and **to travel.** | She is interested in **art, music,** and **travel.** |

### 对照3：从句与短语混搭

| ❌ 不平行 | ✅ 平行 |
|-----------|---------|
| I told him **to work hard** and **that he should be patient.** | I told him **to work hard** and **to be patient.** |
| He is a man **of courage** and **who is determined.** | He is a man **of courage** and **determination.** |

## 五、两个"平行陷阱"——容易忽视的情况

### 陷阱1：比较结构

在比较结构中，than前后的成分也需要平行。

- ❌ I enjoy playing basketball **more than** **to watch TV.**
- ✅ I enjoy playing basketball **more than** **watching TV.**

- ❌ Swimming is **as fun as** **to run.**
- ✅ Swimming is **as fun as** **running.**

### 陷阱2：列清单时

写作中用冒号后跟列表时，列表中的每一项形式要一致。

- ❌ She has three goals: **to get a job, saving money,** and **buy a car**.
- ✅ She has three goals: **to get a job, to save money,** and **to buy a car**.
（或全部用ing形式：getting a job, saving money, and buying a car）

## 六、写作自查清单

写完一个句子后，问自己三个问题：

1. **并列成分的语法形式是否一致？**
   ——如果一个是ing，另一个也应该是ing；如果一个是to do，另一个也应该是to do。

2. **not only...but also...两边对齐了吗？**
   ——not only后面接什么，but also后面就接什么。不要一边接句子一边接短语。

3. **and/or/but前后的结构相同吗？**
   ——如果是两个形容词并列就和形容词并列，不要一个形容词一个名词。

## 七、升级范例

**普通版（无平行结构）：**
> Reading books can improve your vocabulary, and you can also learn new knowledge and to think in English.

**升级版（注意平行结构）：**
> Reading books can improve your vocabulary, **expand your knowledge,** and **develop your English thinking skills.**

一个简单的平行结构调整，就让句子从"东拼西凑"变成了"整齐有力"。阅卷老师看这样的句子，自然会认为你的写作功底不错。
