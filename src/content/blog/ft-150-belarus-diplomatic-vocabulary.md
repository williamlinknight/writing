---

中国学生写作文时，最缺乏的一类词汇是**正式语体中的动词和形容词**。中高考和雅思作文中，只要涉及"争议""冲突""回应"等场景，大多数同学都只会用 `bad`、`wrong`、`stop` 这类基础词，得分自然上不去。

今天这篇FT报道讲的是一起真实的外交事件——瑞安航空客机被白俄罗斯当局迫降——其中包含大量正式文体中的高频词，对议论文提分极为实用。

---

## 📖 原文段落

> Two European airlines had to cancel flights to Moscow after Russian authorities failed to approve new flight paths that avoided Belarus's airspace in response to the **interception** of an EU commercial flight by the Belarusian regime.
>
> ...Belarusian president Alexander Lukashenko sent a military jet to **divert** a Vilnius-bound Ryanair flight carrying an opposition activist.
>
> Willie Walsh...has said it is "disturbing" to see a commercial flight diverted "for clearly **bogus** reasons".
>
> "I would like to think this does not **set a precedent**. It's important we build on public and united **condemnation**..."

---

## 🔑 五个必学表达

### 1️⃣ intercept / interception — 拦截

**原文：** `in response to the interception of an EU commercial flight`

**中文解释：** `intercept` 指"中途拦截、截获"，常用于军事、通信、交通语境。名词形式是 `interception`。这个词比简单的 `stop` 更正式、更专业。

**❌ 学生初版**
> The police stopped the car on the highway.

**✅ 升级版**
> The police intercepted the vehicle on the highway.

**💡 效果**
`intercept` 带有"半路截停"的主动性和计划感，比 `stop` 更正式，适合用于描述政府、机构采取的行动。

**例句：** The agency intercepted the suspicious shipment before it reached its destination.

---

### 2️⃣ divert — 使转向；改变航线

**原文：** `to divert a Vilnius-bound Ryanair flight`

**中文解释：** `divert` 表示"使……改变方向/路线"。在交通/航空语境中指"改变航线"，在更广的语境中指"转移注意力、资源"。

**❌ 学生初版**
> The heavy rain changed the plane's route.

**✅ 升级版**
> The heavy rain diverted the plane to another airport.

**💡 效果**
`divert` 比 `change direction` / `change route` 更简洁精准。用在议论文中可以表示"转移资源"或"分散注意力"——用处很广。

**例句：** The government should divert more resources to public healthcare.

💡 更多用法：
- `divert attention from` = 转移注意力（常用！）
> Critics say the announcement was an attempt to divert attention from the real problems.

---

### 3️⃣ bogus — 虚假的；伪造的

**原文：** `for clearly bogus reasons`

**中文解释：** `bogus` 是一个比 `fake` 更正式、更有力的形容词，表示"虚假的、伪造的"，常用于描述借口、理由、文件、指控等。

**❌ 学生初版**
> He made a fake excuse for being late.

**✅ 升级版**
> He gave a bogus excuse for being late.

**💡 效果**
`bogus` 比 `fake` 更适合正式文体，且带有"蓄意欺骗"的评判色彩。在反驳某个观点时格外有力。

**例句：** The company was accused of using bogus claims to attract investors.

---

### 4️⃣ set a precedent — 开创先例

**原文：** `I would like to think this does not set a precedent`

**中文解释：** `precedent` 是"先例"，`set a precedent` 是"开创先例/树立先例"。常用于讨论某件事的后果——如果这次允许，以后就会成为惯例。

**❌ 学生初版**
> If we allow this, the same thing will happen again in the future.

**✅ 升级版**
> If we allow this, it will set a dangerous precedent.

**💡 效果**
`set a precedent` 是一个固定搭配，简洁地道。一个短语就能表达"开了这个口子以后就麻烦了"的完整意思。

**例句：** The court's decision set a precedent for similar cases in the future. / Critics warned that the policy would set a dangerous precedent for government overreach.

---

### 5️⃣ condemnation — 谴责

**原文：** `build on public and united condemnation`

**中文解释：** `condemnation` 是 `condemn` 的名词形式，表示"强烈的谴责"。比 `criticism` 语气更重、更正式。在国际关系、社会争议的语境中最常用。

**❌ 学生初版**
> Many people criticized the government's decision.

**✅ 升级版**
> The government's decision drew widespread condemnation.

**💡 效果**
`condemnation` 比 `criticism` 语气更强，适合正式语境。常用于：`draw condemnation` / `face condemnation` / `express condemnation`。

**例句：** The policy drew condemnation from human rights groups around the world. / The UN issued a strong condemnation of the attack.

---

## 📝 写作建议

今天这5个词的共同特点是：**不是"日常单词"，而是"正式文体单词"。** 中国学生普遍的问题，不是词汇量不够大，而是**语体意识薄弱**——该用正式词时用了口语词，该用具体词时用了笼统词。

具体到考试：
- **中考/高考议论文**：`divert`、`bogus`、`condemnation` 这类词能让你的作文和同龄人拉开明显差距
- **雅思大作文**：`set a precedent`、`intercept` 这类搭配直接对应高分范文的用词水平

一定要把这些词写下来、造句、在一篇作文里刻意用上2-3个——用了才是你的。
