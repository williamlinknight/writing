## Agree / Disagree Essays — 同意/不同意

### 1. 广告 (Advertising)
Some people believe that advertising manipulates consumers into buying unnecessary products. Others argue that advertisements provide useful information. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 2. 替代医学 (Alternative Medicine)
Some people believe that alternative medicine is harmful, while others think it is effective. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

### 3. 艺术开支 (Arts Spending)
Some people think that governments should not spend money on supporting the arts (e.g., music and painting), and instead they should spend more on public services. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 4. 人工智能 (Artificial Intelligence)
Some people think that artificial intelligence will replace most jobs in the future. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 5. 社交媒体的影响 (Effects of Social Media)
Some people believe that social media has improved communication, while others think it has led to a decline in real-life social interaction. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 6. 航空税 (Airline Tax)
Some people believe that increasing taxes on air travel is the best way to reduce air pollution. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 7. 免费大学教育 (Free University Education)
Some people think that university education should be free for everyone, regardless of their financial background. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 8. 科学研究 (Scientific Research)
Some people argue that governments should fund scientific research rather than private companies. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 9. 公共场所禁烟 (Ban Smoking in Public Places)
Smoking should be banned in all public places. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 10. 雇佣老年人 (Employing Older People)
Some believe that older employees are more valuable to a company than younger ones. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 11. 素食主义 (Vegetarianism)
Some people argue that adopting a vegetarian diet is better for individuals and the environment. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 12. 纳税 (Paying Taxes)
Some people believe that paying taxes is the most important civic duty. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 13. 远程办公 (Remote Working)
Some people believe that working from home is more beneficial than working in an office. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 14. 人工智能在教育中的应用 (AI in Education)
Some people believe that AI should be widely used in education, while others think it will negatively impact students. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 15. 技术发展 (Technological Development)
Some people believe that technology is making life more complex rather than simpler. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 16. 濒危语言 (Dying Languages)
Governments should take measures to preserve languages that are at risk of extinction. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### 17. 自动驾驶汽车 (Self-driving Cars)
Some people believe that self-driving cars will make roads safer, while others think they will increase traffic problems. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

## Discuss Both Views and Give Your Own Opinion — 讨论两种观点

### 18. 大学教育 (University Education)
Some people think that university education should focus on practical skills, while others believe that theoretical knowledge is more important. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

### 19. 减少犯罪 (Reducing Crime)
Some people argue that increasing police presence is the best way to reduce crime, while others believe that improving education and social conditions is more effective. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

### 20. 儿童发展 (Child Development)
Some people think that children should be raised in a strict environment, while others believe that a more relaxed approach is better. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

### 21. 工作与生活平衡 (Work-Life Balance)
Some people believe that work-life balance is important for employees, while others argue that success requires full dedication to work. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

### 22. 机器人在社会中的作用 (Robots in Society)
Some people believe that robots will take over most human jobs, while others think that they will create more job opportunities. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

### 23. 慈善捐款 (Donating Money to Charity)
Some people prefer to donate to local charities, while others believe it is better to donate to international organizations. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

### 24. 进口外国影视 (Importing Foreign Films and TV Shows)
Some people think that importing foreign films and TV programs is beneficial for cultural exchange, while others believe it is harmful to local culture. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

## Advantages and Disadvantages — 优缺点

### 25. 在线购物 (Online Shopping)
Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of online shopping compared to traditional shopping.

### 26. 远程工作 (Remote Work)
What are the advantages and disadvantages of working from home instead of working in an office?

### 27. 社交媒体的影响 (Social Media's Influence)
Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of using social media for communication.

### 28. 旅游业 (Tourism Industry)
What are the advantages and disadvantages of the increasing popularity of tourism?

### 29. 公共交通 (Public Transport)
Discuss the benefits and drawbacks of using public transportation instead of private cars.

## Causes, Effects, Solutions Essays — 原因、影响、解决方案

### 30. 儿童肥胖 (Child Obesity)
What are the causes of childhood obesity, and how can it be addressed?

### 31. 全球变暖 (Global Warming)
Discuss the causes and effects of global warming and suggest possible solutions.

### 32. 久坐生活方式 (Sedentary Lifestyle)
What are the causes and effects of a sedentary lifestyle, and how can people be encouraged to be more active?

### 33. 心理健康问题 (Mental Health Issues)
What are the main causes of increasing mental health problems, and how can they be solved?

### 34. 城市污染 (Urban Pollution)
Discuss the causes and effects of urban pollution and suggest solutions.

### 35. 老龄化社会 (Aging Population)
What are the causes and consequences of an aging population, and how can this issue be managed?

### 36. 电子垃圾 (E-waste)
What are the causes of electronic waste, and how can it be reduced?

### 37. 就业竞争 (Job Market Competition)
What are the causes of intense job competition, and how can job seekers improve their chances of employment?

## 💡 备考建议

- **Agree/Disagree** 是最常考的题型，占 2020-2024 题库的近一半
- **Discuss Both Views** 近年来比例上升，尤其是教育类和科技类话题
- **优缺点题** 和 **原因解决题** 结构较固定，记好模板即可
- **高频话题重叠区：** AI、社交媒体、远程工作 这三个话题在多种题型中出现，务必重点准备
