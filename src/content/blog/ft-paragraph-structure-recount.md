---

很多学生写英语作文，段落看起来"平平的"——每个句子都在说差不多的事，没有推进感。真正的段落是**有方向的**：从概括到具体，从事实到评价，从过去到现在。

FT №1是一篇仅296词的短文，讨论的是2020年美国大选后的"重新计票"(Recount)。但它的绝妙之处在于，**每个段落只用2-4句话，就完成了一个完整的叙事推进**。今天我们从中提炼五种段落结构模式。

---

## 一、定义-扩展式：Definition + Context + Significance

开篇段既要解释核心词，又要引入写作背景。

**原文：**

> **Recount** (noun) the act of tallying voter ballots — again.
>
> Many US elections have been subject to recounts, permitted (depending on the state) when a candidate's margin of victory is narrow — usually less than 1 per cent. But no presidential election in modern memory has been subjected to as many challenges as 2020's between Donald Trump and Joe Biden.

**结构拆解：**

- **定义先行**：用破折号引出简洁定义（the act of... again）
- **一般背景**：什么情况下允许recount（narrow margin）
- **特殊例外**：But... 2020年打破了所有先例

**公式：** Key Term + Definition → General Rule → Exception/Significance

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
A recount means counting votes again. It happens when an election is close. The 2020 election had many recounts.

**✅ 升级版**
**Recount** — the act of tallying voter ballots again. Recounts are permitted when a candidate's margin is narrow, usually less than 1 per cent. But no presidential election in modern memory has faced as many challenges as the 2020 race between Donald Trump and Joe Biden.

**💡 效果**
先给定义锚定概念，再用"一般→例外"的对比拉出文章张力。读者读完就知道：这不是一篇科普文，而是一篇有观点的评论。

## 二、行动-结果式：Action Taken → Result Achieved → Implication

中间段的核心功能是**推进叙事**：做了什么，发生了什么，意味着什么。

**原文：**

> Mr Trump's legal team twice demanded recounts in the state of Georgia. Neither process changed the result, which delivered a slim victory to Mr Biden. They also triggered recounts in two Wisconsin counties, which resulted in slightly larger totals for Mr Biden.

**结构拆解：**

- **行动**：Twice demanded recounts in Georgia
- **结果**：Neither changed the result
- **延伸**：Wisconsin recounts → larger totals for Biden

注意：**仅三句话，讲完了两个州的重新计票**。每句话都在推进：Georgia第一次→没改变→Wisconsin反而更糟。

**公式：** Specific Action → Neutral Result → Unexpected Outcome

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
Trump asked for a recount in Georgia. It didn't work. Then there were recounts in Wisconsin. Biden got more votes.

**✅ 升级版**
Mr Trump's legal team twice demanded recounts in Georgia — neither changed the result. In Wisconsin, recounts in two counties actually delivered slightly larger totals for Mr Biden.

**💡 效果**
用破折号连接行动与结果，用"actually"暗示反预期。三句话压缩成两句话，信息密度翻倍，逻辑链条不变。

## 三、让步-转折式：Concede + Contrast + Rebuttal

高级段落结构：先承认一部分事实（让步），再用更强的事实反转论点。

**原文：**

> Though unusual, these recounts were within the rules. Mr Trump's supporters broke all precedent, however, by petitioning the US Supreme Court to overturn the results in four swing states that tipped the race against him.

**结构拆解：**

- **让步句**：Though unusual... within the rules（承认合理）
- **转折句**：broke all precedent...（强调越界）
- **程度升级**：petitioning the Supreme Court（从州级到最高法院）

**公式：** Though + [admit limitation], [contrast marker] + [stronger action]

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
The recounts were legal. But Trump supporters did something more extreme. They asked the Supreme Court to help.

**✅ 升级版**
Though unusual, the recounts were within the rules. Mr Trump's supporters, however, broke all precedent by petitioning the Supreme Court to overturn the results in four swing states.

**💡 效果**
让步-转折结构产生"虽然合法，但过了线"的批判性议论。破折号或逗号隔开的同位语（Mr Trump's supporters, however,）比"but"更正式。

## 四、递进叠加式：One Action → Compound Reaction → Outcome

当想说"对方做了很多事"时，不要逐条列举，而是用递进叠加。

**原文：**

> In addition, Mr Trump's team filed 60 lawsuits in swing states that sought to throw out absentee ballots or other kinds of postal vote, or find other ways to alter the outcome.

**结构拆解：**

- **核心动作**：filed 60 lawsuits
- **目的一**：throw out absentee ballots
- **目的二**：find other ways to alter the outcome
- **衔接词**：In addition（承接上一段）

**公式：** [Transition] + [Core Action: number + object] + [Purpose 1] or [Purpose 2], or [Purpose 3]

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
Trump's team filed many lawsuits. They wanted to change the election. They also wanted to throw away some votes.

**✅ 升级版**
In addition, Mr Trump's team filed 60 lawsuits in swing states — seeking to throw out absentee ballots, invalidate postal votes, or find any other way to alter the outcome.

**💡 效果**
一个长句用三个并列目的动词，密度高、节奏快。数字（60 lawsuits）做锚点，比"many"具体10倍。

## 五、结论-留白式：Summary Judgment + Warning Preview

结尾段两个功能：总结判断（系统work了）+ 留下思考（但未来呢？）。

**原文：**

> The net result of 2020's legal shenanigans is that the US constitutional guardrails did their job. The system worked. But Mr Trump's onslaught of demands could set a precedent for future elections, in spite of the fact that 2020 produced no significant evidence of fraud.
>
> The fear is that what was once unthinkable — that a presidential candidate would refuse to accept an election outcome — will now be seen as possible.

**结构拆解：**

- **短句判断**：The system worked.（极致简洁）
- **转折预警**：But... could set a precedent
- **升华**：what was once unthinkable... will now be seen as possible

**公式：** Short Verdict + [But] Future Risk + Open-Ended Thought

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
In conclusion, the system worked well. But there might be problems in the future because of what Trump did.

**✅ 升级版**
The system worked. But Mr Trump's onslaught could set a dangerous precedent — what was once unthinkable will now be seen as possible.

**💡 效果**
短句（The system worked.）+ 长句（But...precedent）+ 升华句（unthinkable→possible），**节奏由快到慢，格局由具体到抽象**。结尾要有"余音"，不是"句号"。

---

## 写作建议

这篇仅296词的短文展示了一个重要原则：**段落的核心是"推进"，不是"重复"**。写每段时问自己：这一段比上一段说了什么新东西？

- 开篇段：定义+背景+张力
- 中间段：行动+结果+延伸（或让步+反转）
- 结尾段：判断+警示+留白

把这些结构模板用到你的英语作文里，段落立刻有了"层次感"和"方向感"。
