---

上篇我们学了四种基础段落结构，今天换一个角度：**当段落需要呈现"问题→应对措施"的逻辑链条时，该怎么组织？**

FT №6报道五家航空公司推出数字健康通行证CommonPass。这篇文章在段落结构上的最大亮点是：每个段落既独立成立，又沿着"问题–方案–反应–分歧"的叙事线推进。我们来拆解它的五种段落模式。

---

## 一、问题-解决式：Problem Context + Solution Preview

开篇段同时交代背景问题和解方案方向，一句话抓住读者注意力。

**原文：**

> Five global airlines are to start offering passengers use of a digital health pass to certify they are Covid-free before travel, as the sector seeks to navigate its way out of a historic collapse in passenger demand.

**结构拆解：**

- **解决方案前置**：Five airlines... start offering a digital health pass
- **背景问题后置**：as the sector seeks to navigate... a historic collapse

**公式：** [Solution doer + action + what] + as [problem context + motivation]

这种"先给答案，再交代背景"的结构，比"先讲问题再说方案"更有冲击力。

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
The airline industry has many problems because of the pandemic. So some companies decide to use a health pass.

**✅ 升级版**
Three major airlines are to launch a digital health pass system, as the travel industry seeks to recover from a year-long collapse in international passenger demand.

**💡 效果**
升级版的"答案先行"结构让读者先看到行动方案，再了解原因，逻辑顺而不拖沓。用 `as` 连接两个层次，比 `because / so` 更凝练。

---

## 二、具体细节式：从概述到实例的递进

第二段紧接第一段的方案，提供具体细节——谁参与、何时启动、叫什么名字。

**原文：**

> United Airlines, Lufthansa, Virgin Atlantic, Swiss International Air Lines and JetBlue will begin rolling out the so-called CommonPass to passengers on some flights from December.

**结构拆解：**

- **具体参与者**：列举5家具体公司
- **具体时间**：from December
- **具体产品名**：CommonPass

**公式：** [Specific actors] + [specific action + timeline] + [specific product/name]

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
Many companies will use this new system. It will be available next month.

**✅ 升级版**
Singapore Airlines, Cathay Pacific and Emirates will begin rolling out the so-called TravelPass to passengers on selected routes from January, with plans to expand to all destinations by March.

**💡 效果**
具体名字（Singapore Airlines/Cathay/Emirates）、具体时间（January/March）、具体系统名（TravelPass）——这三个"具体"让一段话从"泛泛而谈"变成"有信息量"。

---

## 三、技术原理式：How it works + 关键限定

介绍一个新技术时，需要说清楚原理，同时点出它的界限（不是什么）。

**原文：**

> The project, developed by non-profit group The Commons Project and backed by the World Economic Forum, uses a digital certificate downloaded to a mobile phone to show a passenger has tested negative for Covid-19. Users can then use the certificate as proof of a negative test if the country they are visiting requires one. The airlines are not making the CommonPass mandatory, but in time it will also be used to provide proof of vaccination.

**结构拆解：**

- **谁开发**：developed by... backed by...
- **怎么用**：uses... to show...
- **什么条件下用**：if the country requires one
- **不是什么（关键限定）**：not making it mandatory

**公式：** [Developed by / backed by] → [How it works] → [Usage condition] → [What it is NOT]

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
The system can check if people are healthy. It is very useful.

**✅ 升级版**
The platform, developed by a leading health-tech startup and backed by the National Health Commission, uses a smartphone-based digital ID to verify a person's vaccination status. Users can present the ID at venues that require proof. However, the system is not compulsory — citizens can still provide paper documents if they prefer.

**💡 效果**
学生版只说"有用"，升级版交代了"谁做的→怎么运作→什么情况下用→什么情况下不用"。最后一句的限定（not compulsory）尤其重要——说明技术的边界，比一味吹捧更显客观。

## 四、数据支撑式：Data as Evidence

用具体数字支撑论点，而不是空喊"情况很严重"。

**原文：**

> On Tuesday Iata warned that global airlines faced combined losses of 118.5bn this year, deeper than the 84.3bn forecast in June, and 39bn next year. Passenger numbers are forecast to fall 60 per cent on last year to 1.3bn.

**结构拆解：**

- **数据锚定**：118.5bn loss this year
- **对比数据**：deeper than 84.3bn forecast in June
- **延伸数据**：39bn next year / 60% fall to 1.3bn

**公式：** [Key data point] + [Comparative data] + [Supporting data / projection]

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
The situation is very serious. Many companies lose a lot of money.

**✅ 升级版**
A recent industry report warned that the manufacturing sector faced combined losses of ¥200bn this year, nearly double the ¥108bn recorded in the previous quarter. Exports are projected to fall by nearly 35 per cent compared to last year.

**💡 效果**
数据的力量来自于对比——"nearly double" 让读者直观感受到恶化速度，而不是只给一个孤立的数字。学生版缺少"参照系"，所以传递不了紧迫感。

## 五、观点分歧式：One side... However, other sides...

当段落需要展示不同立场时，用对比结构呈现分歧，而不是只说自己知道的。

**原文：**

> No major airline has said it will follow the Australian carrier's lead, with easyJet and Ryanair both telling the Financial Times they do not expect to require vaccinations on their flights.

> "Under the EU system of free movement, we believe quarantine restrictions will be removed in spring 2021 once effective vaccines become available to protect high-risk groups from Covid-19," Ryanair said.

**结构拆解：**

- **分歧线**：No major... has said it will follow (否定+对比)
- **立场A（澳航）**：要求疫苗
- **立场B（易捷+瑞安）**：不要求疫苗 + 原因（free movement框架下的逻辑）
- **直接引语佐证**：Ryanair的直接表态

**公式：** [Statement of divergence] + [Position A] vs [Position B] + [Direct quote supporting B]

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
Some people think it's good, but some people think it's bad.

**✅ 升级版**
No major tech company has followed Apple's lead in removing the charger from the box, with Samsung and Xiaomi both telling industry media they do not expect to adopt a similar policy. "Consumers expect a complete product. Removing accessories would damage brand trust," a Xiaomi spokesperson said.

**💡 效果**
分歧段不是"两边各说一句"，而是要指出：谁做了（Apple）、谁没做（Samsung/Xiaomi）、为什么没做（引语交代理由）。具体的公司名+行动+引语，三段信息缺一不可。

---

## 总结

今天的五种段落结构，从功能角度可以归纳为：

| 段落类型 | 核心句式 | 一句话记住 |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| 问题-解决式 | [Solution] + as + [problem] | 先给答案，再交代问题 |
| 具体细节式 | 具体主体+具体时间+具体名称 | 让信息看得见 |
| 技术原理式 | How + what it's NOT | 说清边界，比吹捧更可信 |
| 数据支撑式 | 数据+对比+延伸 | 数字要配对才有意义 |
| 观点分歧式 | No major... with... both... | 用具体反对者制造张力 |

**练习建议：** 拿出你最近写的一篇议论文，找出最单薄的一个段落，用今天学的公式重写。先问自己：这个段落的功能是什么？——是解释方案、呈现数据、还是展示分歧？
