## 为什么对比论证是高分作文的核心？

雅思写作Task 2、高考读后续写、中考英语作文——几乎每一种议论文题目，都要求你做**对比**：

- 在家学习 vs. 在校学习
- 大城市发展 vs. 小城市生活
- 传统媒体 vs. 新兴媒体

大部分中国学生写对比只有三板斧：

```text
But... / However... / On the other hand...
```

这些用多了，文章结构扁平，读起来像套模板。

今天我们看《金融时报》一篇**Robinhood IPO**的报道——一个被誉为"Lex专栏"的商业评论——从中提取3种**专业记者写对比**的结构，让你的议论文瞬间提升一个档次。

> **📖 素材来源：** FT №179 — *Robinhood IPO: model is a tempting target for regulators*

---

## 技巧一：用鲜明的数字对比建立论点

很多同学写对比时只说"不同"，但没说"差多少"。FT的做法是：**用具体数字把差距钉在读者眼前**。

看原文：

> Revenue **more than tripled** to 958m last year...
> That explosive growth has continued in 2021. Revenue already **topped 522m** in the first quarter.
> A viral meme-stock and cryptocurrency trading boom **helped the platform to more than double** the number of funded accounts over the past 12 months to 18m.

这里用了三个层层递进的数据：
1. 营收增长 **3倍多** → 去年958m
2. 今年Q1已超 **522m** → 增长在延续
3. 付费账户数 **翻倍** → 1800万

**写作应用：**

写对比时，不要只写"A比B大"，要写出**具体的差距**：

| ❌ 模糊对比 | ✅ 数据对比 |
|---|---|
| Robinhood grew very fast in 2020. | Revenue **more than tripled** to 958m in 2020. |
| More people use the app now. | The number of funded accounts **more than doubled** to 18m. |
| The company is profitable but not very. | Charles Schwab made **3.3bn** in profit last year, against Robinhood's **7m**. |

**公式：** `倍数词(more than doubled / tripled / quadrupled) + 具体数字(to Xm / to Y million)`

> 这个公式学生可以直接背进作文。写"科技对生活的影响"时，别写"Technology changed our lives a lot"，写"Smartphone usage **more than tripled** to 3.8 billion users over the past decade."

## 技巧二：用"反之亦然"句式做反向对比

FT在描述完Robinhood的增长后，笔锋一转，写风险。这个转折不是简单用"however"，而是用了一个非常高级的结构：

> **But** the filing also highlights just how **vulnerable** Robinhood's business model is **to regulatory scrutiny**.

然后紧接着用数据做反向对比：

> Revenue **more than tripled** last year → **But** the model is **vulnerable**
>
> 40bn **valuation** → **vs.** Charles Schwab trades on just **12 times** earnings

这才是高级论证：**先说正面，然后指出反面，两边都有数据支撑。**

FT甚至在第8段写了一个教科书级的对比段落：

> **Add to this** Robinhood's meagre profitability **and** the 40bn valuation the company is seeking **looks steep**.
>
> **Assuming** the app can double its revenue this year, **that still implies** a valuation of 20 times that.
>
> **Charles Schwab**, which made 3.3bn in profit last year **against** Robinhood's 7m, trades on a ratio of just 12 times.

看这个结构：
- Add to this [A] and [B] looks steep → 点出问题
- Assuming...that still implies... → 退一步论证（即使好的假设，结论也不乐观）
- [Company X], which...against [Company Y] → 用对手做对比参照

**写作应用：**

写"线上学习的利弊"时：

> **Add to this** the lack of face-to-face interaction **and** the efficiency of online learning **looks questionable**.
>
> **Assuming** students can stay focused throughout a 40-minute online class, **that still implies** a significant gap compared to in-person discussion.
>
> **Traditional classrooms**, where teachers can **instantly** read students' facial expressions **against** a computer screen, create a feedback loop that online platforms struggle to match.

## 技巧三：用"if...then..."的假设对比制造张力

FT Lex专栏的结尾太漂亮了，我直接放上来：

> **If** the stock tanks on opening day...**it also risks** further public outcry from an army of unhappy buyers.

注意这个结构：
- **If + 假设条件** → 提出一个不利场景
- **If的反面(implied)** → 如果IPO成功会怎样？
- **then + 风险** → 无论成败，都可能有问题

不写"如果这样就这样"，而是写**"即使在最乐观的假设下，也..."**——这种反转式的假设论证，就是Lex专栏出彩的地方。

再看第5段更经典的：

> **Any changes** could **deal a big blow** to Robinhood's business model.
>
> As it does not charge clients a fee on making stock trades, the company makes the **bulk** of its revenue from payment-for-order flows.
>
> **These accounted for about 63 per cent** of Robinhood's first quarter revenue.

结构清晰：
1. **Claim** (Any changes could deal a big blow)
2. **Reason** (because the company...)
3. **Evidence** (63 per cent of revenue)

**写作应用：**

> **Any changes** to the current exam system could **deal a big blow** to students' learning habits.
>
> As many students rely on **standardised tests** as their only motivation, a sudden shift might leave them without a clear direction.
>
> **Exams account for over 80 per cent** of high school students' primary study goals, according to a recent survey.

## 本课总结

| 技巧 | 核心句式 | 适用场景 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 数据对比 | `more than doubled/tripled to [number]` | 描述变化、增长、差距 |
| 反向对比 | `Add to this [A] and [B] looks [adj]` / `...against...` | 先扬后抑、全面论证 |
| 假设对比 | `If...then...` / `Any [change] could [effect]` | 分析利弊、预测后果 |

这三个结构，不是背模板，而是**思维框架**——对比不是"A和B不一样"，而是"A在这样的条件下，比B好/差这么多"。
