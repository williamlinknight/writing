描写"经济压力"是中国学生最常遇到的场景之一——不管是讨论通货膨胀、企业竞争还是政府补贴，都会用到"抵制压力""传导效应""承受冲击"这些语义。问题是，大多数学生只会在 **resist** 和 **pressure** 之间打转。

今天的素材来自FT关于再保险（reinsurance）行业的一则短讯。再保险是保险公司买来分担极端天气损失的"保险的保险"。当自然灾害频发，再保险公司面临降价压力——这场博弈中的每一个动词，都在教我们如何写出有"力量感"的经济类写作。

---

## 📖 FT原文选段

> Property reinsurers **are resisting calls to** lower prices or **soften terms** for cover against extreme weather, brokers say, **signalling** there will be **no let-up** in affordability pressures that have had **ripple effects** throughout the global economy.
>
> Property catastrophe reinsurance... **has surged in price** over the past 18 months, **supporting a recovery** in reinsurers' underwriting profits and share prices.
>
> Senior industry figures **have called on** reinsurers to cut prices and increase the amount of risk they **are willing to absorb**, to **relieve pressure** on insurers and their customers.

> **中文对照**
>
> 据经纪人表示，财产再保险公司正在抵制降低价格或放宽极端天气保险条款的呼吁，这表明已对全球经济产生连锁反应的负担压力不会减弱。
>
> 财产巨灾再保险价格在过去18个月飙升，支撑了再保险公司承保利润和股价的回升。
>
> 业内资深人士呼吁再保险公司降低价格，增加他们愿意承担的风险量，以减轻保险公司及其客户的压力。

## 🎯 三大类词汇解析

### 第一类：「抵制」——如何表达"拒绝服从压力"

#### 1️⃣ resist calls to / soften terms

**原文出处：** "Property reinsurers **are resisting calls to** lower prices or **soften terms** for cover..."

**为什么好：** 学生对"拒绝做某事"只写 **refuse to**。**resist calls to** 字面是"抵制……的呼吁"，语气比 **refuse** 更正式、更专业——暗示外界有压力，但主体在坚持不动。**soften terms**（放宽条款）是一个商务固定搭配，对应中文"松口/让步"。

**❌ 学生初版**
The company **refused to** change its policy.

**✅ 升级版**
The regulator **is resisting calls to** relax lending rules, insisting that tighter controls are necessary.

**💡 效果：** "抵制呼吁"比"拒绝做"多了一层"承受压力而坚持"的意味，议论文中更有张力。

#### 2️⃣ signal / no let-up

**原文出处：** "...**signalling** there will be **no let-up** in affordability pressures..."

**为什么好：** 学生写"表明/暗示"只写 **show** 或 **mean**。**signal** 是"发出信号"，比 **show** 更精准——暗示有意识地传递信息。**no let-up** 是一个极有力量的名词短语（let-up = 减弱/放松），比 **will not stop** 简洁得多。

**❌ 学生初版**
This **shows** that the problem **will not stop**.

**✅ 升级版**
The latest economic data **signals** that there will be **no let-up** in housing affordability pressures for young buyers.

**💡 效果：** signal 给"表明"注入了"发出信号"的画面感；no let-up 用两个词就表达了"没有缓解"的全部含义。

---

### 第二类：「传导」——如何表达"影响的扩散"

#### 3️⃣ ripple effects

**原文出处：** "...pressures that have had **ripple effects** throughout the global economy."

**为什么好：** 学生写"影响很大/波及广泛"只写 **affect** 或 **have a big impact on**。**ripple effects**（涟漪效应）是一个经济学/社会学领域的专门术语——用一个比喻（扔石头入水产生的涟漪）精确描述了"一个事件引发一系列连锁反应"。

**❌ 学生初版**
The policy **affected** many industries in the country.

**✅ 升级版**
The new trade tariffs are expected to have **ripple effects** throughout the supply chain, from raw material suppliers to retail stores.

**💡 效果：** 从模糊的 affect 升级为具象的 ripple effect，信息量瞬间翻倍。在雅思大作文中，这个词能让考官一眼看出你的词汇深度。

#### 4️⃣ has surged in price / supporting a recovery

**原文出处：** "...**has surged in price** over the past 18 months, **supporting a recovery** in reinsurers' underwriting profits..."

**为什么好：** 这里展示了一个"因→果"的高效写法。前面用 **surge in price**（价格飙升）描述现象，后面用 **supporting**（支撑/推动）引出结果。**surge** 比 **increase** 或 **rise** 更有冲击力，暗示急剧的、大幅的上涨。**supporting a recovery** 中的 **support** 比 **cause** 或 **lead to** 更精准——不是"导致"复苏，而是"支撑/维持"复苏。

**❌ 学生初版**
Prices **increased a lot**, and this **caused** the industry to recover.

**✅ 升级版**
Housing prices **have surged** in major cities over the past two years, **supporting a recovery** in the construction sector.

**💡 效果：** surge 比 increase a lot 简洁70%，supporting 也比 causing 更准确（暗示因果关系中的辅助角色）。

---

### 第三类：「受力」——如何表达"施加与承受"

#### 5️⃣ called on / absorb / relieve pressure

**原文出处：** "Senior industry figures **have called on** reinsurers to cut prices and increase the amount of risk they **are willing to absorb**, to **relieve pressure** on insurers..."

**为什么好：** 这三组动词构成了一条完整的"施压→受力→缓解"链：**called on**（呼吁/要求）→ **absorb**（吸收/承受）→ **relieve pressure**（缓解压力）。学生写"要求某人做某事"只写 **asked**，**called on** 更正式、更有权威感。**absorb** 比 **take** 或 **bear** 更精准——字面是"吸收"，比喻"承受（风险/冲击）"。**relieve pressure** 比 **reduce stress** 更学术化。

**❌ 学生初版**
The government **asked** companies to **take** more responsibility to **reduce problems**.

**✅ 升级版**
The government **has called on** manufacturers to **absorb** higher raw material costs themselves, in order to **relieve pressure** on consumers.

**💡 效果：** called on → asked 的升级（正式+权威）；absorb → take 的升级（精准）；relieve pressure → reduce problems 的升级（具体化）。一套组合拳直接拉高写作层次。

## 📝 考场实战模板

**描述"经济压力与抵制"的高分句型：**

> **[主体] is/are resisting calls to [动作]，signalling that there will be no let-up in [压力来源]，which could have ripple effects on [受影响方].**

**范文示例（科技与教育）：**
> Many public universities **are resisting calls to** raise tuition fees, **signalling that there will be no let-up** in the financial strain on their budgets, **which could have ripple effects on** the quality of education and faculty recruitment.

> **[现象] has surged, supporting a recovery in [受益领域], while [受影响方] are being asked to absorb [冲击] to relieve pressure on [保护对象].**

**范文示例（环境与城市）：**
> Pollution levels **have surged** in the city over the past summer, **supporting** a booming business in air purifiers, **while** low-income residents **are being asked to absorb** the health costs **to relieve pressure on** industrial polluters.
