写议论文时，最怕什么？最怕你只会写 `The government should solve this problem`。政策类话题（移民、环保、教育改革）中，真正的高分表达不是"解决问题"本身，而是描述**"在面对困境时各方做了什么反应"**。

今天我们从FT一篇《美墨边境闯关者增多令拜登难办》中，提炼5个政策困境类的高分表达。

---

## FT原文精选

> The Biden administration is **struggling to address** an **influx** of unaccompanied minors attempting to cross the US southern border as figures confirmed a **sharp jump** in attempted crossings.
>
> While it has placed blame for the current border crisis on the previous White House, it has struggled to simultaneously **tout a promising message** to migrants while **deterring** people from crossing into the country outside the proper legal channels.
>
> Republican lawmakers have **hit out at** the administration for not doing enough to **stem** the crisis.

---

## 词汇解析

### 1. struggle to address — 难以应对、疲于招架

**原文出处：** "The Biden administration is struggling to address an influx of unaccompanied minors."

**中文解释：**
`struggle to address` 比 `try to solve` 或 `fail to handle` 都更有层次感——它暗示"虽然努力了，但很难应付"。这个短语出现在几乎每一篇描述政策挑战的报道中。

**❌ 学生初版**
```text
The government tried to solve the housing problem.
```

**✅ 升级版**
```text
Local authorities are struggling to address the growing homelessness crisis.
```

**💡 效果**
`struggle to address` 比 `try to solve` 更诚实、更有动态感——读者能感受到"面对困境的努力"。

**💡 例句**
```text
Many schools are struggling to address the widening achievement gap between rich and poor students.
```

### 2. influx — （人/物）大量涌入

**原文出处：** "an influx of unaccompanied minors attempting to cross the US southern border"

**中文解释：**
`influx` 特指"大量涌入"（通常是人、资金、信息）。它比 `a lot of people coming` 地道多了，而且是雅思读写高频词。

**❌ 学生初版**
```text
A lot of tourists came to the city.
```

**✅ 升级版**
```text
The influx of international tourists placed immense pressure on local infrastructure.
```

**💡 例句**
```text
The sudden influx of foreign investment transformed the city's skyline within a decade.
```

### 3. tout a message — 宣扬、大力宣传（某种信息）

**原文出处：** "struggled to simultaneously tout a promising message to migrants while deterring people from crossing"

**中文解释：**
`tout` 不是简单的 `say` 或 `announce`，而是"大力宣传、兜售某种说法/信息"。媒体常用这个词描述**一个矛盾动作**——一方面要释放欢迎信号，另一方面要阻止实际行动。

**❌ 学生初版**
```text
The government said that immigration was good.
```

**✅ 升级版**
```text
The minister touted the policy's benefits while acknowledging its implementation challenges.
```

**💡 例句**
```text
Universities tout their global rankings to attract international students, while quietly raising tuition fees.
```

### 4. deter...from — 阻止、威慑（某人做某事）

**原文出处：** "deterring people from crossing into the country outside the proper legal channels"

**中文解释：**
`deter ... from doing` 是正式文体的"阻止某人做某事"，强调"通过威慑/恐惧/警告等手段让人不敢做"。比 `stop/prevent` 更精细——它不是物理阻拦，而是心理威慑。

**❌ 学生初版**
```text
The government wanted to stop people from breaking the law.
```

**✅ 升级版**
```text
Stricter penalties alone cannot deter young people from using social media irresponsibly.
```

**💡 例句**
```text
The rising cost of college tuition is deterring many students from pursuing higher education.
```

### 5. hit out at / stem the crisis — 猛烈抨击 / 遏制危机

**原文出处：** "Republican lawmakers have hit out at the administration for not doing enough to stem the crisis."

**中文解释：**
`hit out at` 是"猛烈抨击"，比 `criticize` 更有攻击性。`stem` 是"遏制（不好的趋势）"，如 stem the tide（遏制潮流）、stem the spread（遏制扩散）。

**❌ 学生初版**
```text
Many people criticized the government for failing to stop the problem.
```

**✅ 升级版**
```text
Environmental groups hit out at the government for failing to stem the loss of biodiversity.
```

**💡 例句**
```text
Parents hit out at the education department for not doing enough to stem the rise in student anxiety.
```

---

## 写作建议

这5个表达的核心价值在于：它们让你从"描述事实"升级到**"描述各方反应"**。

写议论文时，不要只写 `The problem is serious`。试试用这些结构：
- `struggle to address` — 描述当局的无力感
- `influx of` — 描述某个趋势的爆发
- `tout ... while ...` — 描述自相矛盾的信息
- `deter civil from doing` — 描述政策的目的
- `hit out at ... for not doing enough to stem ...` — 描述批评的激烈程度

一篇高分的议论文，就是由这样的"动态描述"构成的。
