## 为什么你的开头总是不够"紧"？

很多学生写议论性文章，开头喜欢铺垫——"With the development of technology…" 绕了半天才进入正题。但高分英语写作的开头有一个规律：**越短、越具体、越直接，越好。**

今天这篇文章来自 FT 关于白俄罗斯事件的深度报道（2021年5月）。记者用一个"因果链"开头，把原因、结果、背景压缩在了一句话里；结尾则用**四个不同角色的原声**来收束，让结尾既有分量又有余味。

## 素材原文

当白俄罗斯政权拦截欧盟客机后，欧洲的回应是怎样的？记者这样开头：

> Two European airlines had to cancel flights to Moscow after Russian authorities failed to approve new flight paths that avoided Belarus's airspace in response to the interception of an EU commercial flight by the Belarusian regime.

而结尾，记者这样安排：

> Willie Walsh, head of the International Air Transport Association, has said it is "disturbing" to see a commercial flight diverted "for clearly bogus reasons".
>
> "I would like to think this does not set a precedent. It's important we build on public and united condemnation… to ensure we don't see a repeat of this behaviour," he told a media conference on Wednesday.
>
> …
>
> "The hijacking of the plane and the detention of the two passengers is completely unacceptable, and we will start discussing implementation of the sectoral and economic sanctions," said Josep Borrell, EU foreign policy chief.
>
> "I think it would hurt Lukashenko very much if we managed something in this area," said Jean Asselborn, Luxembourg's foreign minister.

## 技巧一：因果链开头——把"因为所以"塞进一句话

### 记者做了什么？

先看这个开头句的"骨架"：

> [结果] Two European airlines had to cancel flights to Moscow
> [原因] after Russian authorities failed to approve new flight paths
> [深层原因] that avoided Belarus's airspace
> [根本原因] in response to the interception of an EU commercial flight by the Belarusian regime.

一句话里塞了**四个层次的因果链条**。从航班取消这个最具体的现象出发，一步步往回推，交代了整件事的来龙去脉。

### 为什么这个开头高级？

普通学生的开头写法是："Belarus intercepted a plane. So Russia didn't approve new flights. Then two airlines cancelled flights." 三句话，节奏拖沓。

记者的写法是：**把最吸引眼球的结果放在最前面**（航班取消），然后用 `after...that...in response to...` 的链条往前推，一句话搞定。

### 结构公式

```
[最具体的结果] + after [直接原因] + that [中间环节] + in response to [根本原因]
```

### 学生 vs 升级版

**❌ 学生初版**
Many countries criticized Belarus for what they did. Some airlines even had to cancel flights to Russia.

**✅ 升级版**
Two major airlines had to cancel flights to Moscow after the Russian government refused to approve new routes that avoided Belarus in response to the forced landing of a European passenger plane.

**💡 效果**
升级版把四个信息点压缩在一个句子里，节奏紧凑，信息密度高。阅卷老师一看就知道这位学生能驾驭复杂因果关系。

### 模板

```
[最具体的结果：Something happened] after [直接原因：a condition was not met] that [中间环节：which was meant to do X] in response to [根本原因：a triggering event].
```

**造句练习：**
Three local schools had to suspend in-person classes after the education bureau failed to approve new safety protocols that addressed air quality concerns in response to the recent spike in respiratory illnesses.

## 技巧二：千万不要写一句就停——立即补充细节

### 记者做了什么？

第一句抛出因果链之后，第二句立刻**加深细节**：

> Air France and Austrian Airlines had planned to reroute flights that would normally fly over Belarus after EU countries requested their airlines to avoid the country's airspace after Belarusian president Alexander Lukashenko sent a military jet to divert a Vilnius-bound Ryanair flight carrying an opposition activist.

这里的信息密度再次非常高：具体说了哪两家航空公司、为什么要改航线、白俄罗斯具体做了什么。

### 教学要点

开头不要只写一句抽象陈述就进入正文。**第一句是钩子，第二句是锁扣**——把钩子锁住，让读者读完前两句就"入局"。

## 技巧三：多方声音结尾——用"角色"而不是"观点"收尾

### 记者做了什么？

结尾部分，记者没有自己总结，而是让**四个不同角色的原声**来说话：

| 角色 | 引语 | 功能 |
|------|------|------|
| IATA理事长 Willie Walsh | "disturbing… clearly bogus reasons" | 行业权威定性 |
| 欧盟外交负责人 Borrell | "completely unacceptable" | 官方政治立场 |
| 卢森堡外长 Asselborn | "would hurt Lukashenko very much" | 具体制裁方向 |
| 外交官（背景） | "aiming to pass new measures on June 21" | 时间线/下一步 |

### 为什么这样结尾比"我总结一下"好？

- **客观性**：记者不直接说"这件事很严重"，而是让权威人物说"disturbing"——读者自己得出结论
- **层次感**：从行业批评→政治谴责→具体措施→未来时间表，层层递进
- **可信度**：每个观点都有具体的人名、头衔、出处

### 结构公式

```
[更高层面的定性：行业领袖的核心判断] + [政治层面的回应：官员的官方表态] + [具体方向的暗示：细节性发言] + [下一步的预期：背景信息或时间线]
```

### 学生 vs 升级版

**❌ 学生初版**
In conclusion, this event was very serious and many people criticized it.

**✅ 升级版**
The head of the International Aviation Association called the incident "deeply troubling" and urged the international community to take action. The EU's foreign policy chief said the forced landing was "completely unacceptable" and announced discussions on economic sanctions. Diplomats say a formal proposal is expected by the end of the month.

**💡 效果**
升级版用了三个层面的声音——行业、官方、外交——让结尾像"审判席"一样有分量。注意没有"in conclusion"这个词，但读者自然知道文章要结束了。

## 写在最后

今天学了两招：

1. **开头用因果链**——把最吸引眼球的结果放最前面，用 `after → that → in response to` 链条往前推
2. **结尾用多方声音**——让不同角色的原声替你说结论，层次比"自己总结"深得多

这两招在雅思大作文和高考议论文里特别好用。下次写"政府该不该干预市场"或"科技带来的变化"这类话题，试试看用因果链开头、多方声音结尾——分数会告诉你区别。
