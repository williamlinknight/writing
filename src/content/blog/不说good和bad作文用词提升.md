你的英语作文是不是这样的？
> It is a good book. He is a good student. This is a good idea. I had a good time.

"good"出现了4次，但读完什么感觉？——**空洞、简单、毫无画面感。**

> **📊 厦门灯塔威廉老师批改数据：** 在我批改的 200+ 篇初中英语作文中，一篇150词的作文平均出现 3.8 次"good"和 2.2 次"very"。换掉其中50%的泛泛用词后，文章质量评分平均提升 2-3 分。

## "good"的替代方案

| 场景 | 用什么替代"good" | 例句 |
|------|---------------|------|
| 书/电影 | fascinating, inspiring, thought-provoking | This is a **fascinating** book that I couldn't put down. |
| 学生/员工 | outstanding, excellent, remarkable | He is an **outstanding** student who always goes the extra mile. |
| 机会 | valuable, golden, rare | This is a **valuable** opportunity to learn from experts. |
| 想法 | brilliant, creative, innovative | That's a **brilliant** idea! How did you come up with it? |
| 体验 | enjoyable, memorable, rewarding | I had a **memorable** experience volunteering at the shelter. |
| 食物 | delicious, tasty, mouth-watering | The homemade pasta was absolutely **delicious**. |

## "bad"的替代方案

| 场景 | 用什么替代"bad" | 例句 |
|------|---------------|------|
| 天气/环境 | terrible, unpleasant, harsh | The **harsh** winter made travel nearly impossible. |
| 影响 | negative, harmful, detrimental | This habit has a **harmful** effect on your health. |
| 行为 | inappropriate, unacceptable, disgraceful | Such **inappropriate** behavior will not be tolerated. |
| 心情 | awful, miserable, dreadful | She felt **miserable** after failing the exam. |
| 质量 | poor, inferior, substandard | The product was of **inferior** quality and broke quickly. |

## 更高级的替换："very + adj"的升级

"very good"可以用一个词搞定：

| 不要写 | 替换为 |
|--------|-------|
| very good | excellent / superb / outstanding |
| very bad | terrible / awful / appalling |
| very big | enormous / gigantic / immense |
| very small | tiny / minute / minuscule |
| very fast | rapid / swift / lightning-fast |
| very slow | sluggish / gradual / leisurely |
| very happy | overjoyed / ecstatic / thrilled |
| very sad | devastated / sorrowful / heartbroken |
| very tired | exhausted / drained / worn out |
| very important | crucial / vital / essential |

## 词汇积累的正确方法

很多学生的问题不是"没背过"，而是"背了不会用"。试试这个积累本方法：

**三栏积累本：**

| 泛泛词 | 升级词 | 例句 |
|--------|-------|------|
| good | remarkable | He made **remarkable** progress in just three months. |
| like | adore / admire | I **admire** her determination and perseverance. |
| important | critical | Sleep is **critical** for brain development. |
| get | obtain / acquire | She **acquired** valuable skills through practice. |

**使用规则：** 每天记住3-5组，每组必须写一个自己的原创例句。只有出现在你笔头下的词，才是你真正掌握的词。

## 考场速查

考试的时候不要追求"所有地方都用高级词"。把**关键位置**用上就好：

1. **开头段的第一句**——用1-2个好词抓住老师的注意力
2. **每个段落的中心句**——用精准词汇传达核心观点
3. **结尾段的总结**——画龙点睛之笔

一句话：**关键位置用好词，其他地方保证准确。** 准确率 + 亮点词 = 高分作文。
