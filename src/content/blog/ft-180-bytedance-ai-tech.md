> 厦门灯塔 · 第180期FT精读

上周，**字节跳动**开始在海外卖AI技术了——不是TikTok，而是TikTok背后的"秘密配方"。

这条新闻对咱们学英文有什么用？**大量的商业/科技类高频词汇**，恰好是中高考和雅思作文最缺的那类词。

今天从这篇FT报道（[原文链接](https://www.ft.com)）里挑 **4个** 顶级实用表达，让你下次写"科技改变生活"或者"公司发展"类作文时，立刻拉开和同学的差距。

---

## 原文段落

> ByteDance is selling some of the artificial-intelligence technology that **powers** its viral video app TikTok to websites and apps outside China, as it **broadens** its revenue streams ahead of a long-anticipated initial public offering.
>
> A new division called BytePlus offers businesses the chance to **tap** some of TikTok's secret ingredient: the algorithm that keeps users scrolling by recommending them videos that it thinks they will like.
>
> The international **debut** of BytePlus follows the launch of a similar business-to-business service in China.

> 字节跳动正向中国以外的网站和应用出售其高人气视频应用TikTok背后的一些人工智能技术，其背景是该公司在各方期待已久的首次公开发行之前拓宽营收来源。名为BytePlus的新部门让企业有机会利用TikTok的一部分秘诀。BytePlus在国际市场亮相之前，字节跳动已在中国推出类似的B2B服务。

## 四个值得学的表达

### 1. power — 驱动、提供动力

**原文出处：** "the artificial-intelligence technology that **powers** its viral video app TikTok"

**为什么好：** 大多数中国学生写"驱动"只会用 `drive`（The technology drives the development...）。`power` 作动词，原意是"给……供电"，引申为"为某事物提供核心动力"。用在科技类描述中，语气比 `drive` 更精准、更地道。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The technology drives the app. | The technology **powers** the app. |
| What makes this platform so popular? | What **powers** this platform's popularity? |
| AI is the key factor behind smart education. | AI **powers** the transformation of smart education. |

**例句：** *Machine learning algorithms **power** the recommendation system behind most social media platforms today.*

---

### 2. tap (into) — 利用、获取

**原文出处：** "offers businesses the chance to **tap** some of TikTok's secret ingredient"

**为什么好：** `use` 太普通，`take advantage of` 太长。`tap`（或 `tap into`）表示"接入并利用一个资源"，比 `use` 更生动，比 `utilize` 更自然。在商业议论文中特别好用——"利用市场潜力""利用新兴技术"都用它。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| Companies can use this technology. | Companies can **tap into** this technology. |
| We should take advantage of online resources. | We should **tap into** online resources. |
| The program helps students use their potential. | The program helps students **tap into** their full potential. |

**例句：** *To succeed in the digital age, businesses must learn to **tap into** the power of data analytics.*

---

### 3. broaden — 拓宽、扩大（范围）

**原文出处：** "as it **broadens** its revenue streams ahead of a long-anticipated IPO"

**为什么好：** `broaden` 不是新词，但大多数学生只用它配 `horizons`（broaden one's horizons）。其实它能搭配的东西远不止"视野"——**broaden one's skill set / broaden access to education / broaden the range of services**。比 `widen` 更正式，比 `expand` 更侧重"横向拓展"。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The company wants to increase its income. | The company wants to **broaden** its revenue streams. |
| Students should learn more skills. | Students should **broaden** their skill sets. |
| The government provided more job opportunities. | The government **broadened** access to employment opportunities. |

**例句：** *International exchange programs help students **broaden** their perspectives and develop cross-cultural communication skills.*

---

### 4. debut — 首次亮相、首秀

**原文出处：** "The international **debut** of BytePlus"

**为什么好：** `debut` 源自法语，比 `first appearance` 或 `launch` 更简洁有力。它既可以作名词（the debut of...），也可以作动词（the product debuted last week）。适用于描述新产品发布、新领域开拓、新阶段开始。雅思写作 Task 1 描述产品生命周期时特别好用。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The product was first shown last month. | The product made its **debut** last month. |
| This was the first time the app appeared. | This was the app's international **debut**. |
| The company launched a new service. | The company **debuted** a new service in the Asian market. |

**例句：** *The latest version of the smartphone made its global **debut** at a press conference in Shanghai.*

## 写作建议

这4个词有个共同特点：**它们都是动词（或名词化用法），而且是高频可迁移词**。

- **power** 用在"科技/革新/驱动类"话题
- **tap into** 用在"资源利用/潜力开发"类话题
- **broaden** 用在"教育/视野/技能/渠道"类话题
- **debut** 用在"新产品/新阶段/首次"类话题

下次写作文前，把这4个词写在草稿纸角——写完后检查，看看你原本用的词能不能替换成它们。**替换一个，语言档次就上一个台阶。**
