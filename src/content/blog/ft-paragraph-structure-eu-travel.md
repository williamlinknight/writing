很多学生在写英语作文时，每段只有一个要点，写完就"断气"，下一段的逻辑跳跃得像坐过山车。问题不在词汇量，而在**段落结构意识**——不知道该用怎样的句式组合来支撑一个段落。

今天我们从FT №173《欧盟推出数字旅行证书》学四种实用的段落结构。这篇文章报道欧盟推出"数字绿色证书"便于旅行，虽然已经是2021年的素材，但它的**段落组织方式**至今仍是考场作文的经典参考。

---

## 一、新闻公告式：Topic Sentence + 细节展开

最基础也最实用的段落结构：第一句亮出核心信息，后续句子提供具体细节。

**原文：**

> An EU-wide travel system was launched on Thursday, allowing European citizens to resume travel across the bloc without quarantine in a move designed to help boost tourism over the summer.

> The EU's "digital green certificate" will allow Europeans who have proof of vaccination, a negative Covid-19 test, or proof of recovery from the virus, to travel to other member states. The app-based certificate gives travellers a QR code to allow them to avoid quarantine in other member states.

**结构拆解：**

- **Topic Sentence**：新系统面世，目的明确（帮助恢复旅行）
- **Detail 1**：谁能用、用什么证明
- **Detail 2**：具体怎么用（QR码机制）

**公式：** Topic Sentence → Specific Details → Mechanism/How it works

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
Many countries have a new travel system. It helps people travel. People can use their phone.

**✅ 升级版**
A new EU-wide travel system was launched on Thursday, allowing European citizens to resume cross-border travel without quarantine. The digital certificate, accessible via a mobile app, provides travellers with a QR code that exempts them from self-isolation in other member states.

**💡 效果**
升级版用 `allowing... to resume` 交代目的，用 `accessible via... provides... that exempts` 形成信息递进。一段说了3件事：系统发布 → 凭证方式 → 功能效果，而学生版三句话在重复同一个信息。

## 二、专家引语式：Statement + Quote as Evidence

英文写作中，引用权威人物的直接引语（direct quote）是增强说服力的黄金手段。

**原文：**

> "The European Digital Certificate is a symbol of an open and safe Europe that is opening cautiously putting the protection of the health of our citizens first," said commission president Ursula von der Leyen.

**结构拆解：**

- **前置引语**：直接引语作为证据
- **后置说话人**：动词在前（said）+ 头衔 + 姓名

**公式：** "[Quote about significance/symbolism]," said [Title] [Name].

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
The government said that the new policy is good for people.

**✅ 升级版**
"This new policy represents an important step towards reopening our society while keeping public health as our top priority," said Health Minister Dr. Chen Wei.

**💡 效果**
直接引语比间接转述更有冲击力。加上具体头衔（Health Minister）和名字，更显真实可信。学生版的问题是：说了"好"，但没说好在哪。升级版用了 `represents an important step towards...` 交代了意义方向。

## 三、转折限制式：However + 客观理由 + 专家佐证

记者报道新政策时，几乎必然加上"局限性"段落——这是英文议论文的标配：先肯定，再转折，点出不足。

**原文：**

> However the system will not completely ease intra-EU travel this summer. Nor, economists said, is it likely to give tourism a substantial boost as EU residents can already travel within the bloc's "green list" of countries with low Covid-19 infections.

> "Most adults are not fully vaccinated and the Delta variant is making people and governments more cautious about the health implications of foreign holidays," said Jessica Hinds, economist at Capital Economics. "We expect the tourism sector to remain very subdued this summer."

**结构拆解：**

- **转折词**：However
- **限制陈述**：will not completely / Nor is it likely
- **客观理由**：because... / as...
- **专家引语佐证**：quote from economist

**公式：** However + [limitation statement] + [reason/evidence] + [expert quote confirming the limitation]

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
But there are some problems. The policy is not perfect. Some people don't like it.

**✅ 升级版**
However, the policy will not fully solve the problem. Nor is it expected to deliver immediate results, as many citizens remain hesitant about international travel. "Most people are still cautious about the health implications of long-distance journeys," noted Dr. Wang, a public health expert.

**💡 效果**
转折段的关键不是说"有问题"，而是说明**哪种限制** + **为什么** + **谁说的**。学生版三个空泛句子，升级版一个段落把限制内容、原因、专家佐证全部串联。

## 四、对比式：Some X... By contrast, Y...

并列两个事物，用对比词凸显差异，是议论文中极为常见的手法。

**原文：**

> Some individual EU countries already recognise the UK's NHS app as proof of vaccination, such as Portugal and Greece. By contrast, Spain does not currently recognise the NHS app although it does accept British vaccination documents.

**结构拆解：**

- **第一组**：Some countries + already recognise + examples
- **对比词**：By contrast
- **第二组**：Spain + does not recognise + caveat (although...)

**公式：** Some [category] + [action], such as [examples]. By contrast, [other entity] + [opposite action], although [caveat/additional info].

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
Some countries accept this system. But other countries don't accept it.

**✅ 升级版**
Some Asian countries have already adopted the digital health pass system, such as Singapore and South Korea. By contrast, Japan has not yet implemented it, although it is currently conducting pilot trials in major cities.

**💡 效果**
对比段的信息密度和说服力来自三要素：具体国名、明确的动作、附属条件的交代（although从句）。学生版缺少任何一个都显得苍白。

## 写作建议

段落结构的本质不是"有多少种写法"，而是每种写法服务于什么目的：

| 段落类型 | 适用场景 | 核心技巧 |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 新闻公告式 | 开篇、引入话题 | 第一句亮结论，后续补充细节 |
| 专家引语式 | 强化论点 | 选一句有画面感的原话 |
| 转折限制式 | 辩证分析 | However + 具体限制 + 理由 |
| 对比式 | 论证差异 | 举出具体案例，不要泛泛而谈 |

**练习建议：** 找一个你最近写的议论文段落，用今天学的公式去改写——先写出Topic Sentence，然后用"However"加一段限制，最后找一个具体例子做对比。
