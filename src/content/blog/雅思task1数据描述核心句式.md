雅思Task 1往往是很多人的"送分题"——但前提是你掌握了数据描述的专属语言。

> **📊 厦门灯塔威廉老师批改数据：** 在我批改的50+篇雅思小作文中，超过70%的考生在第1段（overview）没有给出任何具体数据对比。结果：Task Achievement评分直接掉到6分以下。

Task 1不需要你编观点，不需要你分析原因，只需要你把数据"说清楚"。但"说清楚"这3个字，恰恰是很多人过不去的一关。

## 句式一：趋势描述（Trend Language）

**上升表达：**
| 力度 | 动词 | 副词搭配 |
|------|------|---------|
| 急剧 | surge, soar, skyrocket | dramatically, sharply |
| 稳步 | rise, increase, grow | steadily, gradually |
| 缓慢 | edge up, climb | slightly, modestly |

**下降表达：**
| 力度 | 动词 | 副词搭配 |
|------|------|---------|
| 急剧 | plunge, plummet, collapse | dramatically, sharply |
| 稳步 | decline, decrease, fall | steadily, gradually |
| 缓慢 | dip, drop, slide | slightly, marginally |

**持平表达：**
- remain stable / level off / plateau

## 句式二：极值描述（Highest & Lowest）

**最高值：**
- The figure reached a peak of X in [year].
- X hit the highest point at [number].

**最低值：**
- X bottomed out at [number].
- The lowest point was recorded in [year] at [number].

## 句式三：变化幅度（Degree of Change）

**百分比变化：**
- There was a X% increase in...
- X experienced a significant rise of Y%.

**具体数值变化：**
- The number rose from X to Y, an increase of Z.
- This represents a jump of Z compared to the previous year.

## 结构模板

一篇好的Task 1只有4段：

**第1段（改写题目）：** 不要抄原题，换词换结构
- "The line graph illustrates..." → "The chart provides data on..."

**第2段（Overview — 最重要！）：** 总体趋势，不写具体数字
- "Overall, there was a significant upward trend in..."
- "It is notable that while X showed steady growth, Y fluctuated considerably."

**第3段（细节1）：** 选一个主要趋势展开
**第4段（细节2）：** 对比/另一个趋势

## 完整示范

以"某国男女运动参与率"图表为例：

> The line graph illustrates the percentage of men and women who participated in regular physical exercise in Country X between 2000 and 2020.

> Overall, both genders showed an upward trend over the two-decade period, although women's participation rate consistently lagged behind men's.

> The figure for men rose steadily from 35% in 2000 to 45% in 2010, after which it surged dramatically, reaching a peak of 68% in 2020. By contrast, the female participation rate remained relatively stable at around 25% for the first decade, before climbing sharply to 42% by the end of the period.

> Notably, the gender gap narrowed from 10 percentage points in 2000 to 26 points in 2020 — but narrowed proportionally, not absolutely.

## 避坑

❌ **自己编数据** — 在原数据上加1%都不行
❌ **写原因** — "因为政府推出了健身计划"是Task 2的内容
❌ **漏掉overview** — 这是Task Achievement的命门

掌握这三个句式族，Task 1至少6.5起步。
