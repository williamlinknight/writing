> **句型模仿**是提升英文写作最快的方法之一。与其背单词，不如背句子结构——一个优质句型，可以套用在几十个不同的作文题目上。

今天我们从FT一篇关于Facebook进军音频领域的报道中，提取3个**万能句型**，看看它们如何让普通的"公司做产品"叙述，变成有深度的商业分析。

---

## 原文精读

> Facebook is making a push into audio, launching a suite of new features that will allow users to host audio conferences and podcasts, **in a dash to compete with** up-and-coming apps such as Clubhouse.
>
> **The move is** Facebook's latest effort to copy popular products from its rivals, **after it** raced to introduce new videoconferencing features last April **following** the rise of Zoom and Google Hangouts.
>
> **In particular**, the Facebook launch **could hurt** the audio start-up Clubhouse, **which became** the fastest-growing social media app in the world this year **by providing** a platform to discuss topics such as entrepreneurship, politics and the latest news events.

---

## 句型一：竞争行动 + 目的连动

**结构公式：**
> [Subject] **is making a push into** [领域], [doing something], **in a dash to compete with** [竞争对手].

### 为什么好？

学生写"公司做新产品"时，最常见的写法是：
- *Facebook made a new audio feature. They want to compete with Clubhouse.*（两个简单句，平淡）

FT只用一句话就完成了三重信息：**做了什么**（making a push into audio）→ **具体动作**（launching features）→ **竞争目的**（in a dash to compete with）。**in a dash to** 是一个比 "in order to" 更有紧迫感、更生动的表达。

### 考场用法

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| Apple made a new AI tool. They want to compete with Google. | Apple is making a push into AI, launching a new smart assistant, in a dash to compete with Google's Gemini. |
| The school started a new course. They want to attract more students. | The school is making a push into online education, launching live-streamed courses, in a dash to compete with other institutions. |

### 模板 + 例句

> [Company/School/Country] is making a push into [field], [V-ing specific action], in a dash to compete with [competitor].

*例：China is making a push into electric vehicles, building a nationwide charging network, in a dash to compete with established automakers.*

## 句型二：事件链条 + 历史背景

**结构公式：**
> The move is [Subject]'s **latest effort to** [当前目标], **after it** [之前行动] **following** [触发事件].

### 为什么好？

学生写背景时，通常只会用 "because" 或 "first..., then..." 串成时间线，缺乏层次感。

FT用三层结构：
1. **当前事件定位**：the move is the latest effort（不是孤立事件）
2. **历史前置**：after it raced to introduce...（之前做了什么）
3. **触发原因**：following the rise of...（因为什么触发）

一个句型 = 三个时间层次。这在任何议论文中都极其有用——无论写科技、教育还是环境话题。

### 考场用法

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The government made new rules. Because online shopping grew quickly. | The move is the government's latest effort to regulate e-commerce, after it introduced new data privacy laws last year following the rapid rise of online shopping. |
| Our school stopped using paper. Because COVID happened. | The move is our school's latest effort to go digital, after it adopted online learning platforms following the shift to remote education. |

### 模板 + 例句

> The move is [Subject]'s latest effort to [current goal], after it [previous action] following [trigger event].

*例：The move is China's latest effort to reduce carbon emissions, after it expanded solar energy capacity following the Paris Agreement.*

## 句型三：具体影响 + 背景补充

**结构公式：**
> **In particular**, [事件A] **could hurt** [实体B], **which became** [描述] **by** [方式/手段].

### 为什么好？

学生写影响时，经常只有一句干巴巴的 "This will affect many people"——没有具体对象，没有量化，没有背景。

FT这个句型三层递进：
1. **聚焦**：In particular（不泛泛而谈，指定具体影响对象）
2. **划范围**：could hurt Clubhouse（具体受影响方）
3. **补背景**：which became... by providing...（顺便带出这个受影响方的关键信息）

**could** 的使用也很讲究——比 "will" 更谨慎、更学术，体现分析而非断言。

### 考场用法

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| New technology will affect many companies. | In particular, the new AI tool could hurt traditional translation companies, which became popular by providing human-like services at low costs. |
| Online learning changes education. | In particular, live-streaming courses could hurt traditional tutoring schools, which became the go-to choice for parents by offering face-to-face interaction. |

### 模板 + 例句

> In particular, [Event] could hurt [Entity], which became [status/description] by [method/approach].

*例：In particular, the new policy could hurt small farmers, which became the backbone of rural communities by providing affordable local produce.*

## 写作建议：句型模仿的"三步法"

这三个句型，不要一次全部用。**一个一个来**：

1. **选一个你最需要的** — 比如写科技话题，先练"竞争行动"句；写环境话题，先练"事件链条"句
2. **写它10遍** — 套不同的主题（教育、环保、科技、社会），写到不需要思考就能写出来
3. **写新作文时强制使用** — 写完检查：这篇作文里有没有至少用了一个高级句型？

先熟练一个句型，形成"肌肉记忆"，再加第二个。三个都掌握后，你的作文就从"陈述事实"升级到了"深度分析"。
