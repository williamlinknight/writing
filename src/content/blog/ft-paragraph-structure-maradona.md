> 写人物作文时，你是不是也这样写：**"He was born in... He played football... He died..."** 这种流水账式写法，考官一眼就腻了。
>
> 今天我们读一篇FT关于马拉多纳(Diego Maradona)的讣告，学**3个段落结构技巧**，让你的作文告别平铺直叙。

---

## 技巧一：开篇定调（Summary Lead）

好的段落，**第一句话就告诉读者这段的核心**。FT的做法是：用一句话浓缩"人物身份 + 最高成就 + 矛盾张力"。

先看FT原文：

> **English:**
> Diego Maradona, the footballer who led Argentina to victory at the 1986 World Cup, has died at the age of 60, with the sport mourning an iconic player with few peers on the pitch but who struggled with addiction throughout his life.
>
> **中文对照：**
> 曾带领阿根廷队在1986年世界杯夺冠的足球运动员迭戈·马拉多纳去世，享年60岁。足球界悼念一位在球场上几乎没有对手、但终生与毒瘾苦斗的偶像级球员。

**为什么好？**

这个开篇完成了**三个任务**：
1. **定性：** "the footballer who led Argentina to victory" —— 一句话告诉你他是谁、最牛在哪
2. **事实：** "has died at the age of 60" —— 核心新闻事件
3. **张力：** "few peers on the pitch but struggled with addiction" —— 天才与悲剧的反差，激发读者继续读

**学生常见写法 vs FT写法：**

| 学生版本（流水账） | FT升级版（开篇定调） |
|--------|--------|
| Maradona was a famous football player. He was born in Argentina. He died at 60. He was very good at football. | Diego Maradona, the footballer who led Argentina to victory at the 1986 World Cup, has died at the age of 60, with the sport mourning an iconic player with few peers on the pitch but who struggled with addiction throughout his life. |

**模板公式：**

```
[人物姓名], the [人物身份/角色], [核心事件/最高成就], [补充矛盾/转折].
```

**不同主题应用：**

- 写屠呦呦：*Tu Youyou, the scientist who discovered artemisinin and won the Nobel Prize, has been praised as a pioneer who saved millions of lives but who remained humble throughout her career.*
- 写袁隆平：*Yuan Longping, the agronomist who developed the first hybrid rice strains, is remembered as a national hero who fed a billion people but who never stopped working in the fields.*

## 技巧二：引语佐证（Quote as Evidence）

陈述一个观点之后，**用别人的话做证据**，比你自己反复说"他很伟大"有力一百倍。

FT在介绍完马拉多纳的成就后，引用了加里·莱因克尔（英格兰传奇前锋）的话：

> **English:**
> Gary Lineker, the former England striker who played in that game, wrote on Twitter that Maradona was by "some distance the best player of my generation and arguably the greatest of all time. After a blessed but troubled life, hopefully he'll finally find some comfort in the hands of God."
>
> **中文对照：**
> 曾作为英格兰队的前锋参加那场比赛的加里·莱因克尔在Twitter上写道，马拉多纳"是我那一代遥遥领先的最佳球员，也可以说是有史以来最伟大的球员。在经历了幸福但坎坷的一生后，愿他最终能在上帝手中寻得一些安慰。"

**为什么好？**

这段话的段落结构很讲究：
1. **先引出说话人身份** —— "the former England striker who played in that game"。为什么加这句话？让读者知道：**说这话的人当面和马拉多纳交过手**，不是随便哪个路人甲。
2. **引用的内容有层次** —— "best player of my generation"（肯定成就）+ "greatest of all time"（更高评价）+ "blessed but troubled life"（人性化）。这不是客套话，是有价值的评价。
3. **段落功能单一** —— 这一段就做一个事：**用权威人物的话来佐证前面的观点**。它不介绍背景，不展开叙述，不发表评论。

**学生常见写法 vs FT写法：**

| 学生版本（空泛引用） | FT升级版（引语佐证） |
|--------|--------|
| Many people think Maradona is great. He is a very good football player. He played very well in the World Cup. | Gary Lineker, the former England striker who played in that game, wrote on Twitter that Maradona was by "some distance the best player of my generation and arguably the greatest of all time." |

**模板公式：**

```
[发言者姓名], the [发言者身份/相关背景], [动作词（said/wrote/argued）] that [引用的核心观点].
```

**不同主题应用：**

- 写乔布斯：*Tim Cook, the CEO of Apple who worked alongside Steve Jobs for over a decade, said in an interview that Jobs was "the most innovative mind I have ever encountered, with an almost magical ability to see what others couldn't."*
- 写爱因斯坦：*The Nobel committee stated that Einstein's theories had "fundamentally changed humanity's understanding of the universe," calling his work a milestone in scientific history.*

## 技巧三：对比转折（Contrast & Balance）

人物不能只有一面。FT的段落结构最厉害的地方是——**每个"成就段落"之后，都有一个"困境段落"与之平衡**。

看成就段之后紧接的这段：

> **English:**
> Off the pitch, well documented problems with cocaine addiction and a playboy lifestyle led to a deterioration in his private life which eventually engulfed his career. He was sent home from the 1994 World Cup after failing a drugs test, and continued to struggle with health issues and obesity long after retiring as a player in 1997.
>
> **中文对照：**
> 在球场外，得到广泛报道的可卡因毒瘾和花花公子生活方式导致他在私人生活中沉沦，最终严重影响他的职业生涯。在1994年世界杯上，他因药检不合格而被遣送回国，并在1997年退役后继续艰难应对健康问题和肥胖。

**为什么好？**

这一段的关键结构是 **"Off the pitch"** 这个转折标志。它和前面讲成就的段落形成了明确的分界：

| 前面段落（成就） | 本段（困境） |
|---------|--------|
| On the pitch（球场上） | Off the pitch（球场外） |
| "captained Argentina to victory" | "led to a deterioration" |
| "greatest goals in history" | "failed a drugs test" |

这种 **"一方面……另一方面……"** 的结构让文章有立体感。人物不再是完美的神，而是真实的人。

**学生常见写法 vs FT写法：**

| 学生版本（单面人） | FT升级版（对比转折） |
|--------|--------|
| Maradona was a great player. He was also a bad person because he took drugs. | On the pitch, Maradona was an unstoppable genius who led Argentina to World Cup glory. Off the pitch, however, his well-documented addiction to cocaine eventually consumed his career and personal life. |

**模板公式：**

```
[On one hand / 成就角度], [成就描述]. [On the other hand / Off the pitch / However], [困境/另一面描述].
```

**不同主题应用：**

- 写霍金：*In the academic world, Stephen Hawking was a revolutionary physicist whose theories reshaped our understanding of black holes. Off the page, however, he lived with a debilitating disease that gradually took away his ability to move and speak.*
- 写梵高：*In his art, Van Gogh created some of the most vibrant and emotionally powerful paintings in history. In his personal life, however, he struggled with severe mental illness and died in poverty.*

## 总结：三段结构，告别流水账

| 技巧 | 作用 | 一句话说明 | 不做的事 |
|------|------|-----------|---------|
| 开篇定调 | 第一段就交代"谁+成就+矛盾" | 不铺垫背景，不绕弯子 |
| 引语佐证 | 用权威人物的话代替空泛描述 | 不自己反复说"很厉害" |
| 对比转折 | 展示人物的多面性 | 不平铺直叙，不写完美人格 |

**写作自查三问：**
1. ✅ 我的第一段能一句话说清楚"他/她是谁、做了什么、有什么特别之处"吗？
2. ✅ 我有没有用别人的话来佐证我的观点，而不是自己反复说？
3. ✅ 我有没有展示人物的另一面，还是只写了单方面的评价？
