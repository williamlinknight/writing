昨天有学生问我："老师，我写作文开头总是'I think'或者'Nowadays'，结尾就是'All in all'，怎么改？"

这是中国学生最典型的写作问题之一。开头平平无奇，结尾草草收场，整篇文章的评分上限就被锁死了。

其实，开头和结尾是写作中最容易通过模仿提升的部分。今天我从《金融时报》一篇生物医药报道中，拆解4种可以直接套用的开头结尾技巧。

> *原文：FT №5 — Oxford-AstraZeneca vaccine results raise hopes for Covid fight*

---

## 一、FT原文示范

**英文原文：**

> The coronavirus vaccine developed by Oxford university and AstraZeneca has proved effective in late-stage trials, giving another boost to hopes that the pandemic can be defeated.
>
> The successful results follow the positive trials of rival treatments this month from Pfizer/BioNTech and Moderna, which have lifted global stock markets and signalled a possible end to lockdowns that have wreaked havoc on the world economy.
>
> [...]
>
> The jab is priced at about $3 to $4 a dose, supply deals suggest, a fraction of the price of other vaccines. The vaccine can be stored long-term at normal fridge temperature, between 2C and 8C; other treatments require a storage temperature as low as minus 70C.

**中文对照：**

> 牛津大学和阿斯利康合作研发的新冠疫苗在后期试验中被证明有效，进一步提振了人类战胜疫情的希望。
>
> 这一成功结果紧随辉瑞/BioNTech和莫德纳本月发布的积极试验消息——后两者的消息提振了全球股市，标志着有可能结束已经搅乱世界经济的防疫封锁。
>
> [...]
>
> 供应协议显示，该疫苗价格约为每剂3至4美元，仅为其他疫苗价格的一个零头。这款疫苗可在正常冰箱温度（2至8摄氏度）下长期储存；其他疫苗要求的储存温度最低达到零下70摄氏度。

## 二、开头技巧拆解

### 技巧1：成果宣布式开头（Results Announcement）

**结构公式：** `[主体] + [动作/成果] + [意义/影响]`

> "The coronavirus vaccine developed by Oxford university and AstraZeneca **has proved effective** in late-stage trials, **giving another boost to hopes that** the pandemic can be defeated."

这句话的结构是：研究结果（has proved effective）→ 结果的意义（giving another boost to hopes）。不绕弯子，第一句话就告诉读者"什么结果 + 为什么重要"。

**❌ 学生初版**
> I think studying hard is very important for students. It can help us get good grades.

**✅ 升级版**
> The new study method introduced by our school has proved effective in improving test scores, giving another boost to hopes that exam stress can be reduced.

**💡 效果**
"has proved effective" 是陈述经过验证的事实，自带说服力；"giving another boost to hopes that..." 把具体结果升华为更广泛的意义。两个核心表达组合，刚好构成了一个"先宣布结果→再说明意义"的强力开头。

**考场模板：**
> [The new approach/policy/method] introduced by [主体] has proved effective in [具体成果], giving another boost to hopes that [更广泛的意义].

---

### 技巧2：对比铺垫式开头（Comparative Context）

**结构公式：** `[当前结果] + follow(s) [之前的相关事件] + [之前事件的连锁影响]`

> "The successful results **follow** the positive trials of rival treatments this month from Pfizer/BioNTech and Moderna, **which** have lifted global stock markets **and** signalled a possible end to lockdowns **that** have wreaked havoc on the world economy."

这是"背景先行"式的开头。第一句宣布成果后，第二句立即用"follow"把当前事件置于更大的叙事背景中——先有辉瑞和莫德纳的成功，再引到牛津-阿斯利康。接着用三个递进从句：which...and signalled...that...——把"股价上涨→封锁有望结束→封锁已重创经济"三层信息压缩进一句话。

**❌ 学生初版**
> Many people are using smartphones now. Smartphones are very popular. They change our life.

**✅ 升级版**
> The rapid adoption of smartphones follows the expansion of 4G networks across the country, which have connected rural communities and signalled a shift in how people access education and healthcare.

**💡 效果**
"follow" 一词连接前后因果，比第一句就宣布结论更自然。"which...and...that" 的递进结构让一个长句讲清楚三层信息，比拆成三个短句更有文字张力。

**考场模板：**
> [A] follows [related development], which has [immediate effect 1] and [immediate effect 2] that [broader consequence].

## 三、结尾技巧拆解

### 技巧3：比较总结式结尾（Comparative Summary）

**结构公式：** `[A的价格/特点] is [具体数据], a fraction of [B的价格/特点]; [A的优点] while [B的局限]`

> "The jab is priced at about **$3 to $4 a dose**, **a fraction of** the price of other vaccines. The vaccine can be stored long-term at normal fridge temperature, between 2C and 8C; **other treatments require** a storage temperature as low as minus 70C."

结尾用两个对比句，清晰地告诉读者这家疫苗的独特优势（便宜 + 易于储存）。比说"This vaccine is cheap and easy to store"有力得多——因为具体的数字（$3-4 vs other vaccines）和具体的温度对比（2C-8C vs -70C）让结论有据可依。

**❌ 学生初版**
> This method is better than other methods. It is cheaper and easier to use.

**✅ 升级版**
> The new device is priced at about ¥200 per unit, a fraction of the cost of imported alternatives. It can operate using standard batteries; other devices require specialized charging equipment.

**💡 效果**
"a fraction of" 比 "cheaper than" 更精确、更有画面感。"while" 或分号对比结构，把两个事物的差异并列呈现，比"but"更有节奏感。

**考场模板：**
> [A] is priced at about [具体数据], a fraction of [对比对象]. [A] can [优点]; [对比对象] requires/requires [局限].

---

### 技巧4：客观事实收束（Fact-based Closure）

**结构公式：** `[具体事实]；[补充事实]，[对比或转折]`

文章最后一句话：
> "The jab is priced at about $3 to $4 a dose, supply deals suggest, a fraction of the price of other vaccines. The vaccine can be stored long-term at normal fridge temperature, between 2C and 8C; other treatments require a storage temperature as low as minus 70C."

不写"总之"，不喊口号。记者把两个最关键的事实（价格、储存条件）并列摆出来，让读者自己去判断。这是新闻写作的高级收尾技巧——让事实说话。

**❌ 学生初版**
> All in all, I think this is a very good solution. It has many advantages. We should use it.

**✅ 升级版**
> The solution costs about ¥50 per month, half the price of subscription services. It requires only a basic internet connection; competing platforms demand dedicated hardware.

**💡 效果**
没有"All in all"或"In conclusion"的总结套路，而是用并列的事实收束全文。这种结尾在考官看来更成熟、更自信。

**考场模板：**
> [A] costs about [具体数据], [对比数据]. [A] requires [简单条件]; [对比对象] requires [复杂条件].

## 四、针对性练习建议

1. 写一个"成果宣布式"开头——选用最近考试的一道作文题（如环保、科技或教育话题），第一句直接陈述一个研究或改革的结果及其意义
2. 写一个"比较总结式"结尾——用"a fraction of"和分号对比结构，比较两个事物的价格或特性
3. 把下面这个"套路化"结尾改写成"事实收束式"结尾：

> All in all, I think public transport is better than driving cars. It is cheaper and more environmentally friendly. We should take buses and subways more often.
