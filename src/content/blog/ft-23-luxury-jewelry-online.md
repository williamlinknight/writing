你有没有在作文里写过 "compared with"、"face the challenge" 这种表达？语法没错，但阅卷老师每天看几十篇——他需要的是更有画面感、更精准的词。

今天我们从 FT 一篇关于疫情如何推动奢侈品珠宝品牌走向线上的文章里，提取 **4个表达**。它们可以直接放进你的议论文、图表作文、甚至雅思大作文里，让你和同龄人拉开差距。

---

## FT原文

> Online chat rooms and video conferencing **may pale in comparison with** the ceremonious service luxury houses are known for.
>
> Ecommerce used to **account for** 6 to 7 per cent of its watch and jewellery sales.
>
> British fine jeweller Annoushka was **confronted with** the challenge through its business in China.
>
> Many companies plan to **keep** the arrangements **in place** once the pandemic is over.

---

## 1. pale in comparison with — 相形见绌

**原文：** Online chat rooms and video conferencing **may pale in comparison with** the ceremonious service luxury houses are known for.

**中文：** 聊天室和视频会议与奢侈品牌标志性的仪式感服务相比，可能显得黯然失色。

**为什么好：** 学生写对比最爱用 `compare with` 或 `A is not as good as B`，但 `pale in comparison with` 自带一种"颜色变淡"的画面感——像一张褪色的照片。它精准表达"相比之下差得远"，比 `cannot compare with` 更地道，比 `A is inferior to B` 更自然。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| Online learning is not as good as face-to-face classes. | Online learning **pales in comparison with** face-to-face classes. |
| Small shops cannot compare with big supermarkets. | Small shops **pale in comparison with** big supermarkets in terms of variety. |
| My spoken English is not as good as hers. | My spoken English **pales in comparison with** hers. |

**造句练习：** The convenience of fast food **pales in comparison with** the nutritional value of home-cooked meals.

**适用场景：** 比较两种事物、优劣对比、议论文中驳论。任何想表达"差很多"的地方。

---

## 2. account for — 占（比例）

**原文：** Ecommerce used to **account for** 6 to 7 per cent of its watch and jewellery sales.

**中文：** 电子商务此前只占其腕表和珠宝销售额的6%到7%。

**为什么好：** 学生写数据图表作文时常写 `is` 或 `make up`（`make up 30%` 不算错，但偏口语化）。`account for` 是图表描述题的标准高分词——雅思小作文、高考图表作文直接给分。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The service industry is 60% of the economy. | The service industry **accounts for** 60% of the economy. |
| Girls make up 45% of the students. | Girls **account for** 45% of the total students. |
| Reading takes up 40% of my free time. | Reading **accounts for** 40% of my free time. |

**造句练习：** Renewable energy now **accounts for** over 30% of the country's total electricity generation.

**适用场景：** 图表作文、数据说明、任何需要精确描述比例的地方。

---

## 3. be confronted with — 面对（困境/挑战）

**原文：** British fine jeweller Annoushka was **confronted with** the challenge through its business in China.

**中文：** 英国高端珠宝品牌 Annoushka 在其中国业务中面临了这一挑战。

**为什么好：** 学生写"面对问题"常用 `face`。`be confronted with` 带上被动语态，暗含"被迫面对"或"不得不面对"的意味——比 `face` 更多了一层情态。用在议论文中讲述困难时格外有力。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| Many countries face serious environmental problems. | Many countries are **confronted with** serious environmental problems. |
| The company faces financial difficulties. | The company is **confronted with** severe financial difficulties. |
| Young people face the problem of stress. | Young people are **confronted with** growing levels of stress. |

**造句练习：** Schools are **confronted with** the challenge of integrating AI technology into traditional teaching.

**适用场景：** 环境问题、社会挑战、个人困境——任何"不得不面对"的困难。

---

## 4. keep...in place — 保持/继续实施

**原文：** Many companies plan to **keep the arrangements in place** once the pandemic is over.

**中文：** 许多公司计划在疫情结束后继续保持这些安排。

**为什么好：** 学生写"保持"只用 `keep` 或 `maintain`。`keep...in place` 是一种非常地道、有"度"的表达——不是简单地"保持"，而是"原封不动地保留一套体系"。在雅思大作文讨论政府政策、社会制度时特别好用。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The government should keep this policy. | The government should **keep** this policy **in place**. |
| We should maintain the current system. | We should **keep** the current system **in place**. |
| The school decided to keep the new rule. | The school decided to **keep** the new rule **in place** for another year. |

**造句练习：** Many experts argue that working-from-home arrangements should be **kept in place** even after the pandemic.

**适用场景：** 政策讨论、制度延续、习惯维持、任何"保持现状"的描述。

---

## 小结

今天学了4个能用上的表达：

| 主题 | 表达 | 原来你可能写 | 现在试试 |
|------|------|-------------|---------|
| 对比 | **pale in comparison with** | is not as good as | 高级对比 |
| 占比 | **account for** | make up / is | 数据描述 |
| 面对挑战 | **be confronted with** | face | 正式写作 |
| 保持延续 | **keep...in place** | keep / maintain | 政策话题 |

**两步法：** 下次写作文时——
1. 先用自己的话写完
2. 回头看一眼这4个表达，哪里能替换就替换

一个精准的表达，胜过三个平淡的句子。从今天开始，有意识地用起来吧。
