## 一个句子，三种写法

同样一个意思，初中生的三种水平一眼可见：

| 水平 | 例句 | 评分 |
|-----|------|------|
| 基础（12–14分） | I am happy. | 简单句，无亮点 |
| 中等（15–17分） | I am very happy because I got good grades. | 有原因，但结构普通 |
| 高分（18–20分） | I feel extremely delighted not only because of my progress, but also because my efforts finally paid off. | 并列结构+地道词汇+逻辑递进 |

区别在哪？不是词汇量的差距，是**"扩写意识"的差距。**

### 五大核心扩写手法

| 手法 | 简单版 → 升级版 |
|-----|---------------|
| **添加原因** | I like reading. → I enjoy reading because it broadens my horizons. |
| **添加结果** | He studied hard. → He studied so hard that he made great progress. |
| **添加让步** | It was difficult. → Although it was challenging, I never gave up. |
| **添加并列** | She is kind. She is also smart. → She is not only kind but also intelligent. |
| **添加定语** | I have a friend. → I have a friend who always encourages me. |

每种手法都配有：方法讲解 → 模仿练习 → 自主输出 → 参考答案，循环训练直到形成肌肉记忆。
