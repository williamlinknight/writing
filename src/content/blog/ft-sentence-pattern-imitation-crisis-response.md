## 写作的差距在句型，不在词汇量

很多同学背了几千个单词，作文还是只有简单句。为什么？因为**你没有句型库**——你认识每个词，但不知道怎么把它们组装成有层次感的句子。

今天我们从FT一篇关于印度疫情危机的报道中，提取4个**可在任何议论文中复用的高级句型**。这4个句型覆盖了：行动描述、对比手法、数据呈现、最高级论证。

## FT原文

> India is airlifting emergency oxygen tanks as it battles with a catastrophic second wave of Covid-19 infections that Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday said "had shaken the nation".

> Chronic shortages of beds and oxygen have left hospitals in hotspots such as Delhi pleading publicly for relief, while patients die for want of treatment — even while they queue outside hospitals waiting to be seen.

> A total of 190,000 people are reported to have died from Covid-19 in India, though experts believe the true number of cases and deaths is being widely undercounted as sick patients struggle to get tested and fatalities are misreported.

> Ramachandra Guha, a prominent historian of modern India, wrote that the current wave might be "the gravest crisis the nation has faced since Partition".

## 句型一：「as it battles with」动态行动描述

**原文**

> 「India is airlifting emergency oxygen tanks as it battles with a catastrophic second wave of Covid-19 infections...」

**为什么好**

学生写「做某事应对某问题」时，最常用的就是 do something to deal with/solve the problem。这种表达没有错，但不生动。FT用的 as it battles with 不仅说明了「同时发生」，还通过 battle（战斗）这个词暗示了严峻性和紧迫感。整个句子的动态感远强于中文思维的简单因果句式。

**结构公式**

> **[Subject] is [verb+ing] [object] as it battles with [adjective] [problem] that [authority] said "[quote]"**

**考场用法**

**❌ 学生初版**
The city is taking action to solve the pollution problem.

**✅ 升级版**
The city is banning high-emission vehicles as it battles with a worsening air quality crisis that health experts have called "a public health emergency".

**💡 效果**
as it battles with 把「应对」这个动作变得有画面感——不是机械地做某事，而是在「战斗」。这个动词本身就有强烈的说服力。

**跨话题模板**

- **环境话题：** The country is expanding its renewable energy grid as it battles with an unprecedented drought that scientists say is "a clear warning of climate change".
- **科技话题：** The company is introducing stronger encryption as it battles with a growing wave of cyberattacks that industry leaders have described as "the new frontier of warfare".
- **教育话题：** Schools are adopting hybrid learning models as they battle with persistent educational inequality that the minister called "the greatest challenge of our generation".

## 句型二：「for want of」对比绝望

**原文**

> 「...patients die for want of treatment — even while they queue outside hospitals waiting to be seen.」

**为什么好**

学生在写对比时最常见的是 but 或 however 开头的句子。FT这里用了极为精悍的 for want of（因缺乏）+ 破折号对比 —— 前半句说「缺乏治疗而死」，后半句用 even while 引出更残酷的细节「排队时死去」。这种先概括再展开细节的对比写法，比简单的 but 有力十倍。

**结构公式**

> **[Subject] [verb] for want of [resource] — [contrast marker] [dramatic detail]**

**考场用法**

**❌ 学生初版**
Many students cannot improve their writing, but they spend a lot of time practicing.

**✅ 升级版**
Many students struggle to improve their writing for want of meaningful feedback — even while they spend hours completing practice exercises without a clear direction.

**💡 效果**
for want of 比 because they don't have 正式得多，而破折号后的对比细节让读者感受到「努力却无效」的遗憾。这个句式在中考/高考/雅思的议论文中都能制造很强的情感冲击。

**跨话题模板**

- **科技话题：** Many small businesses fail for want of digital skills — even while the country pours billions into technology advancement.
- **环境话题：** Rare species continue to disappear for want of effective protection — even while governments sign ambitious conservation agreements year after year.
- **社会话题：** Children in rural areas drop out of school for want of basic facilities — even while urban schools enjoy state-of-the-art equipment.

## 句型三：「统计数据 + 专家质疑」嵌套结构

**原文**

> 「A total of 190,000 people are reported to have died from Covid-19 in India, though experts believe the true number of cases and deaths is being widely undercounted as sick patients struggle to get tested and fatalities are misreported.」

**为什么好**

学术写作中数据呈现后的一个关键动作是：**对数据进行解读和质疑**。学生通常只会说完数据就结束了（190,000 people died），没有批判性思维。FT这里的结构是：官方数据 + though 专家质疑 + as 原因解释，三个层次环环相扣，这是典型的高分议论文段落结构。

**结构公式**

> **A total of [number] [noun] are reported to have [verb+ed], though experts believe [contrary claim] as [explanation 1] and [explanation 2]**

**考场用法**

**❌ 学生初版**
About 70% of students use smartphones. This is a very high number.

**✅ 升级版**
A total of 70% of high school students are reported to own a smartphone, though educators believe the true impact on academic performance is being underestimated as students struggle to concentrate in class and their sleep quality declines.

**💡 效果**
「数据+转折+原因」的三层结构展示了学术写作中最核心的能力——**批判性思维能力**。不只是一个冷冰冰的数据，而是一段有观点的分析。

**跨话题模板**

- **环境话题：** A total of 8 million tons of plastic are reported to enter the ocean every year, though researchers believe the actual figure is far higher as illegal dumping goes unreported and waste tracking systems remain incomplete.
- **科技话题：** A total of 60% of companies are reported to have adopted AI tools, though experts warn the real rate of meaningful adoption is significantly lower as employees struggle to integrate them and training programs lag behind.

## 句型四：「最高级 + since」参照系论证

**原文**

> 「Ramachandra Guha, a prominent historian of modern India, wrote that the current wave might be 'the gravest crisis the nation has faced since Partition'」

**为什么好**

学生写「非常重要」只会用 very important 或 crucial。FT这里展示了最高级论证的标准范式：**引入权威人士 → 用最高级描述严重程度 → since 给出历史参照系**。这个结构让读者立刻理解问题的严重性，因为它和读者已知的历史事件建立了联系。

**结构公式**

> **[Authority figure], [appositive describing their expertise], wrote/said that [subject] might be "the [superlative] crisis [entity] has faced since [historical reference]"**

**考场用法**

**❌ 学生初版**
Climate change is a very serious problem.

**✅ 升级版**
Dr Chen, a leading scientist at the National Academy, warned that the current rate of ice melt might be "the most alarming environmental shift the Arctic has faced since the last ice age".

**💡 效果**
引入权威人物+最高级+since参照系，三个要素缺一不可。这种论证方式让读者无法忽视问题的严重性，因为它把你正在讨论的事件放到了历史维度中去定位。

**跨话题模板**

- **科技话题：** Professor Liu, a renowned cybersecurity expert, warned that the recent data breach might be "the most devastating cyberattack the industry has faced since the 2017 ransomware outbreak".
- **教育话题：** The principal, a 30-year veteran of public education, wrote that the funding cut might be "the most severe challenge our school has faced since its founding in 1965".

## 写作建议

今天学的4个句型有一个共同点：**它们都不靠生僻词，全靠在语法结构上的巧思。** 一个不算高级的动词（struggle, face, battle）搭配对的句型，效果远超一个没人认识的GRE词汇。

建议你分两步练习：
1. **先抄写句型公式**，每个句型抄5遍，直到能不看笔记写出结构
2. **再改造**——用每个句型写一个关于你熟悉的话题的句子（环保、科技、教育），而不是原文章的话题，这样训练的是迁移能力

句型学得好，作文不用愁。坚持每天模仿一个FT句型，一个月后你的句式多样性会翻倍。
