读《金融时报》学英文写作：让文章更流畅的4个同位语与插入语技巧

很多学生写英语句子时，有一个共同的毛病：**信息一个一个往外蹦，像报菜名一样直白**。写一句话就是一个主谓宾，想加信息就只能再起一句话。结果是文章又长又碎，读起来像小学生说话。

而真正地道的英文写作，会用**同位语(Appositive)**和**插入语(Parenthetical)**把额外信息"塞"进句子中间，让信息密度翻倍，句子节奏自然流畅。

今天我们拿FT一篇关于Apple在欧洲被集体诉讼的报道，看看母语写作者怎样用这4种技巧。

## 技巧一：同位语解释身份（Appositive for Titles & Roles）

### FT原文

> **Els Bruggeman, head of policy and enforcement at Euroconsumers**, said "Apple pushed updates to mask problems with the battery"

> **Bart Volders, partner at Arcas Law**, said "the individual filings could lead to a 'snowball effect' across Europe"

### 为什么好

中文思维写"某人说了某话"，习惯用两个句子：*Els Bruggeman is the head of policy and enforcement. She said...* 读起来像在念简历。而FT用一个逗号+同位语，**一句话完成身份介绍+引语**，信息密度翻倍，节奏不拖沓。

### 考场对照

| 学生写法 | FT升级写法 |
|---------|-----------|
| *Els Bruggeman is the head of policy and enforcement. She said that the company pushed updates to hide problems.* | **Els Bruggeman, head of policy and enforcement at Euroconsumers, said that the company pushed updates to hide problems.** |
| *Professor Wang is an expert in climate change. He argued that carbon emissions must be reduced.* | **Professor Wang, an expert in climate change, argued that carbon emissions must be reduced.** |

### 模板

> **[Person's Name], [Position / Role / Description], said / argued / noted / warned that [quote / claim]**

### 例句

- **Dr. Li, a leading researcher in renewable energy, warned that the current transition pace is still too slow.**
- **Mr. Chen, the head of admissions at Peking University, noted that holistic evaluation matters more than test scores.**

## 技巧二：破折号插入补充信息（Dash Parenthetical）

### FT原文

> Italy's competition authority fined Apple €10m **— its maximum penalty —** after determining that software updates caused "serious malfunctions"

### 为什么好

学生写这句话会写成：*Italy fined Apple €10m. This was the maximum penalty.* 两个句子，读起来断断续续。而FT用两个破折号把**补充信息（这是最高罚款）**塞进句子中间，主句的动词节奏没被打断，额外信息同时到位。

**关键区别：** 破折号插入的信息是**对前面那个名词的解释/强调**，不是新的论点。

### 考场对照

| 学生写法 | FT升级写法 |
|---------|-----------|
| *The government fined the company 5 million. This was the maximum penalty allowed by law.* | **The government fined the company 5 million — the maximum penalty allowed by law — for violating environmental regulations.** |
| *The school introduced a new policy. This was a controversial move that divided parents.* | **The school introduced a new policy — a controversial move that divided parents — requiring all students to wear uniforms.** |

### 模板

> **[Subject] [Verb] [Object] — [explanation or emphasis about object] — [additional context]**

### 例句

- **The university admitted 500 students — the largest cohort in its history — despite budget constraints.**
- **The city imposed a plastic bag ban — a measure supported by 80% of residents — effective from next month.**

## 技巧三：现在分词插入伴随状态（Participial Parenthetical）

### FT原文

> Apple agreed to the US settlement in March, **admitting no wrongdoing but saying it wanted to avoid protracted litigation.**

### 为什么好

学生写这个信息需要两句：*Apple agreed to the settlement in March. It admitted no wrongdoing and said it wanted to avoid litigation.* 句子很平，像在写清单。

FT用一个逗号加**现在分词短语**，把Apple的态度（"不承认过错，但想避免诉讼"）作为主句的伴随信息插入。主句干了什么 + 做这件事时是什么态度，一句话搞定。

### 考场对照

| 学生写法 | FT升级写法 |
|---------|-----------|
| *The company agreed to compensate customers. It also apologized for the delay.* | **The company agreed to compensate customers, apologizing for the delay while emphasizing it was not at fault.** |
| *He accepted the job offer. He also requested a flexible work schedule.* | **He accepted the job offer, requesting a flexible work schedule as a condition.** |

### 模板

> **[Main Clause], [V-ing phrase describing the manner / attitude / additional action]**

### 例句

- **The government launched a new environmental policy, aiming to reduce carbon emissions by 50% by 2035.**
- **She declined the scholarship, citing personal reasons and the need to stay close to her family.**

## 技巧四：逗号分隔的时间/附加信息插入（Comma-Separated Appositive）

### FT原文

> In a letter to Euroconsumers from its lawyers at Gibson Dunn & Crutcher, **dated August 6**, Apple said it would "never…degrade the user experience"

### 为什么好

"dated August 6"是对前面那封信的时间补充说明。学生可能会写：*A letter was sent on August 6. In the letter, Apple said...* 又是两个句子。

FT用逗号分隔的插入语**把日期塞进已经有介词短语开头的句子**，开头就是一大串信息（什么人给谁写的信+日期），然后才进入主句。这就是母语者的信息密度。

### 考场对照

| 学生写法 | FT升级写法 |
|---------|-----------|
| *A report was published on March 15. It showed that pollution levels had dropped significantly.* | **In a report published by the Ministry of Environment, dated March 15, researchers found that pollution levels had dropped significantly.** |
| *A study was conducted in 2023. It concluded that regular reading improves writing ability.* | **In a study conducted by Beijing Normal University, published in 2023, researchers concluded that regular reading improves writing ability.** |

### 模板

> **[Prepositional Phrase], [additional modifier separated by comma], [Main Clause]**

### 例句

- **In an email sent to all employees, dated June 1, the CEO announced a new remote work policy.**
- **In a survey conducted among 2,000 students, released last week, more than 70% reported feeling stressed about exams.**

## 写作建议：如何练习同位语与插入语

1. **先找再写。** 下次批改自己的作文时，用荧光笔标出所有可以"合并"的两个相邻句子——有没有一个句子可以变成同位语插进另一个里面？

2. **从一个技巧开始。** 别一下子全用。先掌握"同位语解释身份"（技巧一），写到变成肌肉记忆。

3. **注意标点。** 插入语的标点（逗号/破折号/括号）必须成对出现。很多学生只加前半部分不加后半部分——写完后检查：每个插入语前后是否都有标点？

4. **不要过度使用。** 一段话里用1-2个插入语就够了。整个段落都是破折号和逗号，反而让句子读起来喘不过气。
