改了这么多篇作文，有一个问题几乎每个学生都有：**只要想描述一个名词，就上完整定语从句。**

> *"Maradona, who was born in the Lanús district of Buenos Aires, was one of the greatest footballers who have ever played the game."*

语法没错。但「who was」「who have」让句子节奏拖沓。而《金融时报》的讣告告诉我们：很多定语从句可以**省掉who/which + be，直接上分词**。

---

## FT原文节选

本文选自FT 2020年11月26日的马拉多纳讣告。一篇优秀的人物传记——描写、评价、追忆，各种定语从句密集出现：

> Diego Maradona, the footballer who led Argentina to victory at the 1986 World Cup, has died at the age of 60, with the sport mourning an iconic player **with few peers on the pitch but who struggled with addiction** throughout his life.

> Maradona, **who had been born into poverty in the Lanús district of Buenos Aires**, also captained his country at the 1990 World Cup.

> **Born into poverty** in the Lanús district of Buenos Aires, Maradona rose to become one of the greatest footballers in history.

> **Off the pitch**, well documented problems with cocaine addiction and a playboy lifestyle led to a deterioration in his private life **which eventually engulfed his career**.

## 4种定语从句简化技巧

### 技巧一：Born → 过去分词前置（最简单，最高频）

**原文：**
> **Born into poverty** in the Lanús district of Buenos Aires, Maradona rose to become one of the greatest footballers in history.

FT并没有说 "Who was born into poverty..."，而是直接用 **Born into poverty** 开场。过去分词短语直接放在句首，交代背景信息。

**❌ 学生初版**
> Maradona, **who was born** into poverty in the Lanús district, was a great footballer.

**✅ 升级版**
> **Born into poverty** in the Lanús district, Maradona rose to become one of the greatest footballers in history.

**💡 效果**
- 删掉 "who was" → 信息密度翻倍
- 开头直接交代背景 → 比放在主谓之间更流畅
- 句子重心落在 rose to become（"上升为"），比 was 更有动感

**公式：** `Born/Known/Located → [具体信息], [主语 + 动作动词]`

**例句：** Known for his innovative teaching methods, Professor Li has inspired thousands of students.

---

### 技巧二：过去分词后置（取代 which was / that was）

**原文中类似的模式：**

FT在描述马拉多纳赛场以外的故事时，不会说 "the cocaine addiction which was well documented" 或 "the lifestyle which was described as playboy"——而是直接把形容词/名词短语接在后面。

**❌ 学生初版**
> He struggled with drug addiction **which was well documented** by the media throughout his career.

**✅ 升级版**
> He struggled with **well documented** drug addiction throughout his career.

**💡 效果**
- 删掉 "which was" → 词数减半
- "well documented" 前置 → 名词短语更紧凑
- 信息密度瞬间拉满

**公式：** `[名词] [V-ed短语]` → 取代 `[名词] + [which/that + be + V-ed]`

**例句：** The data **collected from 500 students** shows a significant improvement in writing scores.

---

### 技巧三：定语从句做同位语（with + 名词 + 修饰短语）

**原文：**
> with the sport mourning an iconic player **with few peers on the pitch but who struggled with addiction** throughout his life.

这里 FT 没有用 "who had few peers" 的完整从句，而是用 **with few peers**（介词短语）来修饰 "an iconic player"，然后转折用了 **but who struggled**——用并列结构把相反的信息放在同一个修饰语里。

**❌ 学生初版**
> He was a great player, **and he had very few competitors who could match his skills. However, he struggled with addiction.**

**✅ 升级版**
> He was an iconic player **with few peers on the pitch but who struggled with addiction** throughout his life.

**💡 效果**
- "with few peers" 取代整句描写 → 极其简洁
- "but who struggled" 在同一修饰结构中完成转折 → 对比强烈
- 一个修饰语包住了整个矛盾的人生——文字密度极高

**公式：** `[名词] with [特征A] but who [特征B]`

**例句：** She is a student **with remarkable talent but who struggles with time management**.

---

### 技巧四：非限定性定语从句的 who 简化（上下文已知的信息直接用名词短语）

**原文第一句：**
> Diego Maradona, **the footballer who led Argentina to victory at the 1986 World Cup**, has died at the age of 60.

这里注意：主语 "Diego Maradona" 后面跟的不是一个完整的 "who was the footballer who..."，而是直接用了 **名词短语做同位语**：

- 主句：Diego Maradona...has died at the age of 60
- 同位语：**the footballer who led Argentina to victory**（不是"who was the footballer"）

去掉 "who was"，直接把身份名词放在名字后面。

**❌ 学生初版**
> Diego Maradona, **who was a footballer and who led Argentina to the 1986 World Cup victory**, died at age 60.

**✅ 升级版**
> Diego Maradona, **the footballer who led Argentina to victory at the 1986 World Cup**, has died at the age of 60.

**💡 效果**
- 去掉 "who was a" → 同位语直接定义身份
- 删一个完整从句 → 主语到动词的距离缩短一半
- 先给核心身份（the footballer），再补充具体事迹（who led...）

**公式：** `[人名], [the + 身份名词 + 定语从句], [动词 + 主要信息]`

**例句：** Elon Musk, **the entrepreneur who founded Tesla and SpaceX**, announced a new AI startup.

---

## 写法要点总结

写人物介绍时，记住三个"能省则省"：

1. **能前置就前置**：Born in... / Known for... → 直接放在句首，别让 who was 拖节奏
2. **能用过去分词别用定语从句**：a player **born into poverty** ✓ → a player **who was born into poverty** ✗
3. **能用同位语别用完整从句**：Maradona, **the footballer who**... ✓ → Maradona, **who was a footballer who**... ✗

给学生的建议：写完一段后，专门扫描所有 **who is/was, which is/was, that is/was**——试着删掉 "is/was"，看看句子是否依然成立。大部分情况可以。
