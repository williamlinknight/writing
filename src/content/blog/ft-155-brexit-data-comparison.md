英语图表作文和议论文中，**纯数据不如有对比的数据**。

"英国服务出口损失1130亿英镑"是一个事实；但**"比未脱欧场景少了1130亿英镑"**就是一个有说服力的论点。差距就在于——**对比结构**。

今天我们用FT一篇关于英国脱欧冲击服务出口的深度分析，学习**5个数据对比与因果推断的万能句型**。这篇文章的精髓不在于数字本身，而在于作者如何用语言制造"如果没有X，会怎样"的对比空间。

---

## 句型一：使用强势动词表达影响 — "X shrank Y by [数量] over [时间]"

**原文段落：**
> **Brexit shrank UK services exports by more than £110bn over a four-year period**, new research shows, highlighting the far-reaching trade implications of Britain's decision to break away from the EU.

> 最新研究显示，英国退欧使英国服务出口在四年期间缩水逾1100亿英镑，这突显了英国退出欧盟这一决定的深远贸易影响。

**为什么好：** 这个开篇句的效率极高。按理说，这句话需要包含：什么原因（Brexit）、什么后果（exports shrink）、多大程度（£110bn）、多长时间（4 years）、在哪方面（trade implications）。普通学生会写成：

> Because of Brexit, UK services exports decreased a lot over four years.

但FT用了 **shrank…by…over…** 这样一个紧凑结构——**shrank**（缩水）是比 decreased / went down 更有力的动词，**by** 直接跟影响幅度，**over** 跟时间跨度。然后把"这个数据意味着什么"插入到 **highlighting** 这个分词短语中，一句话就把"发生了什么"和"意味着什么"全说清楚了。

**学生写法 vs 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The policy caused many problems. | The new regulation **shrank corporate profits by more than 20 per cent over the past two years**, highlighting the far-reaching consequences of government intervention. |
| My grades went down after the new schedule. | The new timetable **reduced my study efficiency by nearly 30 per cent over the semester**, revealing the hidden cost of poor time management. |
| Pollution in the city became worse. | Industrial emissions **raised air pollution levels by 40 per cent over a five-year period**, underscoring the urgent need for environmental regulation. |

**模板：**
> [原因] **[强势动词 + ed] [受影响主体] by [数量] over [时间/时期]** , [分词短语]揭示/突显[深层意义]

**常用强势动词：** shrank (缩水), boosted (拉动), slashed (削减), drove up (推高), cut (减少), expanded (扩大), reduced (降低)

## 句型二：虚拟对比 — "[事实] would have been [程度] had [条件句]"

**原文段落：**
> Experts at Aston University in Birmingham found that UK services exports from 2016 to 2019 were cumulatively £113bn **lower than they would have been had the UK not voted to quit the EU** in June 2016.

> 伯明翰阿斯顿大学的专家们发现，相比英国在2016年6月未曾投票决定退出欧盟的情形，英国服务出口在2016年至2019年期间累计低了1130亿英镑。

**为什么好：** 这是全文最核心的句型——**虚拟对比（counterfactual comparison）**。结构是：A is X amount **lower than it would have been had** [反事实条件]。正常语序是 "if the UK had not voted"，但这里省略了 **if** 并把 **had** 提前（倒装虚拟语气），制造了一个非常优雅的学术表达。这个句型在经济学和社科写作中极其常见。

**学生写法 vs 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| If the company hadn't changed its strategy, its profits would be higher. | The company's profits this quarter were **45 per cent lower than they would have been had management not shifted its marketing focus**. |
| Without the scholarship, I couldn't afford this school. | My total education costs were **roughly 60 per cent lower than they would have been had I not received the full scholarship**. |
| If there was no traffic jam, I would have arrived earlier. | My arrival time was **nearly two hours later than it would have been had the traffic not come to a standstill**. |

**模板：**
> [主语] [系动词/动词] [数量差距] **lower/higher/more/less than [it/they] would have been had** [反事实条件]

**注意：** 后面的 **had** 相当于 **if + had** 的倒装，所以不能重复写 if。

## 句型三：方法论描述 — "calculated X by projecting Y and compared with Z"

**原文段落：**
> **The researchers calculated the figure by projecting** how industries from IT and finance to business services would have grown if they had continued on their previous paths, **and compared that with** how they had actually progressed since the vote for Brexit.

> 研究人员计算得出这一数字所用的方法是，预测各个行业沿着各自之前的增长路径将会增至多大规模，然后将其与各行业自退欧公投以来的实际发展进行比较。

**为什么好：** 议论文中引用研究数据时，光说"研究发现XXX"不够有说服力。你要告诉读者：**这个数据是怎么算出来的。** Calculated A **by** doing B, **and compared** the result **with** C——这个结构清晰交代了研究方法：预测（projection）vs 实际（actual）。不是凭空得出数字，是用"反事实推演"的方法算出来的。

**学生写法 vs 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The study found that traffic increased. | **The researchers calculated the increase by projecting** pre-pandemic traffic growth rates **and compared that with** actual traffic data from 2020 to 2024. |
| My essay got a better score. | **I calculated my overall improvement by projecting** my previous monthly scores **and compared that with** my actual results after three months of practice. |
| The report said pollution decreased. | **The team calculated the reduction by projecting** emissions based on 2019 levels **and compared that with** actual measurements taken after the new policy. |

**模板：**
> **The researchers/scientists/analysts calculated** [指标] **by projecting** [假设场景] **and compared that with** [实际发生的场景]

## 句型四：对比数据并列 — "[主语] ran an [金额] surplus in [领域A], against a deficit of [金额] in [领域B]"

**原文段落：**
> In 2019 **the UK ran an £18bn surplus in services trade with the EU, against a deficit of £97bn in goods.**

> 2019年，英国对欧盟的服务贸易顺差为180亿英镑，而商品贸易逆差为970亿英镑。

**为什么好：** 这是**不对称对比**的经典写法。不是简单说 "the UK had a surplus in services and a deficit in goods"，而是用 **against** 这一个词制造出"顺差 vs 逆差"的对立感。**Against** 在这里相当于"相比之下，另一边是"——一个字就完成了对比的连接。

**学生写法 vs 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The UK does better in services than in goods. | In 2023, **the company ran a $5m profit in its domestic market, against a loss of $12m in its overseas operations.** |
| Boys like sports more than girls. | **The survey recorded a 75 per cent participation rate among boys in outdoor activities, against a rate of only 38 per cent among girls.** |
| One city has more tourists. | **Beijing attracted 95 million domestic tourists in 2023, against 32 million inbound visitors.** |

**模板：**
> In [时间]，[主体] **ran/recorded/posted a [金额] surplus/profit/gain in** [领域A]，**against a [金额] deficit/loss/drop in** [领域B]

## 句型五：引语增强论点 — "X said Y had fallen by [程度], citing [来源]"

**原文段落：**
> **He said the data showed the UK's trade with the EU had fallen by a fifth** because of Brexit, citing his own research and figures from the Office for National Statistics, the London School of Economics and the Tony Blair Institute for Global Change.

> 他说，数据显示，由于英国退欧，英国与欧盟的贸易已经下降了五分之一。他引用了他自己的研究以及英国国家统计局、伦敦政治经济学院和托尼•布莱尔全球变革研究所的数据。

**为什么好：** 这一段和句型一的结构有点像，但增加了一个关键要素：**信源列举（citing…）**。不是空口说"数据显示下降了"，而是列举了四个信源：自己的研究 + ONS + LSE + TBI。学生写作中的常见问题就是只给结论不给证据来源；而写作的"可信度"恰恰来自于你能否给出**可见的、可追踪的信源支撑**。另外 **fallen by a fifth** 的分数表达也值得学习——不写20%而写 a fifth，更书面更自然。

**学生写法 vs 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| Some experts say the number of tourists went down. | An economic analyst **said the data showed tourist arrivals had fallen by a quarter** since the new visa policy, citing government statistics and industry reports. |
| A report said pollution is getting worse. | **The report concluded that air quality had deteriorated by nearly a third** over the decade, citing WHO data and local monitoring stations. |
| My teacher said my writing improved. | **My teacher said my essay scores had risen by nearly 40 per cent** over three months, citing my diagnostic test results, weekly assignments, and mock exam performance. |

**模板：**
> [某人] **said the data showed** [主体] **had** [动词 + ed] **by** [比例/数量]，**citing** [信源1], [信源2] **and** [信源3].

## 篇章结构：因果推断的"四步走"

这篇FT文章的结构也值得用在议论文中：

1. **结论先行**（第一段）—— Brexit使出口缩水£110bn
2. **方法论说明**（第二、三段）—— 这个数字怎么算出来的？反事实推演
3. **分点佐证**（第四至第八段）—— 引用学者 + 具体行业 + 跨国数据
4. **反驳与补充**（第九段）—— 爱尔兰经济学家不同意，给出其他解释

注意第三步的巧妙之处：在引用学者观点后，作者马上提供了**具体行业数据**（金融服务业受影响最大）和**跨国对比**（爱尔兰获益），让"脱欧有害"的论点从学者的一句话变成了多角度证据链。

## 今日练习

把下面这段**学生写法**改写成**FT风格**：

> 学生写法：
> If there was no new policy, more people would go to university. A study found the number of university students is 15% lower because of the policy. The researchers looked at data before and after the policy.

> FT风格参考：
> The new admissions policy **reduced the number of university entrants by approximately 15 per cent over three years**, raising concerns about access to higher education. Education researchers at Peking University found that total enrolments were **significantly lower than they would have been had the policy not been implemented** in 2024. **The team calculated the impact by projecting** enrolment growth based on pre-policy trends from 2018 to 2023, **and compared that with** actual enrolment data from 2024 to 2026. One senior analyst said the data showed undergraduate enrolment **had fallen by roughly one-sixth**, citing ministry of education statistics, university admission reports, and independent research by the China Education Association.

---

数据对比不是冷冰冰的数字游戏——它是**语言上的反事实推演**。写好一个"如果没有X，就会怎样"的句子，比堆砌十个数字更能说服你的读者。
