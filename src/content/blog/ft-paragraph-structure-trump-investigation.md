## 资讯类段落 vs 叙事类段落

上一篇我们学了叙事为主的段落结构（开篇→背景→引语→分析→对峙）。但英文写作还有一类更常见的模式：**资讯类段落**。这类段落的目的是传递信息、说明情况、呈现多方立场——不是讲故事，而是做报告。

今天用FT关于纽约州总检察长调查特朗普集团的报道，学习五种资讯类段落。

## 素材来源

FT双语文章 №146 — 纽约州总检察长詹乐霞宣布对特朗普集团的调查从民事转向刑事。全文仅537词，但段落层次极其清晰。

## 段落结构拆解

### 结构一：升级开篇段——"变化在前，解释在后"

资讯类文章的开篇通常不是"从头说起"，而是**把最新变化放在最前面**，然后用解释性的句子补充背景。

> The legal threats to Donald Trump and his business empire have escalated after the New York attorney-general indicated that a long-running investigation of those entities was leading to possible criminal — and not just civil — misconduct.

**结构公式：** [核心变化/升级/转折] + [after/because/following + 触发事件]

**为什么会这么写？**

英文读者阅读习惯是"结果优先"——先知道发生了什么变化，再看变化的原因。这个结构与中文习惯正好相反（中文喜欢"先因后果"）。在雅思/高考议论文中，用这种句式开篇能让考官立刻抓住你的论点核心。

**❌ 学生初版**
The New York attorney-general was investigating Trump. The investigation became criminal. The legal threats to Trump increased.

**✅ 升级版**
The legal threats to Trump and his business empire have escalated after the attorney-general indicated that a long-running investigation was leading to possible criminal misconduct.

**💡 效果**
升级版一个句子完成了"最新变化（威胁升级）→ 触发事件（调查转向刑事）"的完整逻辑链，信息密度极高，节奏紧凑。

---

### 结构二：并列信息段——"一方面 / 另一方面"的平行结构

当事情涉及两个平行的执行机构或两条线索时，好的作者不会把两个信息混在一起写，而是用**清晰的平行结构**并列呈现。

> The investigation, being led by Letitia James, the state's attorney-general, is part of a two-pronged legal pursuit of Trump and his business. The other is being carried out by the office of Cyrus Vance, the Manhattan district attorney, which more commonly focuses on criminal as opposed to civil matters.

**结构公式：** [A is...] + [The other is...]（用定冠词the + other建立平行关系）

**为什么好？**

学生常见的错误是把两个不同的信息用and连接成一个杂乱的长句。FT的做法是：第一句介绍"双管齐下"这个大概念（two-pronged），第二句分别讲"第一支"和"第二支"。



**❌ 学生初版**
There were two investigations. One was by Letitia James and one was by Cyrus Vance. They were both looking at Trump.

**✅ 升级版**
The investigation is part of a two-pronged legal pursuit of Trump and his business. One is led by Letitia James, the state's attorney-general. The other is being carried out by the office of Cyrus Vance, the Manhattan district attorney.

**💡 效果**
升级版先用two-pronged给出"总分结构"的预览，再用One / The other平行推进，每个机构的身份(appositive)直接嵌入在句子中，不另起一句。

---

### 结构三：回应对峙段——"声明 + 不回应 + 反驳"的三层结构

资讯类文章经常需要呈现两方的立场。FT的经典模式是：引用A方声明 → 记录B方不回应 → 记录B方一贯立场。

> A spokesperson for James said: "We have informed the Trump Organization that our investigation into the organization is no longer purely civil in nature. We are now actively investigating the Trump Organization in a criminal capacity along with the Manhattan DA."
>
> The Trump Organization did not immediately respond to a request for comment. The former president has repeatedly dismissed the investigations as "witch-hunts" carried out by Democratic foes.

**结构公式：** [A方声明（直接引语）] + [B方没有回应（did not immediately respond）] + [B方一贯立场（has repeatedly dismissed）]

**为什么会这么写？**

这个三层结构的精妙之处在于"无声胜有声"：写B方"没有回应"本身就是一个信息——暗示对方可能不想正面回答。然后再补充"但B方一贯说什么"来平衡报道。

**❌ 学生初版**
The spokesperson said the investigation was now criminal. Trump said the investigation was a witch-hunt. He didn't answer right away.

**✅ 升级版**
The spokesperson said: "We are now actively investigating the Trump Organization in a criminal capacity." The Trump Organization did not immediately respond to a request for comment. The former president has repeatedly dismissed the investigations as "witch-hunts".

**💡 效果**
升级版用"声明 → 无回应 → 一贯立场"的三层结构完成了客观报道，读者自然能判断双方的立场差距。学生版把这些信息打散了，失去了结构的张力。

---

### 结构四：证据说明段——"问题 + 细节 + 来源"的展开模式

当需要解释调查的具体内容时，FT不是直接抛结论，而是加上信息来源和具体细节。

> A focus of the probes, according to people briefed on the matter, is whether the company committed fraud by inflating the value of certain properties to secure bank loans while minimising them to reduce its taxes.

**结构公式：** [核心问题（whether...）] + [信息来源（according to...）] + [具体操作细节（by doing X while doing Y）]

**为什么好？**

学生往往只会写结论："They are investigating fraud." FT的写法：用whether引出问题的"两极性"（到底是不是欺诈），用according to标注信息来源的匿名性，用by...while...的平行结构说明两种操作逻辑的对比。

**❌ 学生初版**
The investigation is about fraud. The company might have lied about property values.

**✅ 升级版**
A focus of the probes, according to people briefed on the matter, is whether the company committed fraud by inflating property values to secure loans while minimising them to reduce taxes.

**💡 效果**
升级版用a focus of the probes（不是the only focus，留有余地），用according to（标注信息局限），用by...while...对比结构（合法借钱 vs 避税），一句话完成了"问题+来源+操作逻辑"三层信息。

---

### 结构五：结尾与展望段——"事情没有结束"

资讯类文章的结尾很少用"总而言之"式的总结。FT的结句往往是**一个开放的、提示未来走向的陈述**。

> A person briefed on James's probe said the focus on criminal matters did not rule out the possibility that it could still result in civil charges.

> Bachner said it was unclear whether James and Vance were pursuing the same criminal charge or separate ones.

**结构公式：** [新信息源] + [said/added] + [不确定/开放性问题（did not rule out / unclear whether）]

**为什么会这么写？**

一个以"依然不清楚..."结尾的句子，效果远比"In conclusion, the investigation is important"要好得多——它暗示故事还在发展，给读者留下思考空间。

**❌ 学生初版**
In conclusion, this investigation is very serious and will have important consequences.

**✅ 升级版**
A person briefed on the probe said the focus on criminal matters did not rule out the possibility of civil charges. It remains unclear whether the two offices are pursuing the same charge or separate ones.

**💡 效果**
升级版的结尾不是空洞的判断，而是用"did not rule out"和"It remains unclear"两个开放式结构，把结尾变成了对读者智力的邀请——你自己判断吧。

## 五种段落适用场景对比

| 段落结构 | 适用场景 | 核心句式 |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 升级开篇段 | 文章首段 | [变化] + after/because/following |
| 并列信息段 | 介绍两个相似或对立概念 | The other / Meanwhile / In contrast |
| 回应对峙段 | 呈现两方立场 | [A方引语] + [B方未回应] + [B方一贯立场] |
| 证据说明段 | 解释一个论点或指控 | whether... + according to... + by... |
| 结尾与展望段 | 文章末段 | did not rule out / remains unclear / whether... |

这五种段落结构可以直接套用在雅思大作文的"双方观点+讨论"题型中——每段承担一个明确功能，文章逻辑自然清晰。
