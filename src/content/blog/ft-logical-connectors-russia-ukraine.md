## 如何让你的作文产生"推理感"？

很多同学的作文里只有一个因果关系链："Because A, so B." 但真正高级的英文写作，需要在一个段落里构建**多层次逻辑**——既要表达目的（"为了……"）、也要标明原因（"由于……"）、还要加上对比（"尽管……但是……"）和条件（"在……情况下"）。

今天我们从FT一篇国际政治报道——《俄罗斯从俄乌边界撤离军队》——学习5个构建推理链条的逻辑结构。

---

## FT原文精选

> Russia's defence minister has ordered tens of thousands of troops recently deployed close to the border with Ukraine to return to their bases **in a move likely to** ease immediate fears of a conflict and tensions with the west.
>
> Moscow had moved as many as 100,000 troops to its border with Ukraine over recent weeks, sparking fears of a potential invasion and prompting condemnation from the US and Nato.
>
> Russia's rouble, **which is sensitive to** any threat of western sanctions, jumped as much as 1.4 per cent against the dollar on the news.
>
> The possibility of war between Russia and Ukraine has put more pressure on strained relations **due to** new US sanctions on Moscow, a string of tit-for-tat diplomatic expulsions and international anger at the poor health of jailed opposition activist Alexei Navalny.
>
> **While** the call for a withdrawal looks set to alleviate short-term fears of a conflict, the region will remain a potential flashpoint.
>
> Shoigu on Thursday said all Russian troops should remain "in a state of readiness for an immediate response **in case of** the unfavourable development" of Nato's annual exercises.

## 5个逻辑衔接结构详解

---

### 1. in a move to / in a move that —— 目的+结果

**原文：**
> Russia's defence minister has ordered tens of thousands of troops to return to their bases **in a move likely to** ease immediate fears of a conflict.

**为什么好：**
`in a move to` / `in a move that` 是FT记者最常用的逻辑结构之一——用一个名词短语（move）概括前面的行动，然后说明该行动的**目的或预期结果**。这比简单说 "This will ease fears" 更精致，因为它创建了一个"行动 → 逻辑意义"的桥梁。

**❌ 学生初版**
The government decided to reduce class sizes. This will help students learn better.

**✅ 升级版**
The government decided to reduce class sizes, in a move likely to improve student learning outcomes.

**💡 效果**
`in a move likely to` 把两个短句合成一个紧凑的逻辑链，从"做什么"直接过渡到"为什么做"，句子更有层次。

**模板：**
> [某人做了某事], **in a move** + [不定式短语 / 定语从句，说明目的或结果].

---

### 2. due to —— 形式正式的原因表达

**原文：**
> The possibility of war has put more pressure on strained relations **due to** new US sanctions, a string of tit-for-tat diplomatic expulsions and international anger at the poor health of Navalny.

**为什么好：**
`Due to` 是 `Because of` 的正式版，在学术写作中更受欢迎。而且原文展示了 `due to` 后接**并列的多重原因**——制裁、驱逐、愤怒——让原因链条更加丰富。

**❌ 学生初版**
Relations between the two countries got worse because of many problems.

**✅ 升级版**
Tensions escalated due to a series of sanctions, diplomatic expulsions, and growing international pressure.

**💡 效果**
`Due to` + 并列名词短语 = 一个句子塞入三个原因，信息密度和逻辑层次同时提升。

**模板：**
> [结果] **due to** [原因1], [原因2], and [原因3].

---

### 3. which is sensitive to —— 非限制性定语从句作因果说明

**原文：**
> Russia's rouble, **which is sensitive to** any threat of western sanctions, jumped as much as 1.4 per cent against the dollar on the news.

**为什么好：**
这个 `which` 从句的作用不是修饰"哪一种卢布"，而是**解释原因**——为什么卢布会跳涨？因为它对制裁威胁敏感。非限制性定语从句（有逗号）用作因果说明，是FT写作的标志性技巧。

**❌ 学生初版**
The Russian rouble jumped 1.4%. This is because the rouble is sensitive to western sanctions.

**✅ 升级版**
The Russian rouble, which is highly sensitive to the threat of western sanctions, surged 1.4 per cent on the news.

**💡 效果**
一个 `which` 从句囊括了因果关系，句子流畅、紧凑，不需要拆成两句。

**模板：**
> [主语], **which is** + [属性/特点], [谓语/结果].

---

### 4. While + 从句, 主句 —— 对比的"虽然……但是……"

**原文：**
> **While** the call for a withdrawal looks set to alleviate short-term fears of a conflict, the region will remain a potential flashpoint.

**为什么好：**
`While` 放在句首表示对比——"虽然撤离能缓解短期恐惧，但长期来看地区仍是火药桶"。这是 `Although` 的另一种表达，但比 `Although` 多了一层"同时对比"的意味，特别适合用于**承认短期成效、指出长期问题**的论证结构。

**❌ 学生初版**
The decision reduced fears. But the problem is not solved.

**✅ 升级版**
While the decision may alleviate short-term concerns, the underlying tensions are far from resolved.

**💡 效果**
`While...` 结构在雅思大作文中极为好用——你可以在一个句子里同时呈现事物的两面性。

**模板：**
> **While** + [短期/表面的事实], [长期/深层的结论].

---

### 5. in case of —— 条件式的预防逻辑

**原文：**
> All Russian troops should remain in a state of readiness for an immediate response **in case of** the unfavourable development of Nato's exercises.

**为什么好：**
`In case of` 表示"以防万一"，是一种**预防性条件**——不是"如果X发生，就做Y"，而是"做好准备，以防X发生"。这种条件逻辑在议论文中展示远见和周密思考。

**❌ 学生初版**
If there is an emergency, we should be ready.

**✅ 升级版**
The emergency services remain on standby, ready to respond in case of any escalation.

**💡 效果**
`In case of` 比 `if` 更精确地传达"防患于未然"的态度，在讨论安全、政策、风险管理时非常实用。

**模板：**
> [准备/措施] **in case of** + [潜在风险/不利情况].

## 综合性练习

用这5个结构中的至少3个，改写下面的学生段落：

**学生原文：**
> Many companies are now allowing employees to work from home. This can reduce office costs. But teamwork may become more difficult. The company should be ready for this problem.

**参考示范：**
> Many companies are now allowing employees to work from home, in a move likely to reduce office costs. While this approach may boost efficiency in the short term, weaker team cohesion remains a potential challenge. Employers should develop virtual collaboration tools in case of declining productivity.
