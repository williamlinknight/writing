每个中学生都会举例子来支持论点。但什么让论证有力？学生作文最常见的毛病就是"我认为→因为所以"的单层论证，缺乏数据锚点、权威背书、对比参照和因果链条。

今天我们从FT一篇关于辉瑞/BioNTech新冠疫苗"90%有效"的报道中，学习4个顶尖记者如何搭建**有层次、有说服力**的论证结构。

> A Covid-19 vaccine being developed by Pfizer and Germany's BioNTech has been found to be more than 90 per cent effective, in a breakthrough that could make the shot available for use by the end of the year if drug authorities give it the green light.
>
> The finding was the result of the first independent analysis of any Covid-19 vaccine in phase 3 trials — the final stage before commercial licensing. Out of the 43,000 trial participants, the small number who were infected enabled the independent evaluators to calculate the effectiveness after two doses.

> 辉瑞(Pfizer)和德国BioNTech联合开发的新冠疫苗被发现具有90%以上的有效率。这一突破意味着这种注射剂有望在获得药监部门批准后，在今年底就可用。该项发现是首个对任何COVID-19疫苗第3阶段试验——商业许可之前的最后阶段——进行独立分析的结果。在4.3万名试验参与者中，少量感染者使独立评估者能够计算出注射两剂疫苗后的有效率。

---

## 手法一：用具体数据锚定论证，而非空洞的"很多人"

**原文示例：**
> *Out of the 43,000 trial participants, the small number who were infected enabled the independent evaluators to calculate the effectiveness after two doses.*

为什么好？记者不说"试验规模很大"——他说**43,000人**。不说"效果很好"——他说**注射两剂后计算有效率**。数字本身就是论证。

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
*Many people think that studying abroad is beneficial for students.*

**✅ 升级版**
*According to a 2023 survey by the Ministry of Education, **over 70% of Chinese students** who studied abroad reported significant improvement in their cross-cultural communication skills. **Among 5,000 respondents**, those who completed a full overseas programme scored an average of 15% higher on language proficiency tests than their domestic peers.*

**💡 效果**
加入具体数据（70%、5,000人、15%）后，论证从"观点"升级为"有据可查的判断"。数字让你看起来做过调研。

## 手法二：专家权威引用——三层递进式背书

FT记者不只是引一家之言。他们构建了一个**多层权威金字塔**：

**第一层——当事人：**
> *"To me, this is the best possible outcome," Ugur Sahin, co-founder and chief executive of BioNTech told the Financial Times.*

**第二层——第三方科学权威：**
> *Anthony Fauci, the head of the US National Institutes of Health, described 90 per cent effectiveness as "just extraordinary".*

**第三层——独立学者（带谨慎性：**）
> *Brendan Wren, professor of vaccinology at the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine, said 90 per cent efficacy was "excellent for a new vaccine", while adding a note of caution. "It is a case of 'so far so good' but more confirmatory safety and efficacy studies are required."*

为什么好？三层递进：CEO→政府首席专家→独立学者。每一层增加一分可信度，且最后一位带**谨慎性限定**（nuance），让论证更可信——不偏袒、不夸张。

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
*Many scientists believe that AI will change the world.*

**✅ 升级版**
*Sam Altman, CEO of OpenAI, has described AI as "the most important technological revolution since the internet". Meanwhile, **Dr Fei-Fei Li, a professor at Stanford University and a pioneer in computer vision**, warns that the technology requires "careful governance". **A 2024 paper published in Nature** further notes that while AI boosts productivity in 60% of tested industries, its impact on employment remains "highly uneven".*

**💡 效果**
多方权威（企业创始人、学术界、同行评审期刊）交替支撑，且最后一方补充了数据限定。论证不再是"一个人说的"，而是"多方共识（含分歧）"。

## 手法三：对比论证——参照物让结论更有力

**原文示例：**
> *The Pfizer results far exceed the US Food and Drug Administration's criteria for approving a Covid-19 vaccine, which mandates an efficacy of at least 50 per cent in placebo-controlled trials.*

> *The drug is expected to be submitted to authorities for emergency approval in the third week of November, ahead of other vaccine developers. Former vaccine frontrunner Moderna does not expect to have the trial data required to go for approval until November 25, while AstraZeneca's partnership with the University of Oxford expects results by the end of this year.*

为什么好？两个对比：**(1)** 90% vs FDA最低标准50%——"远超"不言自明；**(2)** Pfizer vs Moderna vs AstraZeneca——时间线对比凸显"领先"。

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
*Electric cars are becoming more popular.*

**✅ 升级版**
*In 2025, global EV sales exceeded 20 million units, representing a 35% year-on-year increase. **By contrast**, sales of traditional petrol vehicles fell by 12% in the same period, the steepest decline in a decade. While the average EV still costs $5,000 more upfront, **the gap has narrowed from $15,000 just five years ago**.*

**💡 效果**
通过双重对比（EV vs 燃油车增长对比 + 价差缩短对比），论证从"趋势判断"升级为"可量化的结构性变化"。

## 手法四：因果推理——报道的隐藏骨架

这篇报道表面是新闻，底层是精密的因果链：

| 因 | 果 |
|:---|:---|
| 疫苗90%有效 | 股市全面上涨 |
| 旅行股受疫情打击最重 | 领涨（IAG翻倍、法航涨30%） |
| 油价受疫情打压 | 同步上涨8.5% |
| FDA标准50% | 90%远超标准，意义非凡 |

**原文示例（因果链呈现）：**
> *Global equity markets rallied sharply after Pfizer's announcement, led by travel and leisure companies that have been hit particularly hard by the coronavirus crisis.*

一个"after"就把原因说清楚了。随后用具体数字证明因果强度。

**考场用法对比：**

**❌ 学生初版**
*Social media is bad for students' mental health.*

**✅ 升级版**
*A 2024 study published in The Lancet tracked 10,000 teenagers over three years. It found that those who spent more than three hours per day on social media were **twice as likely** to report symptoms of anxiety. **As a result**, several countries, including France and Australia, have introduced legislation to restrict under-16 social media access.*

**💡 效果**
不直接说"有害"——而是给出因果链：社交媒体使用量→焦虑症状概率→政策回应。三段式因果让论证层层推进。

## 全文写作建议

FT这篇报道示范了一个完整论证的结构公式：

> **数据锚点 + 权威分层 + 对比参照 + 因果链条 + 谨慎限定 = 有说服力的论证**

下次写议论文时，给自己一个清单：
1. 用了吗？加到论述里
2. 引用了谁？最好三个层次
3. 有对比吗？找参照物
4. 因果链写清楚了吗？
5. 有"但"吗？加入谨慎限定会让论证更可信
