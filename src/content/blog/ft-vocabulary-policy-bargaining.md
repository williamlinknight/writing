写议论文时，最怕的是什么？是只会写 `A wants to do something, but B disagrees`。真正有深度的议论文，需要描述**各方立场之间的博弈**——谁在推动、谁在抗拒、谁在观望、谁在抨击。

今天我们从FT一篇《希腊呼吁欧盟尽快推出疫苗护照》中，提炼5个描述政策博弈的高分动词。

---

## FT原文精选

> Greece's tourism minister has called on EU leaders to "move more quickly" to embrace the vaccine certificates, as Athens seeks to repair a holiday industry that has been **battered by** the coronavirus pandemic.
>
> "Looking at the reaction of some countries … I feel there's a lot of **short sightedness**," minister Harry Theocharis told the Financial Times.
>
> Member states led by Greece **floated the idea** last month but the bloc is divided and diplomats see early adoption as unlikely.
>
> The restrictions on international travel **wiped** 4 per cent from Greece's GDP, as hotel and accommodation revenues **slumped by** two-thirds.
>
> Athens has been particularly **aggressive in pursuing** vaccination passports, but countries including Germany and France have **remain unconvinced**.
>
> He **hit out at** the British government for warning its citizens against international holidays.

---

## 词汇解析

### 1. batter — 重创、连续打击

**原文出处：** "a holiday industry that has been battered by the coronavirus pandemic"

**中文解释：**
`batter` 字面意思是"连续猛击"，引申为"某个行业/经济被反复打击"。比 `damage` 或 `hit` 强烈得多——暗示不是一次伤害，而是持续的、猛烈的打击。

**❌ 学生初版**
```text
The tourism industry was badly affected by the pandemic.
```

**✅ 升级版**
```text
Small businesses were battered by months of lockdown restrictions.
```

**💡 效果**
`battered by` 比 `badly affected by` 更有画面感——让读者感受到"持续的、反复的打击"，而不是一次性的影响。

**💡 例句**
```text
The manufacturing sector, battered by rising energy costs, has called for urgent government intervention.
```

### 2. float the idea — 提出（试探性的）想法

**原文出处：** "Member states led by Greece floated the idea last month."

**中文解释：**
`float the idea` 不是普通的 `suggest` 或 `propose`——它特指"试探性地提出某个想法，看各方反应如何"。"浮在水面上"的意象暗示这个想法还未定案，仍在观察阶段。

**❌ 学生初版**
```text
The government suggested a new policy.
```

**✅ 升级版**
```text
The education minister floated the idea of extending the school year by two weeks.
```

**💡 例句**
```text
Several board members have floated the idea of merging with a competitor, but no decision has been made.
```

### 3. wipe [...] from — 抹去、消除（通常与数字搭配）

**原文出处：** "the restrictions on international travel wiped 4 per cent from Greece's GDP"

**中文解释：**
`wipe X from Y` 是经济报道中的经典搭配——"从GDP中抹去了X个百分点"。比 `reduce` 更有冲击力——像用橡皮擦擦掉一样。

**❌ 学生初版**
```text
The disaster reduced the country's GDP by 4%.
```

**✅ 升级版**
```text
The financial crisis wiped 15 per cent from the value of pension funds.
```

**💡 例句**
```text
The ongoing trade war has wiped billions of dollars from the company's market value.
```

### 4. slump by — 骤降、暴跌

**原文出处：** "hotel and accommodation revenues slumped by two-thirds"

**中文解释：**
`slimp` 特指"突然的、大幅度的下降"，主要用于经济数据、销售额、价格等。比 `drop` 或 `fall` 强烈得多，暗示不是温和下降，而是暴跌。

**❌ 学生初版**
```text
Sales fell a lot during the crisis.
```

**✅ 升级版**
```text
Consumer confidence slumped to a record low amid rising inflation.
```

**💡 例句**
```text
Enrolment in language courses slumped by 40 per cent after the new policy took effect.
```

### 5. aggressive in pursuing — 积极推动、全力以赴

**原文出处：** "Athens has been particularly aggressive in pursuing vaccination passports"

**中文解释：**
`aggressive in pursuing` 不是"攻击性的"，而是"极其积极主动地推动某件事"。这比 `actively promote` 更强——暗示不惜动用一切资源的决心。

**❌ 学生初版**
```text
The city is actively trying to attract more tourists.
```

**✅ 升级版**
```text
The university has been aggressive in pursuing international partnerships.
```

**💡 例句**
```text
Tech companies are aggressive in recruiting top AI talent, offering salaries far above industry average.
```

## 写作建议

这5个表达的核心价值在于：它们让你从**平铺直叙**升级为**动态描述**。

写议论文时，不要只写：
- `Some people support it, some oppose it.`

试试这些结构：
- `A floated the idea of ... but B was unconvinced.`
- `The sector, battered by ..., called for ...`
- `The crisis wiped X from ... as revenues slumped by ...`
- `The government has been aggressive in pursuing ...`

一篇高分议论文的秘诀，就是把"发生了什么事"变成**"各方在博弈什么"**。
