## 原文精选｜FT Bilingual

> Facebook has agreed to restore Australian news on its platform **following an agreement with the government on amendments** to a draft law that would **force** Big Tech to **pay for** news.
>
> The social media company said on Tuesday it was **satisfied that** changes and guarantees agreed with Canberra **addressed its concerns** about allowing commercial deals that recognize the value our platform provides to publishers.
>
> The agreement **follows a public backlash** in Australia when Facebook last week blocked the sharing of all news on its platform.
>
> A **contentious** final arbitration model contained in the code will be **stipulated as** a "last resort" option.
>
> Nine said it was **pleased that** the government had reached a **compromise** to move Facebook back into the negotiations.

## 为什么好？

这段关于Facebook与澳大利亚政府新闻付费之争的报道，用词精准、句式凝练，是学习**正式英文议论文/说明文写作**的绝佳范本。报道中的动词搭配、名词短语和固定表达，正好帮助中国学生解决"用词太简单""表达不地道"的问题。

很多时候，同学写的句子语法没毛病，但读起来就是"像小学生作文"。原因只有一个：**词汇选择太基础**。今天我们从FT报道中摘取8组高频实用表达，配合学生常见写法 vs 升级写法对照，一看就懂、一学就会。

## 对照学习｜Student vs Upgrade

### 1️⃣ "agree to do" → "has agreed to restore"

**学生常见写法：**
The company said it would bring back news.

**FT升级表达：**
Facebook **has agreed to restore** Australian news on its platform.

| 基础词 | 升级表达 | 使用场景 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| bring back | **restore** | 恢复（原状）、重新启用 |
| say | **agree to + do** | 表示经协商后的承诺 |

**模板：** `[主体] has agreed to [动词] [宾语] following [原因/协议]`

**例句：** The school has agreed to extend the deadline following students' requests.

---

### 2️⃣ "because of" → "following an agreement on"

**学生常见写法：**
This happened because the government and the company made a deal.

**FT升级表达：**
...following an agreement with the government **on amendments** to a draft law.

| 基础词 | 升级表达 | 使用场景 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| because of | **following + 名词** | 表示时间/逻辑上的先后关系（比after更正式） |
| deal | **agreement on + 名词** | 正式协议 |

**模板：** `[结果] following [事件/措施]`

**例句：** The policy was revised following a series of public complaints.

---

### 3️⃣ "make sb do sth" → "force sb to do sth"

**学生常见写法：**
The new law makes companies pay for news.

**FT升级表达：**
...a draft law that would **force** Big Tech to **pay for** news.

| 基础词 | 升级表达 | 使用场景 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| make | **force** | 强迫、强制（法律/规则等强制执行） |
| pay | **pay for** | 为…付费（强调支付对象） |

**模板：** `[法律/规则] forces [主体] to [动作]`

**例句：** Environmental regulations force factories to reduce carbon emissions.

---

### 4️⃣ "was happy that" → "was satisfied that"

**学生常见写法：**
The company was happy that its concerns were solved.

**FT升级表达：**
...it **was satisfied that** changes and guarantees **addressed its concerns**.

| 基础词 | 升级表达 | 使用场景 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| happy | **satisfied** | 满意的（更正式、书面化） |
| solved | **addressed** | 处理、解决（问题/担忧） |
| concerns | **concerns** | 担忧（直接使用，搭配address） |

**模板：** `[主体] was satisfied that [措施] addressed [issues]`

**例句：** Parents were satisfied that the new safety measures addressed their concerns about bullying.

---

### 5️⃣ "many people got angry" → "a public backlash"

**学生常见写法：**
Many people got angry about Facebook's decision.

**FT升级表达：**
The agreement **follows a public backlash** in Australia.

| 基础词 | 升级表达 | 使用场景 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| many people got angry | **a public backlash** | 公众强烈反对（名词短语，简洁有力） |

**模板：** `[事件] triggered / faced / followed a public backlash`

**例句：** The sudden fee increase triggered a public backlash among students.

---

### 6️⃣ "disagreed on" → "a contentious model"

**学生常见写法：**
People disagreed on the final model.

**FT升级表达：**
A **contentious** final arbitration model...

| 基础词 | 升级表达 | 使用场景 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| disagreed on | **contentious** | 有争议的（形容词，比controversial更正式） |

**模板：** `a contentious [议题 / 方案 / 条款]`

**例句：** The contentious clause in the contract divided the board members.

---

### 7️⃣ "was seen as" → "was stipulated as"

**学生常见写法：**
The model was seen as a last method.

**FT升级表达：**
...will be **stipulated as** a "last resort" option.

| 基础词 | 升级表达 | 使用场景 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| seen as | **stipulated as** | 被规定为（法律/合同/政策用语） |
| method | **resort** | 手段、方法（last resort = 最后手段） |

**模板：** `[条款] is stipulated as [内容]`

**例句：** Written notice is stipulated as a mandatory step before termination.

---

### 8️⃣ "was happy that" → "was pleased that" + "reached a compromise"

**学生常见写法：**
The company was glad that the problem was solved.

**FT升级表达：**
Nine said it **was pleased that** the government had **reached a compromise**.

| 基础词 | 升级表达 | 使用场景 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| glad / happy | **pleased that** | 欣喜的（比happy正式，常用于公开声明） |
| problem solved | **reached a compromise** | 达成妥协/和解 |

**模板：** `[主体] was pleased that [双方] reached a compromise on [议题]`

**例句：** The union was pleased that management reached a compromise on working hours.

## 写作升级练习

把下面这段学生作文改写，使用FT学到的表达：

**原文：** "The new rules made many internet companies unhappy. People got angry about the changes. The government was happy that a deal was finally made."

**参考改写：** "The new rules **forced** many internet companies to adjust their operations. The changes **triggered a public backlash**. The government **was satisfied that** both sides **reached a compromise following** weeks of negotiation."

## 总结｜一个核心思维

词汇升级不是"背难词"，而是**用对的词**：

| 口语化表达 | 书面化升级 | 适用文体 |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| because of | following + 名词 | 议论文、报道 |
| make sb do | force sb to do | 议论文、政策评论 |
| people got angry | a public backlash | 现象分析类 |
| be happy that | be satisfied / pleased that | 正式信函、评论 |
| deal | agreement / compromise | 各类写作 |

把这些表达分类记忆，每次写议论文时主动用1-2个，一个月后你的作文"高级感"会明显提升。
