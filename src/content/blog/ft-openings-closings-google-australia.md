英语作文中，开头和结尾决定了阅卷老师的第一印象和最终印象。大多数同学写开头只会"Nowadays, with the development of society"，写结尾只会"In my opinion, I think..."——这些写法已经用了二十年，**阅卷老师看了只想跳过**。

今天我们从《金融时报》一篇关于微软与谷歌在澳大利亚博弈的报道中，学习4个真正高级的开头结尾技巧。这些技巧不需要生僻词汇，只需要换一种**结构思维**。

---

## 技巧一：冲突开场法（Conflict Opening）

平庸的开头是陈述一个"大家都知道的事实"；高级的开头是**直接亮出冲突**。

**FT原文：**

> Microsoft has moved to capitalise on Google's threat to shut its search engine in Australia, throwing its weight behind efforts to make Big Tech pay for news content and offering to transfer small businesses to its rival service Bing at no cost.

**中文对照：** 微软已开始利用谷歌威胁关闭在澳搜索引擎的机会，公开支持要求大型科技公司为新闻内容付费的努力，并主动提出免费帮小企业转移到竞争对手必应。

**🔥 为什么好：**

这句话在第一个词就设定了**矛盾双方**——Microsoft vs Google。整个段落的信息流是：A方（微软）在B方（谷歌）犯错的时刻出手 → 具体做了什么（支持法案 + 帮小企业转移）。信息密度极高，读者读完第一句就知道"这篇文章要讲什么冲突"。

**❌ 学生写法 vs ✅ 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法（冲突开场） |
|---------|------------------|
| Nowadays, many tech companies are competing with each other. Google and Microsoft are two famous companies. | When Google threatened to exit Australia, Microsoft seized the opportunity — offering free migration to its rival search engine Bing. |
| Recently, more and more people are talking about the news media bargaining code in Australia. | The news media bargaining code exposed a rare fracture between Big Tech giants: while Google threatened to walk away, Microsoft chose to stay and compete. |

**📝 模板：**

> When [事件A] happened, [人物/组织B] seized the opportunity — [具体行动].
> When [对方] threatened to [行动], [我方] chose to respond by [具体行动].

## 技巧二：预判式结尾（Projection Ending）

很多学生的结尾只是"总结前面说了什么"。真正有力的结尾是**把结论推向未来**，告诉读者这个事件会带来什么后果。

**FT原文（结尾段）：**

> The push to make Big Tech pay for news also comes as Google, Facebook and other Silicon Valley companies face intensifying global scrutiny over their market dominance.

**中文对照：** 在推动大型科技企业为新闻付费之际，谷歌、Facebook和其他硅谷公司正因其市场主导地位在全球面临越来越严格的审查。

**🔥 为什么好：**

这个结尾没有简单重复"谷歌不想付钱、微软想趁虚而入"，而是把视角**拉远到全球层面**——"intensifying global scrutiny" 暗示这不是澳大利亚一个国家的行为，而是全球趋势。读完这句话，读者会意识到澳大利亚只是冰山一角。

**❌ 学生写法 vs ✅ 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法（预判结尾） |
|---------|------------------|
| In conclusion, this shows that tech companies are facing more difficulties. | The Australia dispute is not an isolated case. As Google and Facebook face intensifying scrutiny worldwide, this battle in Canberra may well set a precedent for other nations. |
| To sum up, people should pay more attention to this problem. | What began as a single country's push for fair compensation could soon become the blueprint for the global regulation of tech giants. |

**📝 模板：**

> The [specific event] is not an isolated case. As [broader trend] unfolds, [the current situation] may well set a precedent for [larger consequence].

## 技巧三：引语收束法（Quote Ending）

最高级的结尾方式之一，是用**第三方评论**来替你说出结论，而不是自己总结。这样既客观又有深度。

**FT原文：**

> John Kettle, partner at McCullough Robertson, a law firm in Brisbane, said the media code was emblematic of the start of a "tech war" as sovereign countries moved to reassert control over trillion-dollar digital behemoths.
>
> He said Google's public threat to exit Australia was a mistake: "It simply points out to people that there are alternatives to its search engine and has provided rivals with an opportunity."

**🔥 为什么好：**

John Kettle说了两个层面的内容：一是大格局（主权国家重新控制科技巨头 = 宏观趋势），二是一句话点评（谷歌的威胁是错误 = 具体判断）。**大格局 + 具体判断**的组合，让结尾既不失高度又不失细节。

**❌ 学生写法 vs ✅ 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法（引语收束） |
|---------|------------------|
| In my opinion, this is a very important event. | As one analyst put it, the dispute marked the start of a "tech war" — a conflict that, in his words, "simply points out to people that there are alternatives." |
| I think the government should take action. | "The code is emblematic of a broader shift," said a legal expert, as "sovereign countries moved to reassert control over trillion-dollar digital behemoths." |

**📝 模板：**

> As [expert role] put it, "[direct quote that captures the essence]."
> "[Short, punchy quote]," said [expert]. The message is clear: [your closing thought].

## 技巧四：视角拉升法（Scope Expansion）

结尾的另一种方式：从**一个国家的故事**拉升到**全球趋势**，让文章结尾有"余音绕梁"之感。

**FT原文中段（可用于结尾收束）：**

> Google and Facebook face intensifying global scrutiny over their market dominance.

**🔥 为什么好：**

从澳大利亚到全球，从两家公司到整个行业——这种**视角拉升**让人读完后不只是在想"澳大利亚怎么了"，而是在思考"全球科技监管的下一步是什么"。好的结尾不是让读者停止思考，而是**让读者开始思考**。

**❌ 学生写法 vs ✅ 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法（视角拉升） |
|---------|------------------|
| This event is very important for Australia. | What happened in Australia may well ripple across the globe, as regulators in Europe and the US watch closely. |
| I hope this problem can be solved. | Whether or not Australia's code succeeds, one thing is clear: the era of tech companies operating without local accountability is coming to an end. |

**📝 模板：**

> Whether or not [specific event] succeeds, one thing is clear: [global trend / irreversible shift].
> [Local issue] may well ripple across [broader scope], as [stakeholders] watch closely.

---

## 总结：开头结尾的4条黄金法则

| 位置 | 法则 | 核心思路 |
|------|------|---------|
| 开头 | **冲突开场** | 第一句话就让读者知道"谁vs谁" |
| 结尾 | **预判未来** | 不是总结过去，而是推测趋势 |
| 结尾 | **引语收束** | 让第三方替你把话说透 |
| 结尾 | **拉升视角** | 从小故事到大格局，留有余味 |

下次写作文，不要再写"Nowadays"和"In my opinion"了。试试这些手法——**开头制造冲突，结尾推向未来**，你的作文分数会上一个台阶。
