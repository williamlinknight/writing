有没有被英语写作「卡到词穷」的同学？一提「介绍中国」的作文，脑袋里只有「长城」「熊猫」，写出来要么干巴巴凑字数，要么翻来覆去就那几句。

「中国名片」类写作可是英语考试的高频题，既要讲清事物特点，又得挖出文化内涵。今天就带大家手把手搞定这类作文，从选名片到套句式，一步到位。

## 题目回放

> 如同意大利的披萨、瑞士的钟表或比利时的巧克力一样，许多国家都有其独特的「名片」。如果让你选择一张能代表中国的「名片」，你会选择什么？请你以此为话题，写一篇不少于 100 词的英语作文。

**内容提示：**
1. What can symbolize China?
2. What is it like?
3. Why do you think it is a good choice for China?

---

## 写作素材库

### 技巧点拨

**确定主题：** 挑选一个能够代表中国的「名片」——你选择的对象应能体现中国的特色、文化或精神。

**三段式结构：**

| 段落 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 第一段 | 介绍你所挑选的事物 |
| 第二段 | 解释选择这个事物的原因 |
| 第三段 | 总结观点，强调该象征物如何代表中国的文化和精神 |

**语言要求：** 使用简单、准确的词汇表达，注意句子结构的多样性，避免重复使用简单句，确保语法和拼写的正确性。

### 重点词汇

| 中国名片 | 相关词汇 |
|----------|---------|
| **The Great Wall** 长城 | magnificent 雄伟的、ancient 古老的、historical 与历史有关的 |
| **Panda** 熊猫 | lovely/cute 可爱的、national treasure 国宝、unique 独一无二的 |
| **Peking Opera** 京剧 | artistic 艺术的、cultural heritage 文化遗产 |
| **Chinese Cuisine** 中国菜 | tasty/delicious 可口的、healthy 健康的、diverse 各式各样的 |
| **Tea** 茶叶 | traditional 传统的、influential 有影响力的、centuries-old 历史悠久的 |
| **Silk** 丝绸 | exquisite 精美的、precious 珍贵的、symbol of elegance 优雅的象征 |

### 写作金句

**引入主题：**

- My choice for the symbol of China is the panda.
- When it comes to a symbol of China, the first thing that comes into my mind is tea.
- For me, the most symbolic thing is Chinese porcelain (瓷器), also known as "china".

**描述事物：**

- They have a unique appearance with black patches (斑块) around their eyes, ears and across their bodies.
- It began in China and spread worldwide throughout the centuries.
- It is well known for its beauty, craftsmanship (工艺) and creative designs.

**选择原因：**

- They play an important role in China's diplomacy (外交), often being used as goodwill ambassadors (友好大使) in international relations.
- As one of the most visited tourist attractions in the world, the Great Wall shows China's rich cultural heritage and attracts millions of visitors every year.
- China's rich tea culture has influenced (影响) many countries throughout history.

## 小试身手

**中译英练习：**

（1）在我心中，最能代表中国的就是丝绸。

> In my heart, what best represents China is silk.

（2）很久以前，只有皇帝和非常重要的人才能穿丝绸。

> Long ago, only emperors and very important people were allowed to wear silk.

（3）中国是世界上第一个制造丝绸的国家，而且在很长一段时间内这一工艺都是保密的。

> China was the first country in the world to make silk, and this skill was kept a secret for a long time.

（4）丝绸曾在连接世界不同文化方面发挥着重要作用，是中国丰富历史的象征。

> Silk played a key role in connecting different cultures around the world and is a symbol of China's rich history.

## 参考范文结构

**可选中国名片：** 长城 / 熊猫 / 京剧 / 中国菜 / 茶叶 / 丝绸 / 瓷器 / 天坛

**写作框架：**
- **Para 1** → My choice for the symbol of China is ...
- **Para 2** → It is ... (特点描述). It plays an important role in / is well known for ...
- **Para 3** → From it, I learned / It shows China's rich ...

## 天坛专题 · 学生范文（B1水平）

### 天坛相关词汇

| 中文 | 英文 |
|------|------|
| 天坛 | the Temple of Heaven |
| 在北京南部 | in the south of Beijing |
| 建于……年 | was built in ... |
| 明清两代 | Ming and Qing dynasties |
| 皇帝 | emperor |
| 祭天 | pray to Heaven / worship Heaven |
| 祈求丰收 | pray for good harvests |
| 圆形建筑 | round building |
| 蓝色琉璃瓦 | blue glazed tiles |
| 祈年殿 | the Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests |
| 回音壁 | the Echo Wall |
| 世界文化遗产 | a UNESCO World Heritage site |

### 范文

> **My choice for the symbol of China is the Temple of Heaven.**
>
> The Temple of Heaven is a famous building in the south of Beijing. It was built in 1420 during the Ming dynasty. The emperors of the Ming and Qing dynasties went there to pray to Heaven for good harvests every year. The most important building is the Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests. It is round and has beautiful blue glazed tiles, so it looks very special.
>
> **I think the Temple of Heaven is a great choice because it shows Chinese culture and wisdom.** The round shape of the building represents the sky, and the square walls around it represent the earth — this was the ancient Chinese idea of "round heaven and square earth". It shows how Chinese people, hundreds of years ago, already understood the importance of living in harmony with nature. Also, the buildings are so beautiful that they attract millions of visitors every year from all over the world. In 1998, it became a UNESCO World Heritage site.
>
> **For me, the Temple of Heaven is more than just a building.** It is a symbol of China's long history, traditional culture, and the wisdom of our ancestors. When people see it, they think of China.
>
> *(181 words)*

这篇范文虽然没有华丽的词汇和高难的句型，但结构清晰、内容充实、表达自然——这正是中考高分作文的关键：**不追求炫技，重在逻辑清晰 + 内容完整 + 语言准确**。
