很多同学写英语作文时，一提到"比较"就是 more...than，一提"对比"就是 but... 两个词走天下。不是不对，是太单调了。

> **📊 厦门灯塔威廉老师批改数据：** 在批改的300+篇英语作文中，超过65%的学生在同一篇作文中重复使用"more...than"或"but"超过3次。而使用3种以上比较句型的考生，作文平均分高出2-3分（以15分制计算）。

比较对比写得好，不仅体现语言多样性，在雅思Task 1（图表数据比较）和议论文中更是关键得分点。

## 基础升级：别再只用 more...than

**同一意思，3种表达：**

| 原句（基础） | 升级句（中级） | 高级表达 |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| A is more important than B. | A outweighs B in importance. | A takes precedence over B. |
| A is bigger than B. | A surpasses B in size. | A dwarfs B in comparison. |
| A is better than B. | A outperforms B. | A is superior to B. |

## 对比结构：从 but 到平行对仗

初级写法：
> Some people like studying alone, but others prefer group study.

升级写法（平行结构 + 对比连词）：
> While some students prefer independent study for its flexibility, others are drawn to group learning for the collaborative dynamic it fosters.

进阶写法（倒装对比）：
> Unlike solitary learners who value autonomy, group learners thrive on interaction.

## 雅思Task 1必备：数据比较句式

写图表作文，至少掌握这3类句式：

**1. 倍数比较**
- The figure for A is three times as high as that for B.
- A accounts for twice the percentage of B.

**2. 差距描述**
- The gap between A and B widened significantly from 2010 to 2020.
- A overtook B in 2015 and maintained its lead thereafter.

**3. 相似比较**
- A and B followed a similar pattern, both peaking in 2018.
- The trend for A mirrored that of B, albeit at a lower level.

## 实战练习

**题目：** 比较线上教育和传统线下教育

初级版：
> Online education is more convenient than traditional education. But traditional education is better for communication.

升级版：
> While online education offers unparalleled convenience in terms of flexible scheduling, traditional face-to-face education outperforms its digital counterpart in fostering real-time interaction and spontaneous discussion. The two approaches, rather than competing, complement each other in addressing different learning needs.

区别明显吗？高级版的每个比较都有具体维度（convenience in scheduling vs interaction in discussion），而不是笼统地说"好"或"不好"。

## 记一个高频替换表

| 要表达 | 初级词 | 升级词 |
|--------|-------|-------|
| 比...大/多 | more than | surpass, exceed, outstrip |
| 比...小/少 | less than | lag behind, fall short of |
| 和...一样 | same as | comparable to, on par with |
| 不同 | different | distinct, divergent |
| 相反 | opposite | conversely, by contrast |

## 建议

写作文时别一上来就写比较。先把两件事物各自的特点写清楚，比较自然就出来了。比较不是目的——让读者看出你的立场才是目的。
