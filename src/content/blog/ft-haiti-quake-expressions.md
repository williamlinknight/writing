## 🎯 为什么要读这篇？

灾难话题是中考、高考、雅思写作中常见的高分考题类型。但中国学生写这类话题时，词汇和句式往往过于简单——永远是"many people died"、"the city was damaged"。

读FT记者如何写海地地震，你会发现**同一个话题可以有完全不同的表达层次**。

---

## 📝 FT原文精选

> Survivors of a powerful earthquake in Haiti **had more misery heaped on them** overnight as Tropical Storm Grace **doused them with heavy rain and hit them with high winds**.

> The magnitude 7.2 quake struck, **killing** at least 1,941 people, **injuring** almost 9,900 and **destroying** more than 37,000 houses.

> A lot of the hospitals are **completely overwhelmed** and are still receiving new patients three days after the earthquake.

> The disaster **rekindled memories** of the devastating 2010 earthquake.

> Prime Minister Ariel Henry **has vowed** that his government **would** "increase our energies tenfold to bring assistance to the maximum number of victims possible".

## 🔍 为什么好？

### 1️⃣ "had more misery heaped on them" — 被动叠加结构

**结构分析**：`have sth done (to sb)` 的被动形式，加上 `heap misery on` 这个固定搭配，意思是"苦难被层层叠加"。

**为什么高级**：普通学生会写"They suffered more"，而这里用了被动+隐喻的双重技巧——既客观又有画面感。

### 2️⃣ "killing... injuring... destroying..." — 分词并列列举

**结构分析**：三个现在分词（killing / injuring / destroying）并列，描述地震造成的三重后果。

**为什么高级**：比"and killed... and injured..."紧凑得多。这种**分词状语列举**在雅思大作文中非常常见，用来展开一个事件的多重影响。

### 3️⃣ "completely overwhelmed" — 强势形容词

**结构分析**：`overwhelm` 本身已经是很强的动词（压倒、淹没），加上 `completely` 加强语气。

**为什么高级**：普通学生会写"very busy"，写"overwhelmed"立刻提升到B2-C1水平。

### 4️⃣ "rekindled memories" — 动词隐喻

**结构分析**：`rekindle` 原意是"重新点燃"，这里比喻"唤起记忆"。

**为什么高级**：`rekindle` 比 `bring back` 或 `refresh` 更诗意、更有力量。这种跨领域的动词用法（abstract use of concrete verbs）是雅思7分+的标志。

### 5️⃣ "has vowed that... would..." — 正式引述结构

**结构分析**：`vow + that-clause` + 间接引语中的 `would`。

**为什么高级**：普通学生会写"He said he would..."，而 `vow`（郑重承诺）传递了更强的语气和正式感。

## 📊 学生写法 vs 升级写法

| 初级写法 | FT升级写法 | 提升点 |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| They suffered more problems. | They had more misery heaped on them. | 被动结构 + 搭配升级 |
| The earthquake killed 1,941 people and injured 9,900. | The quake struck, killing 1,941 people, injuring 9,900 and destroying 37,000 houses. | 分词并列 + 信息密度 |
| The hospitals were very busy. | The hospitals were completely overwhelmed. | 强势动词 + 副词强化 |
| The disaster brought back memories. | The disaster rekindled memories. | 动词隐喻 |
| He said the government would help. | He vowed that the government would "increase our energies tenfold". | 正式引述 |

## 💡 模板 + 例句

**模板一：被动叠加**
> `Survivors / Residents / Victims of [灾难] had [更多负面后果] heaped on them as [后续事件].`
> ✅ *Residents of the flooded city had more misery heaped on them as a second typhoon approached.*

**模板二：分词列举**
> `The [灾难] struck / hit, [verb1]-ing [number], [verb2]-ing [number] and [verb3]-ing [number].`
> ✅ *The wildfire swept through the village, destroying 200 homes, displacing 1,500 residents and claiming 12 lives.*

**模板三：强势形容词**
> `[名词] + be + completely / totally / absolutely + overwhelmed.`
> ✅ *Local hospitals were completely overwhelmed by the sudden influx of patients.*

**模板四：动词隐喻**
> `The [事件] rekindled [名词] of [过去的事件].`
> ✅ *The new outbreak rekindled fears of another global lockdown.*

**模板五：正式引述**
> `[人名] vowed that [主语] would [行动].`
> ✅ *The mayor vowed that the city would rebuild all damaged schools within six months.*
