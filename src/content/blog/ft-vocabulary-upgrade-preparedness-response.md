词汇升级系列继续推进。 今天我们从一篇"计划与准备"类报道中学5个**动词语义群**——拿到任何一个需要描述"应对挑战""建立能力""做好准备"的题目，你都有现成的高级表达可以套用。

> 为什么选这个主题？
>
> 中国学生写"准备"类内容时，翻来覆去就是 **prepare for / make preparations**，频率高到考官审美疲劳。而FT记者在描写欧盟为未来大流行病做准备时，用了至少10种不同的动词语义，每一个都比 **prepare for** 更具体、更有画面感。

---

## 📖 FT原文选段

> Europe must prepare its medical sector **to cope with** an "era of pandemics", the European Commission president said, as she warned the bloc was still in its most difficult period for Covid-19 vaccine deliveries.
>
> Ursula von der Leyen told the Financial Times that the EU **could not afford to sit still** even once Covid-19 has been overcome, as she described her plans for a Europewide fast-reaction system designed **to respond more quickly to** emerging medical threats.
>
> "Europe **is determined to enlarge** its strength in vaccine production," she said.
>
> Von der Leyen last month **unveiled plans** for a biodefence preparedness plan called the HERA Incubator, which will combine researchers, biotech companies, manufacturers and public authorities.
>
> The concept is an attempt **to mirror** some of the benefits **conferred by** America's Biomedical Advanced Research and Development Authority, which **is charged with** the job of responding rapidly to new health threats.
>
> "But Europe has **to build up to be prepared for** whatever comes," she said.

---

## 🎯 5组高分词汇解析

### 1️⃣ cope with — 应对

**原文出处：** "Europe must prepare its medical sector to cope with an 'era of pandemics'."

**为什么好：** 学生写"应对某问题"几乎只用 **deal with** 或 **handle**。**cope with** 强调的是"虽然有困难但努力应对"，天然含有一层"不轻松"的意味，用在议论文中比 **deal with** 更精确。

**❌ 学生初版**
The government must **deal with** the problem of air pollution.

**✅ 升级版**
The government must **cope with** the severe challenge of air pollution while maintaining economic growth.

**💡 效果**
cope with 自带"艰难应对"的语气，比 deal with 更能在第一句就建立文章的张力。

**例句：** Schools are struggling to **cope with** the increasing demand for mental health support among teenagers.

---

### 2️⃣ could not afford to sit still — 不能停下脚步

**原文出处：** "the EU could not afford to sit still even once Covid-19 has been overcome"

**为什么好：** 这整条短语就是一个精彩的动词语义组。**afford to** 暗示某事的代价太高，**sit still** 用"呆坐不动"的具象动作代替了抽象的"不做改变"。两个词叠加，画面感极强。

**❌ 学生初版**
We cannot **stop improving** our education system.

**✅ 升级版**
We **cannot afford to sit still** while other countries are racing ahead in educational innovation.

**💡 效果**
像一幅画面：你坐着不动，别人在冲刺。比任何空洞的"必须继续努力"都有冲击力。

**例句：** In the face of rapid technological change, traditional industries **cannot afford to sit still**.

---

### 3️⃣ be determined to enlarge — 决心扩大

**原文出处：** "Europe is determined to enlarge its strength in vaccine production."

**为什么好：** 学生写"决心做某事"几乎只用 **determined to do**，但很少在后面接 **enlarge/strengthen/build** 这样具体、有力的动词。**determined to enlarge** 比 **determined to improve** 更具体——enlarge 是"扩大规模"，有量化的感觉。

**❌ 学生初版**
The company **is determined to improve** its market share.

**✅ 升级版**
The company **is determined to enlarge** its presence in the Southeast Asian market.

**💡 效果**
enlarge 比 improve 更"可测量"，一看就知道是在说规模扩展，不是泛泛而谈的"变好"。

**例句：** China **is determined to enlarge** its investment in renewable energy infrastructure.

---

### 4️⃣ unveil plans / be charged with — 公布计划 / 负责

**原文出处①：** "Von der Leyen last month **unveiled plans** for a biodefence preparedness plan..."

**原文出处②：** "...which **is charged with** the job of responding rapidly to new health threats."

**为什么好：**

- **unveil**（揭开面纱）比 **announce** 或 **release** 更正式、更有仪式感，适合用在政策、报告、改革等场合。
- **be charged with**（被赋予职责）比 **be responsible for** 更正式、权威。**charged** 有"法官/上级授予责权"的感觉，用在制度/机构描述中非常精准，是高考和雅思写作高端词汇。

**❌ 学生初版**
The government **announced** a new environmental plan. An agency **is responsible for** carrying it out.

**✅ 升级版**
The government **unveiled** an ambitious environmental plan, and a newly established agency **is charged with** implementing it.

**💡 效果**
unveiled 让"公布"变得有画面感（像揭幕仪式），charged with 让"负责"变得有官方权威感。

**例句：** The committee **unveiled** a series of measures designed to tackle youth unemployment, and local authorities **are charged with** putting them into practice.

---

### 5️⃣ build up to be prepared for — 逐步建立以做好准备

**原文出处：** "Europe has to **build up to be prepared for** whatever comes."

**为什么好：** 很多学生知道 **build up**（逐步建立）和 **be prepared for**（为……做准备），但很少把它们组合成一个连贯的语义链。**build up to be prepared for** 包含了"过程→目标"两个阶段——先积累、建设（build up），然后才能应对（be prepared for）。

**❌ 学生初版**
We need to **prepare for** natural disasters.

**✅ 升级版**
We need to **build up** our emergency response capacity **to be prepared for** increasingly frequent natural disasters.

**💡 效果**
build up 把"准备"从一个即时动作变成了一个渐进过程，让句子更有层次感——不是瞬间做好，而是逐步积累。

**例句：** Schools should **build up** students' critical thinking skills **to be prepared for** the challenges of the information age.

## ✍️ 写作建议

这5组表达背后有一个共同点：**它们不是孤立地替换一个词，而是一整套动词语义链**。

比如"准备"这个词，学生只会一个 **prepare for**。但FT作者可以根据侧重点不同，选用：

| 想要强调 | 用哪个 |
|---------|-------|
| 艰难应对 | cope with |
| 不能不做 | cannot afford to sit still |
| 决心扩大 | be determined to enlarge |
| 正式公布 | unveil |
| 官方授责 | be charged with |
| 逐步积累 | build up to be prepared for |

**建议你**在笔记本上分主题积累动词语义链，而不是零散背单词。把"准备"当作一道菜，你的工具箱里要有6把不同的刀——每个场合选最合适的那一把。
