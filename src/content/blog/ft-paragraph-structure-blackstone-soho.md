很多学生写英文作文，最大的问题不是语法，而是**不知道每个段落该干什么**。

一段写观点，一段写例子——听起来简单，但实际操作中，学生常常把背景、数据、人物、评价全部塞进同一个段落，导致段落臃肿且逻辑不清。

今天我们从《金融时报》一篇关于黑石收购SOHO中国失败的报道中，学习英文写作的**段落功能分类法**。

---

## 原文示范

> Shares in Soho China fell as much as 40 per cent on Monday after US private equity group Blackstone pulled its $3bn takeover of the Chinese property company last week.
>
> About $830m was wiped from Soho China's market value as its Hong Kong-listed shares tumbled when the exchange opened for trading.
>
> The failure of the acquisition came against a backdrop of expansive policy overhauls and regulatory clampdowns in China, where authorities have increased scrutiny of foreign investment and cracked down on what they perceive to be monopolistic business practices.
>
> The failed deal has also returned the spotlight to Soho China founders Pan Shiyi and Zhang Xin. The colourful husband and wife pair has been synonymous with the development of the Beijing and Shanghai skylines.
>
> For Blackstone, the failed takeover of Soho's portfolio of prime real estate in China's biggest cities marked a blow to co-founder Stephen Schwarzman's plans to boost his group's position in the country.

这篇文章的五个段落，各自完成一个不同的功能。我们来逐一拆解。

---

## 功能一：开篇定调（Summary Lead）

> Shares in Soho China fell as much as 40 per cent on Monday after US private equity group Blackstone pulled its $3bn takeover of the Chinese property company last week.

**这段在干什么？** 只有一件事：告诉读者"发生了什么"，以及"这事多大"。

它包含三个元素：
- 主体（SOHO中国股价）
- 变化幅度（下跌40%）
- 原因（黑石终止收购）

**它刻意不做的事：** 不解释背景、不分析原因、不引用数据细节。就是"啪"地一下把新闻扔出来。

| 学生常见写法 | FT写法 |
|------------|--------|
| Soho China's share price fell. Blackstone stopped buying the company. This was because... | Shares in Soho China fell as much as 40 per cent after Blackstone pulled its $3bn takeover. |

**模板：**
> [Subject] [verb] + [具体数据/幅度] after [关键事件].

**跨话题例句：**
> *The number of students choosing online learning surged by 35 per cent after the university introduced flexible class schedules.*

---

## 功能二：数据支撑（Data Support）

> About $830m was wiped from Soho China's market value as its Hong Kong-listed shares tumbled when the exchange opened for trading.

**这段在干什么？** 用具体数字证明第一段说的"下跌40%"不是空话。**$830m**这个数字让抽象的比例变得可感知。

学生常犯的错误：写完开篇段就跳到观点分析，忘了先用数据把事实坐实。

| 学生常见写法 | FT写法 |
|------------|--------|
| Many investors lost a lot of money. The stock market was very bad. | About $830m was wiped from Soho China's market value as shares tumbled. |

**模板：**
> About [具体金额/数量] was [动作结果], as [具体指标] [动词] [幅度].

**跨话题例句：**
> *About $2 million was cut from the school's annual budget as enrolment numbers fell by 15 per cent.*

---

## 功能三：背景铺垫（Background Context）

> The failure of the acquisition came against a backdrop of expansive policy overhauls and regulatory clampdowns in China, where authorities have increased scrutiny of foreign investment and cracked down on what they perceive to be monopolistic business practices.

**这段在干什么？** 从前两段的"发生了什么"，拓宽到"在什么背景下发生的"。

核心句式：**came against a backdrop of** —— 这是一个学生很少用但极其高级的"背景引入"结构。

| 学生常见写法 | FT写法 |
|------------|--------|
| This happened because the Chinese government is doing new policies about foreign investment. | The failure came against a backdrop of policy overhauls, where authorities have increased scrutiny of foreign investment. |

**模板：**
> [事件] came against a backdrop of [宏观背景], where [具体变化] have [具体结果].

**跨话题例句：**
> *The rise in tuition fees came against a backdrop of government funding cuts, where universities have been forced to increase reliance on private donations.*

---

## 功能四：人物视角（Human Element）

> The failed deal has also returned the spotlight to Soho China founders Pan Shiyi and Zhang Xin. The colourful husband and wife pair has been synonymous with the development of the Beijing and Shanghai skylines.

**这段在干什么？** 从"数据和经济环境"切换到"人"——让读者看到这件事背后有具体的、有故事的人。

**核心句式：returned the spotlight to** —— 意思是"让某人重新成为焦点"。这个表达比"people started talking about"高级得多。

| 学生常见写法 | FT写法 |
|------------|--------|
| People began to talk about the company's founders again. Their story is very interesting. | The failed deal returned the spotlight to the founders, a colourful pair synonymous with the city's skyline. |

**模板：**
> [事件] returned the spotlight to [人物], [同位语介绍人物特征].

**跨话题例句：**
> *The school's winning streak at national competitions returned the spotlight to its principal, a former Olympic coach turned educator.*

---

## 功能五：影响收束（Impact Closure）

> For Blackstone, the failed takeover of Soho's portfolio of prime real estate in China's biggest cities marked a blow to co-founder Stephen Schwarzman's plans to boost his group's position in the country.

**这段在干什么？** 关闭视角，回到"这件事对谁意味着什么"——把局部事件放回到更大格局中。**For Blackstone**开头，明确告诉读者"这段是从黑石的角度看"。

核心句式：**marked a blow to** —— "对……是一个打击"，比"was bad for"正式很多。

| 学生常见写法 | FT写法 |
|------------|--------|
| This was bad for Blackstone. Their plans in China would be harder. | The failed takeover marked a blow to Blackstone's plans to boost its position in China. |

**模板：**
> For [利益相关方], [事件] marked a blow to [其目标/计划].

**跨话题例句：**
> *For local parents, the school's closure marked a blow to their hopes of keeping their children in community-based education.*

---

## 功能总结

| 段落位置 | 功能 | 一句话说明 | 不做的事 |
|---------|------|-----------|---------|
| 第一段 | 开篇定调 | 告诉读者发生了什么+多严重 | 不做背景解释 |
| 第二段 | 数据支撑 | 用具体数字证明第一段的说法 | 不加观点评价 |
| 第三段 | 背景铺垫 | 把事件放到更大的环境中 | 不重复已说的事实 |
| 第四段 | 人物视角 | 引入故事中的人，增加可读性 | 不堆砌数据 |
| 第五段 | 影响收束 | 回到大局，总结事件意义 | 不写"总之我认为" |

## 写作建议

下次写作文时，写完一段问自己三个问题：

1. **这一段的核心任务是什么？** —— 是描述事实？提供数据？还是解释背景？
2. **这一段有没有做它不该做的事？** —— 开篇段不解释背景，数据段不加观点。
3. **删掉这一段，文章逻辑断不断？** —— 每个段落都应该有存在的理由。

一个段落只做一个功能。比把所有东西塞进一段要好得多。
