## 原文片段

Nicolas Sarkozy has been sentenced to three years in prison, including two suspended, after a Paris court **found the former French president guilty of** corruption and influence peddling.

Sarkozy, who **maintained his innocence** and will appeal, is the second French leader under the Fifth Republic to be convicted after leaving office.

Prosecutors said Sarkozy in 2014 promised to help Azibert get a job in Monaco **in exchange for** information related to another case.

The court decided that the offer in exchange for confidential information was **sufficient evidence of** corruption.

## 为什么这些表达值得学？

很多中国学生写英语作文，用的是"安全词"——正确的、但平淡的。上面的四个表达，有两个特点：

1. **地道** —— 英美媒体和法律文书高频使用，阅卷老师一看就是"读过外刊"的水平
2. **可迁移** —— 不只是法律场景，议论文、记叙文、图表作文都能用

## 逐项拆解

### 1. found sb guilty of sth / be found guilty of

**学生写法：** The court said he did something wrong.

**升级写法：** The court found him guilty of fraud.

**原理：** `find + 宾语 + 宾补（形容词）` 是一个极其实用的句型，表示"判定某人处于某种状态"。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The teacher said he cheated. | The teacher found him guilty of cheating. |
| People think the company caused the pollution. | The company was found guilty of causing pollution. |
| Experts say smartphones harm students. | Smartphones are found harmful to students' concentration. |

**模板：** `[某人/机构] found [宾语] guilty of [错误行为/罪名]` / `[宾语] was found guilty of [错误行为]`

**造句练习：**
> The investigation found the school guilty of neglecting students' mental health.
> Several companies were found guilty of violating environmental regulations.

---

### 2. maintained his innocence / maintain that...

**学生写法：** He said he didn't do it.

**升级写法：** He maintained his innocence throughout the trial.

**原理：** `maintain` 作动词时不只是"维持"，还可以表示"坚持声称"——比 say/claim 更正式、更坚定。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The student said he didn't copy. | The student maintained his innocence. |
| She said the policy was unfair. | She maintains that the policy is fundamentally unfair. |
| Many people say traditional education is better. | Many people maintain that traditional education yields better results. |

**模板：** `[主语] maintained his/her innocence` / `[主语] maintains that + 从句`

**造句练习：**
> The athlete maintained his innocence despite the overwhelming evidence.
> Some experts maintain that social media does more harm than good to teenagers.

---

### 3. in exchange for

**学生写法：** He helped him to get something back.

**升级写法：** He promised to help him in exchange for confidential information.

**原理：** `in exchange for` 比 `to get` / `in return for` 更正式、更精确，在议论文中讨论"代价与回报"时极为实用。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| She worked hard to get a promotion. | She worked hard in exchange for a promotion. |
| Students do homework to get good grades. | Students do homework in exchange for good grades. |
| He gave up his free time to earn money. | He gave up his free time in exchange for money. |

**模板：** `[主语] [动词] [宾语] in exchange for [回报/交换物]`

**造句练习：**
> Many young people move to big cities in exchange for better career opportunities.
> The company promised higher salaries in exchange for longer working hours.

---

### 4. sufficient evidence of / sufficient...to...

**学生写法：** There is enough proof that he did this.

**升级写法：** The court found sufficient evidence of corruption.

**原理：** `sufficient` 比 `enough` 更正式、更学术。`sufficient evidence of + 名词` 是一个高分的固定搭配。

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| There is enough proof that he is hardworking. | There is sufficient evidence of his diligence. |
| We have enough reasons to believe the plan works. | We have sufficient reasons to believe the plan will succeed. |
| The student didn't have enough preparation. | The student did not have sufficient preparation for the exam. |

**模板：** `sufficient evidence of + 名词` / `sufficient + 名词 + to do`

**造句练习：**
> The report provides sufficient evidence of the benefits of reading regularly.
> There is sufficient data to conclude that the new method is more effective.

## 综合练习：一篇范文

把四个表达串起来写一个关于"校园诚信"的段落：

> **Topic: Academic Honesty**
>
> Last semester, a student was **found guilty of** plagiarism by the school's academic committee. The committee found **sufficient evidence of** copied content in his thesis. The student, however, **maintained his innocence**, claiming he had only used the sources as references. Finally, he accepted a suspension **in exchange for** dropping the formal charges. This case reminds us that honesty is the foundation of academic achievement.

## 总结

这四个表达的共同点是——它们都是"观点的动词/短语"。写议论文时，你总是在表达判断、证明、主张。用上这些表达，作文会立刻从"初中水平"进入"读外刊"的档次。

记住这个思路：**不是用对的词，而是用好的词。**
