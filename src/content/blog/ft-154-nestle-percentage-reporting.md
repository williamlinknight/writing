写数据类英语作文（图表题、调查报告、数据分析）时，你是不是经常只会写：

> 30% of people think… 50% of people think…

然后句子就开始**重复**——同一个结构翻来覆去，读起来像一份干巴巴的统计报表。

数据类写作的关键不是"放数字"，而是**用结构让数据自己讲故事**。数字本身没有说服力，但数字的组织方式有。

今天我们从FT一篇关于雀巢内部健康报告的重磅报道中，提炼 **5个写数据的高级句型**。这篇报道堪称数据写作的教科书：它面对一堆百分比，没有写成流水账，而是通过**分层对比、递进列举、排除限定**等方法，让冰冷的数字变得生动。

---

## 句型一：承认/揭露 — "has acknowledged that…, and that…" 双that并列

**原文段落：**
> The world's largest food company, Nestlé, **has acknowledged in an internal document that** more than 60 per cent of its mainstream food and drinks products do not meet a "recognised definition of health" **and that** "some of our categories and products will never be 'healthy' no matter how much we renovate".

> 全球最大食品公司雀巢在一份内部文件中承认，该公司60%以上的主流食品和饮料产品不符合一种"公认的健康"定义，而且"我们的一些品类和产品永远都做不到'健康'，无论我们怎么革新"。

**为什么好：** 这个句子的骨架是 **has acknowledged that X, and that Y**。双that的使用让读者清楚感受到"承认了一件事，再承认一件更严重的事"——两个that各带一个完整的从句，逻辑层级清晰。最妙的是第二个that引用了直接引语（"never be 'healthy'..."），把一个"承认"变成了"承认+自曝"。

**学生写法 vs 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The company said the product is not good. | The company **has acknowledged in a recent report that** many of its products do not meet safety standards **and that** some may never be improved. |
| 30% of students think homework is too much. | A survey **has revealed that** nearly one-third of students find homework excessive **and that** the pressure will continue to increase. |
| My school said uniforms are necessary. | The school **has acknowledged in an internal document that** uniforms do not improve academic performance **and that** the policy may need to be reconsidered. |

**模板：**
> [主体] **has acknowledged/revealed/admitted in** [载体] **that** [事实一] **and that** [事实二，程度递进]

## 句型二：限定百分比 — "only [百分比] of [A] achieve/score/reach [标准]"

**原文段落：**
> A presentation circulated among top executives early this year, seen by the Financial Times, says **only 37 per cent of Nestlé's food and beverages by revenues**, excluding products such as pet food and specialised medical nutrition, **achieve a rating above 3.5** under Australia's health star rating system.

> 据英国《金融时报》看到的一份今年年初在该公司高管中传阅的报告，按照澳大利亚的"健康星级评级体系"，在雀巢的食品和饮料中——不包括宠物食品和专业医疗营养品——只有37%的产品达到3.5星以上的评级。

**为什么好：** 这个句子有三层信息收束在一起。第一层：**插入语 by revenues** 限定统计口径（不是按产品数量算，而是按收入算）；第二层：**excluding…** 排除异常值（把明显不相关品类去掉）；第三层：**only… achieve a rating above 3.5** 给出判断——**only** 这个词本身就是态度，悄悄告诉读者"37%其实很低"。学生通常会直接说 "37% of products got above 3.5"，缺少限定和评价。

**学生写法 vs 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| 37% of products are healthy. | **Only 37 per cent of products by revenues**, excluding snacks and drinks, **achieve a rating above the health threshold**. |
| 60% of students passed the exam. | **Only 60 per cent of students by final grade**, excluding those who withdrew early, **scored above the passing line**. |
| Most people in the survey agreed. | **Only 42 per cent of respondents**, excluding those under 18, **expressed support for the policy**. |

**模板：**
> **Only [百分比] of** [主体] **by** [口径]，excluding [排除项]，[动词] [标准]

## 句型三：递进列举 — "along with…, and…" 三层递进

**原文段落：**
> Within its overall food and drink portfolio, **some 70 per cent of Nestlé's food products** failed to meet that threshold, the presentation said, **along with 96 per cent of beverages** — excluding pure coffee — **and 99 per cent of Nestlé's confectionery and ice cream portfolio**.

> 报告称在雀巢所有食品和饮料产品中，约70%的食品、96%的饮料——不包括纯咖啡——以及99%的糖果和冰淇淋产品未能达到上述阈值。

**为什么好：** 注意数字的排列——**70% → 96% → 99%**。这不是随便排序的。作者按照"最不意外到最触目惊心"的顺序递进：食品（70%）不行已经很糟了，饮料（96%）几乎全军覆没，糖果冰淇淋（99%）基本是"无一幸免"。这种**递增排列**让文章有了节奏和冲击力。另外 **some**（约）和 **per cent**（完整拼写而非%号）的使用也值得模仿——在正式写作中数字表达要严谨。

**学生写法 vs 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| 70% of food, 96% of drinks and 99% of candy failed. | The report found that **some 70 per cent of food products** failed to meet the standard, **along with 96 per cent of beverages and 99 per cent of confectionery**. |
| Many students from different grades failed the test. | The results showed that **some 40 per cent of Grade 9 students** failed, **along with 60 per cent of Grade 10 and 80 per cent of Grade 11**. |
| A lot of products are expensive. | **Roughly 30 per cent of basic items** saw price increases, **along with 55 per cent of mid-range products and 90 per cent of luxury goods**. |

**模板：**
> **Some [百分比] of** [类别A] [结果]，**along with [百分比] of** [类别B] **and [百分比] of** [类别C]

## 句型四：内部分层 — "with [类别] scoring [结果], [细节补充]"

**原文段落：**
> **Water and dairy products scored better, with 82 per cent of waters and 60 per cent of dairy meeting the threshold.**

> 水和乳制品得分较高，82%的水产品和60%的乳制品符合标准。

**为什么好：** 这一句和上一段形成鲜明对比——前面全是"惨烈数据"，这一句用 **scored better** 作为转折信号，然后用 **with + 具体数据** 补充说明。这种 **分+总再分** 的结构（总说A+B比前面好，再分别说A有多少、B有多少）比 "Water: 82%, dairy: 60%" 这种表格式的写法要流畅得多。

**学生写法 vs 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| Water: 82%. Dairy: 60%. | **Water and dairy products performed relatively well, with 82 per cent of water and 60 per cent of dairy meeting the requirement.** |
| Boys did better than girls. | **Male students outperformed their female counterparts, with 75 per cent of boys and only 52 per cent of girls passing the exam.** |
| Some cities have better air quality. | **Coastal cities fared significantly better, with 90 per cent of them recording moderate or above air quality.** |

**模板：**
> [类别A] **and** [类别B] **scored/performed/fared** [结果评价]，**with** [百分比] **of** [A] **and** [百分比] **of** [B] [具体结果]

## 句型五：数据排除声明 — "The data excludes…, meaning… accounts for…"

**原文段落：**
> **The data excludes** baby formula, pet food, coffee, and the health science division, which makes foods for people with specific medical conditions. **This means the data accounts for about half of Nestlé's SFr92.6bn (£72.7bn) total annual revenues.**

> 该数据不包括婴儿配方奶粉、宠物食品、咖啡和为患有特定疾病的人制作食品的健康科学部门。这意味着上述数据约占雀巢926亿瑞士法郎年收入总额的一半。

**为什么好：** 严谨的数据分析不仅要"汇报数据"，还要**交代数据的边界**。excludes + accounts for 的组合告诉读者：这个数据不是全部，它代表的范围是"总收入的一半"。学生写作中经常忽略这个步骤——他们展示数据后不自觉附言，但实际上读者心里会问：这些数据代表什么？有没有遗漏？加上这个排除声明，让你的论点更可信。

**学生写法 vs 升级写法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| This shows 60% of products are not healthy. | **The data excludes** products designed for specific medical needs. **This means the data accounts for roughly half of the company's total product range.** |
| 70% of students agreed with the policy. | **The survey excludes** students who did not complete the questionnaire. **This means the data accounts for approximately 80 per cent of the total student population.** |
| The experiment showed good results. | **The analysis excludes** samples that showed contamination. **This means the data accounts for only 60 per cent of the original sample size.** |

**模板：**
> **The data/survey/analysis excludes** [排除项]. **This means the data accounts for** [百分比/比例] **of** [总体].

## 篇章结构：数据报道的"三部曲"

除了具体句型，这篇报道的数据组织方式也值得学习。它遵循了一个**经典的数据报道结构**：

1. **全局震撼**（第一段）—— 直接给出最触目惊心的消息：60%以上产品不健康
2. **分层细化**（第二至第四段）—— 逐类分析：食品/饮料/糖果各自的数据
3. **排除说明+反例**（第五至第六段）—— 说明数据范围和例外情况：水和乳制品表现较好

这种方法有一个核心原则：**先给你"最震惊的"一锤，再用细节补刀，最后告诉你数据的局限。** 写雅思图表题、议论文数据支撑时，顺序完全一样。

## 今日练习

把下面这段**学生写法**改写成**FT风格**：

> 学生写法：
> A school survey found 60% of students do not get enough sleep. 80% of high school students do not get enough sleep. Only 30% of primary school students had this problem. The survey didn't count students on holiday.

> FT风格参考：
> A school-wide survey **has revealed that** 60 per cent of students do not get sufficient sleep **and that** the figure rises sharply among older age groups. The findings showed that **some 80 per cent of high school students** failed to meet the recommended sleep duration, **along with 55 per cent of middle school students and only 30 per cent of primary school students**. **Primary school students fared best, with 70 per cent of them meeting the threshold. The data excludes** students who were on holiday during the survey period, **meaning it accounts for approximately 85 per cent of the total student population.**
