你是不是写过这样的句子："The company wants to develop new technology, so they hire many people." 表达没错，但太"学生气"了。

真正地道的商务英语，不需要特别难的词汇——关键是学会用那些"看似简单，但中国人就是想不到用"的动词短语和搭配。

今天我们从FT一篇关于苹果招聘加密货币人才的文章里，提取4个可以直接用在作文里的表达。每一个都经过实战检验，适合高考、雅思和大学英语写作。

---

## 表达一：hint at — 暗示，流露出…的迹象

> **原文**：Apple **has hinted at** a growing interest in cryptocurrency, as it seeks new talent for its payments division.

**为什么好**："hint at"比"show"或"express"更含蓄、更地道。当你想表达"某件事暗示了某个趋势"或"某人透露出某种态度"时，这个词组比"I think"或"we can see"高级一个档次。

**学生写法**：
> Apple shows that it is interested in cryptocurrency.

**升级写法**：
> Apple **has hinted at** a growing interest in cryptocurrency through its latest job postings.

**模板**：
> [Subject] **hinted at** [something] by/through [action/evidence]

**造句练习**：
> The government's new policy **hinted at** a shift towards more flexible education methods.
> (政府的新政策暗示了向更灵活教育方式的转变。)

---

## 表达二：seize on … as a sign that — 抓住…视为…的信号

> **原文**：Crypto enthusiasts quickly **seized on** the job posting **as a sign that** Silicon Valley's most valuable company was preparing a deeper move into payments.

**为什么好**：这个结构有两个亮点。第一，"seize on"（抓住、迅速利用）比"notice"或"pay attention to"更有动感，暗示了"立刻意识到其重要性"。第二，"as a sign that"把"某事被视为某事的信号"这个逻辑完整表达出来。结合使用，可以写出非常有力的议论文推理段落。

**学生写法**：
> People noticed the job posting and thought Apple would do more in payments.

**升级写法**：
> Industry observers **seized on** the job posting **as a sign that** Apple was accelerating its push into the cryptocurrency market.

**模板**：
> [Observers/Experts/Markets] **seized on** [event/data/evidence] **as a sign that** [conclusion/trend]

**造句练习**：
> Many educators **seized on** the falling test scores **as a sign that** the current curriculum needed urgent reform.
> (许多教育工作者把考试成绩下降视为现行课程急需改革的信号。)

## 表达三：be charged with — 负责（做…）

> **原文**：The incomer, who will sit in the same team as Apple Pay, **is charged with** striking new alliances with "strategic alternative payment partners".

**为什么好**：很多同学写"负责"只会用"be responsible for"。但"be charged with"在商务英语中更正式、更有"被赋予使命"的感觉。用在介绍某人的职责、描写岗位要求时，比"responsible for"更地道。

**学生写法**：
> The new employee is responsible for finding new payment partners.

**升级写法**：
> The new business development manager **is charged with** building strategic partnerships in the alternative payment sector.

**模板**：
> [Position/Person] **is charged with** [task/responsibility noun phrase/gerund]

**造句练习**：
> As class monitor, Li Ming **is charged with** coordinating group discussions and collecting homework.
> (作为班长，李明负责协调小组讨论和收作业。)

---

## 表达四：extensive experience in … through — 通过…在…方面积累了丰富经验

> **原文**：The company **has extensive experience in** payments **through** Apple Pay, launched in 2014, and its own-branded credit card.

**为什么好**：这个表达的妙处在于"extensive experience in + 领域 + through + 具体途径"的结构，比"I have a lot of experience"具体得多。用在个人介绍、公司背景描写中都非常得体。而且"extensive"这个词比"a lot of"正式，是雅思写作高分词。

**学生写法**：
> The company knows a lot about payments because of Apple Pay and Apple Card.

**升级写法**：
> The company **has extensive experience in** digital payments **through** Apple Pay, which was launched in 2014, and its own credit card.

**模板**：
> [Subject] **has extensive experience in** [area] **through** [specific channel/experience]

**造句练习**：
> Professor Wang **has extensive experience in** second language acquisition **through** over 20 years of teaching English to Chinese students.
> (王教授通过20多年对中国学生的英语教学，在二语习得方面积累了丰富经验。)

---

## 总结

这4个表达有一个共同点：它们都是"动词短语"——由简单动词+介词构成，但表达力远超单个动词。

| 表达 | 适用场景 | 替换对象 |
|------|---------|---------|
| hint at | 描述迹象、暗示趋势 | show / express |
| seize on … as a sign that | 分析事件含义、推理论证 | notice / think that |
| be charged with | 描写职责、岗位介绍 | be responsible for |
| extensive experience in … through | 背景介绍、个人陈述 | have a lot of experience in |

下次写关于公司发展、个人经历、趋势分析的话题时，试着用上其中一个。阅卷老师会明显感觉到你的表达更"像母语者"。
