## 为什么定语从句是写作提分的关键？

读一篇学生作文，最常看到这样写：

> **Jeff Bezos is a billionaire. He founded Amazon. He wants to go to space.**

三个短句，并列堆砌。意思没错，但读起来像清单，不像文章。

再看FT怎么写同一个人：

> **The Amazon founder ... stepped down as chief executive of Amazon.com, the $1.6tn ecommerce group he founded.**

一个句子，三个信息点——身份、公司、价值——通过定语结构一气呵成。

**定语从句的核心价值**：不是"用复杂语法炫技"，而是**把多个相关信息压缩成一个连贯句**，让读者一眼看出信息之间的关系。

## 5种定语从句升级用法（附FT原文对照）

### 用法一：非限制性定语从句——添加"顺便提一下"的信息

**FT原文：**
> This month's launch has given **Blue Origin, which has been funded with billions of dollars from the Amazon founder**, some much-needed momentum.

**为什么好：**
"which has been funded with billions" 不是用来区分"哪个Blue Origin"，而是补充背景——读者也许不知道Blue Origin是谁投资的。非限制性定语从句（逗号+which）最适合添加这种**非必要但有用**的信息。

**学生写法：**
> Blue Origin is a space company. Bezos gave it billions of dollars. It can now launch people to space.

**升级写法：**
> Blue Origin, which has received billions of dollars from its founder Bezos, is now ready to launch its first civilian mission.

**模板：**
> [主体], which + [补充信息], + [主要动作]
> *Tesla, which has faced increasing competition from Chinese EV makers, reported a drop in quarterly sales.*

---

### 用法二：限制性定语从句——准确锁定"哪一个"

**FT原文：**
> Mark Bezos is the founder of HighPost Capital, **a New York-based private equity firm that is partially owned by the Bezos family**.

**为什么好：**
"that is partially owned by the Bezos family" 是**限定性**的——用来说明这是"哪一家"私募公司。如果去掉，读者不知道说的是哪家HighPost Capital。

**学生写法：**
> Mark Bezos started a private equity company. The Bezos family owns part of it. It is in New York.

**升级写法：**
> Mark Bezos runs a New York-based private equity firm that is partially owned by the Bezos family.

**模板：**
> [名词] + that/which + [限定信息]
> *The policy that was introduced last year has already shown significant results.*

---

### 用法三：省略式定语从句（分词短语）——让句子更简洁

**FT原文：**
> taking **Blue Origin's first human flight** just two weeks after stepping down as Amazon's chief executive.

原文其实可以写 "Blue Origin's first human flight, **which is the first human flight of the company**"，但FT选择了 **省略结构**——"first human flight"之后不需要整句定语从句，一个名词短语就够了。

再看另一处：
> the New Shepard spacecraft **scheduled for launch** by Blue Origin

这里 "scheduled for launch" 是 which was scheduled for launch 的省略版。

**为什么好：**
省略式（过去分词/现在分词短语）去掉了 which is/was，**减少字数，信息密度更高**。

**学生写法：**
> The spacecraft which was launched last month carried three people.

**升级写法：**
> The spacecraft launched last month carried three people.

**模板：**
> [名词] + [过去分词/现在分词] + [剩余信息]
> *The document signed by both parties came into effect immediately.*
> *Students applying for the scholarship must submit their essays by Friday.*

---

### 用法四：同位语结构——定语从句的"轻量版"

**FT原文：**
> stepped down as chief executive of Amazon.com, **the $1.6tn ecommerce group he founded**

**为什么好：**
"the $1.6tn ecommerce group he founded" 本质是一个**同位语**——它等价于 "which is the $1.6tn ecommerce group that he founded"，但省略了 which is，更紧凑。这种结构在FT商业报道中极其常见。

**学生写法：**
> Amazon.com is a $1.6 trillion ecommerce company. Bezos founded it.

**升级写法：**
> Bezos stepped down as CEO of Amazon.com, the $1.6tn ecommerce group he founded.

**模板：**
> [名词], + [名词短语（对前者的解释）] + [动词/剩余信息]
> *Beijing, the capital of China with over 21 million residents, has taken steps to reduce air pollution.*

---

### 用法五：where/when引导的定语从句——描述场景和时代

虽然贝索斯这篇文章中没有直接出现，但结合其他FT文章的常见模式，我们补充一个实用技巧：

> This was a moment **when** the company's future hung in the balance.
> We are entering an era **where** remote work is no longer a perk but a default.

**为什么好：**
很多学生只用 which/that，而 **when 和 where 能更精确地表达时间和场景关系**。

**学生写法：**
> I remember the day. On that day, I received the acceptance letter.

**升级写法：**
> I still remember the day when I received the acceptance letter.

**模板：**
> [时间名词] + when + [完整句子]
> [场景/领域名词] + where + [完整句子]
> *There comes a point when every student must decide their own path.*
> *We live in a society where competition starts earlier than ever.*

## 实战对照表

| 写作场景 | 初级写法 | 升级写法（定语从句） |
|---------|---------|-------------------|
| 介绍人物背景 | Elon Musk is a businessman. He founded Tesla and SpaceX. | Elon Musk, who founded Tesla and SpaceX, is one of the most influential entrepreneurs of our time. |
| 描述公司 | The company was established in 2010. It now has over 5,000 employees. | The company, which was established in 2010, now has over 5,000 employees. |
| 限定具体对象 | I need the book. The book is on the desk. | I need the book that is on the desk. |
| 补充信息 | The exam is difficult. It will take three hours. | The exam, which will take three hours, is known for its difficulty. |
| 描述时间点 | I will never forget that summer. I studied abroad that summer. | I will never forget the summer when I studied abroad. |

## 练习：试试用定语从句改写

**原句：**
> Jeff Bezos announced his space flight plan. He is the founder of Amazon. The flight will take place on July 20.

**参考答案：**
> Jeff Bezos, the founder of Amazon, announced his space flight plan, which will take place on July 20.

或者更紧凑：
> Jeff Bezos, the Amazon founder who plans to go to space on July 20, announced his flight alongside his brother.

## 写作建议

用定语从句时记住三条原则：

1. **非限制性（which/逗号）加额外信息，限制性（that/无逗号）锁定身份**
2. **能省略就省略**——能说"the spacecraft launched in July"就别说"the spacecraft which was launched in July"
3. **同位语比定语从句更简洁**——用名词短语替换 which is/was

背熟五种模式，下次写人物介绍、公司描述、场景回忆时，用上其中两种，句子的信息量和质感都会明显提升。
