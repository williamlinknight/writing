> 你是不是也有这种感觉：写到"竞争激烈"就是 very competitive，写到"越来越少"就是 fewer and fewer，写到"迅速发展"就是 grow fast？
>
> 今天这篇 FT 文章讲的是中国美术学院的珠宝首饰设计专业——它本身就是一个绝佳语料库，藏着5个可以在任何作文里直接替换的"高级版"词汇。

## 原文段落

> This spring the usual 100,000 or so applicants **vying for** just 1,700 places at one of China's most prestigious art colleges were not squeezing into vast halls for the gruelling four-day entrance exam… All the CAA's courses, which include fine art, ceramics, architecture and fashion, are **wildly oversubscribed** and for those wishing to study jewellery, there are just 20 places on the undergraduate course and **a handful** for the masters degree.
>
> The jewellery course also attracts international students from around the world… Its evolution reflects a desire to rediscover and celebrate traditional Chinese culture while building a bridge to the modern world and serving a **soaring** consumer market.
>
> This includes teaching the enamelling technique of cloisonné as well as metal folding — both are practised by a **dwindling** number of craftspeople in the country… Some Chinese jewellers made strong work in the traditional way but **brought it up to date**.

> 今年春天，通常约10万名申请人角逐这所中国最负盛名的艺术院校之一的1700个名额……所有课程的申请人数都远远超过名额，对于希望学习珠宝设计的人来说，本科只有20个名额，硕士只有几个名额。它的演变反映了一种渴望：重新发现和庆祝中国传统文化，同时建立一座通向现代世界的桥梁，并服务于飙升的消费市场。这包括教授景泰蓝的珐琅工艺和金属折叠——中国会这两种工艺的工匠越来越少……一些中国珠宝艺术家用传统方式制作了精美的作品，但也与时俱进。

## 词汇升级

### 1️⃣ vying for → 替代 competing for / trying to get

**原文：** 100,000 or so applicants **vying for** just 1,700 places

**为什么好：** `compete for` 是"竞争"，`try to get` 是"试图得到"——都不够精准。`Vie for` 带有一种"多个对手同时争夺同一目标"的动态感，而且非常正式，适合任何议论文。

**考场用法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| Many students compete for university places. | Thousands of students are **vying for** limited university places. |
| Two companies are trying to get the contract. | Two major firms are **vying for** the lucrative contract. |
| Everyone wants the manager position. | Several candidates are **vying for** the managerial role. |

**例句：** In today's job market, graduates from top universities are **vying for** a shrinking number of entry-level positions.

---

### 2️⃣ wildly oversubscribed → 替代 very popular / extremely competitive

**原文：** All the CAA's courses … are **wildly oversubscribed**

**为什么好：** `Very popular` 太笼统。`Oversubscribed` 是"超额申请"的专业表达，前面加 `wildly`（极其）强调程度，组合起来就是"抢破头"的感觉。这个表达在写教育类、工作类话题时是杀手锏。

**考场用法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| This university is very popular. | This university is **wildly oversubscribed**, with ten applicants for every place. |
| The training program has many applicants. | The internship programme is **wildly oversubscribed** every year. |
| The course is very competitive. | The coding bootcamp is so **wildly oversubscribed** that applications close within 24 hours. |

**例句：** Despite being **wildly oversubscribed**, the scholarship programme rejects nearly 95% of applicants.

---

### 3️⃣ dwindling → 替代 fewer and fewer / decreasing

**原文：** practised by a **dwindling** number of craftspeople

**为什么好：** `Fewer and fewer` 口语化，在书面作文里显得不够正式。`Decreasing` 虽然正确但平淡。`Dwindling` 暗示"逐渐减少到快要消失"——有情感色彩，比前两个生动得多。

**考场用法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| There are fewer and fewer traditional shops. | There is a **dwindling** number of traditional shops in the city centre. |
| The forest area is decreasing. | The forest area is **dwindling** due to urban expansion. |
| Fewer young people read books. | The number of young readers is **dwindling** in the digital age. |

**例句：** As the population of rural villages continues **to dwindle**, local schools are being forced to close.

---

### 4️⃣ soaring → 替代 rapidly growing / increasing quickly

**原文：** serving a **soaring** consumer market

**为什么好：** `Growing fast` / `increasing quickly` 可以，但不够有力。`Soaring` 像"一飞冲天"——暗示速度快、幅度大，是雅思大作文描述趋势的最佳用词之一。

**考场用法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| The demand for electric cars is growing fast. | The demand for electric cars is **soaring**. |
| House prices are increasing quickly. | House prices have **soared** over the past decade. |
| There is a rapid growth in online shopping. | Online shopping has experienced a **soaring** increase in popularity. |

**例句：** With **soaring** tuition fees, many students are forced to take part-time jobs to support their studies.

---

### 5️⃣ bring it up to date → 替代 make it modern / update

**原文：** Some Chinese jewellers made strong work in the traditional way but **brought it up to date**

**为什么好：** `Make it modern` 很中式。`Bring it up to date` 是最地道的英语表达——"让它跟上时代"。这是一个非常实用的 phrasal verb，适用场景极广。

**考场用法：**

| 学生写法 | 升级写法 |
|---------|---------|
| We need to make our teaching methods modern. | We need to **bring** our teaching methods **up to date**. |
| The company updated its logo. | The company **brought** its brand image **up to date**. |
| They modernized the traditional design. | They **brought** the traditional design **up to date** without losing its essence. |

**例句：** To attract younger audiences, museums must **bring** their exhibition styles **up to date**.

## 写作应用

这5个表达的共同特点：**都不是生僻词，只是你没习惯用它们。**

`Vying for`、`soaring`、`dwindling` 在英文外刊里出现频率极高，但在中国学生的作文里几乎看不到。为什么呢？因为教材和模拟题里的范文用词太"安全"了。

你可以做一个小练习：找一篇自己写过的议论文（任何话题都行），试着把这5个表达塞进去。你会发现，就算文章主题是环保、教育或科技，它们都能自然地出现——这就是"通用高级词汇"的力量。
